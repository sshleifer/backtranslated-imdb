"on our own" is a touch of four children who fly away from the foster home after their mother dies. With just one suitcase, their dog Ralph and $ 9, they were looking for their uncle Jack in their mother's old car. On the way, their car goes off and they meet Peggy Williams, a vacation teacher and she offers them to help them. Together, she and the kids look for Uncle Jack, but are forced to hide from the authorities planning to bring them back to foster care, as they do. This movie tells a story of love, friendship and how important it is to stay with your family, as the children are on their wonderful journey.
