This movie has an excellent point of departure and is really a call to become a Hollywood blocker. As I recall (and it's been a few years since I saw the movie), the action begins with a London omnibus full of people. There was a terrible accident and one passenger died. The rest of the film is then flashbacked, with 13 characters on the bus exploring their recent lives in complex details. At the end of the movie we go back to the crash and find out which of these chirpy, live characters have come to an abominable end. Big things, a bit like a good tabloid news story, fell out in precise, handy details. If only available on video ...
