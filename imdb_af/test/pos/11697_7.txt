Crackerjack is another classic Aussie movie. Like so many Australian films such as The Castle, The Dish and Sunday too Far Away, it is not yet widely explored in film. This time it is the game of Lawn Bowls and bowling clubs. Crackerjack is a much slower sport movie than many you will find, such as Remember the Titans or Million Dollar Babybut the characters involved are athletes in their own right. This movie is a showcase of a vast area of ​​Australian culture and features a sport that is popular and growing in popularity in Australia. Mick Molloy offers a classic, memorable character. It's really a must see.
