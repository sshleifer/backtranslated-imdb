Frank Horrigan (Clint Eastwood) is a secret service agent found guilty of killing President John F. Kennedy while on duty. Thirty years later and the current president is entering a re-election campaign, but he is getting death threats; and Horrigan has been called in to assist in what a routine research operation should be. John Malkovich plays the professional assassin and tired master following the president, and with the help of the past he tortures Horrigan in a psychological cat and mouse. <br /> <br /> Malkovich, Eastwood and Rene Russo all give wonderful performances in this top thriller. The direction is excellent and the whole picture is constantly charged with excitement and intrigue. <br /> <br /> A must see for thriller fans 8/10
