If you want an excellent overview of Byzantine history in colorful fashion, this is for you. This documentary will also be excellent for educators who learn about Roman or Medieval history. These documentaries are divided into three parts. It deals with the fall of the Western Roman Empire, the rise of Christianity and the beginning of the Byzantine Empire. The second video deals with Byzantine diplomacy and the iconoclastic controversy. The third and final video explains the decline and fall of Byzantium. The series is shot in several countries, and Byzantine history integrates beautifully into the realities of the modern world, which is the place of this civilization as part of human civilization in general. Take the time to look.
