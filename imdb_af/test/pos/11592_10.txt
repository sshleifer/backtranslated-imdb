This story of a man's 28-year-old battle for a death liberating him from his already dead bodies becomes a masterpiece to remember, thanks to a team of artists in a graceful state. Aligned, written, edited and recorded by Alejandro Amenabar, it touches you from the very first images and doesn't leave your eyes and your heart to the last credits, thanks to Alejandro and a group of great actors and actresses at their best. Bardem is an acting animal: One of those little comedians who can make a masterpiece of almost any character, the supporting actresses are wonderful in their roles and the story is told with such sensitivity that one laughs in the same minute and cry like us It got used to the big old masterpieces. The year's best movie in all senses. 10/10
