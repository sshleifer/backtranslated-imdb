This mini-series is iconic of the Australian spirit. Although there may be some blatant inconsistencies, the film shows a spirit that is unique to Australia and one to be cherished. As something, this mini-series shows the Aussie sense of humor. The ability to laugh at the most ridiculous. Our willingness to have a laugh in even the most dire of situations. While much of this series focuses on company and how humor can be used as a way of survival, it also has elements of drama calling true emotions. The main actors that appear are absolutely sensational and very convincing in their different roles. Capture the essence of their characters perfectly. This mini-series should be mandatory for all Australians.
