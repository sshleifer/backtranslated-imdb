"Grab with old shaving razors. Can I help if I'm not cousin Basil? I think the piano is out of mouth. Ginger Gray. It's your little snookums." Laugh for about 20 minutes as the boys spoof the gold diggers and opera singers. They even managed to show us how to properly demonstrate to attractive ladies how to handle a rifle and a crib. Wonder how many times they rehearse the scene with the phone cabin. The addition of Christine McIntyre and Emil Sitka, 2 regular collaborators, to the mix makes it even better. Only Vernon Dent is missing. The Stooges did some great individual scenes, but it was their best overall.
