The best pet movie since Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around a 23-year-old inventor, Brian Foster, whose systems seem to hold his boss's company, Brian is also busy with boss's daughter, Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robot dog named CHOMPS, modeled on his own dog Rascal. But CHOMPS is not an ordinary dog, it's as fast as a cheetah, it has an x-ray vision, can jump about 6 feet and has a strength of 20 men. The new invention impresses its boss and makes his business a success. But when the company's competitors from CHOMPS hear, they are trying to find a way to capture it. May CHOMPS weaken them before it's too late. This movie is a classic for all ages.
