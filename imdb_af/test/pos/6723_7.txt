A young Korean artist lives in Amsterdam. She is a loner and has never had a serious relationship and insists she is waiting for the right person. She works in the public square, draws portraits for passers-by, but also gives herself the opportunity to paint her favorite flowers, daisies. But suddenly she had a secret admirer. Flowers are delivered to her residence every day at 4:15, usually daisies, but she can't catch the transmitter in the act. This is because, unknown to her, her beau is a Korean hit, and he now wants to stay hidden. However, one day in the square another attractive Korean man sits for the artist and wears a pot of daisies. She concludes that he is her shameful hero, and also the man she was waiting to find. This second lord also has a secret; He is an Interpol agent. The killer can see everything in the square, due to an apartment close by. Of course he was disturbed that another man had entered the young girl's life. How will this shadow spell out love triangle? It's a beautiful picture to look at. The area in Amsterdam and the surrounding countryside is very, very beautiful. Add in three most beautiful young Korean actors and visually, any viewer has a beautiful panorama before them. The story is also nice, also a mixture of drama and action, with a drop of the unexpected. Costumes are very feasible and the production values ​​are high. In short, anyone looking for a quality foreign film with a compelling story and wonderful scenery will find this movie a great choice for a distracting evening. If you have someone's hand to hold during the view, so much the better.
