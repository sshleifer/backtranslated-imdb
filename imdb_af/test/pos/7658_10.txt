Latcho Drom is a film recording of Gypsy music from several countries. It is touching, sad and joyful. Most of the segments appear to be completely unborn, uncontrolled. The music, ranging from the sensual flamenco music of the Spanish gypsies, to the melancholy of the Central European Gypsies, is beautiful. If you like Gypsy music, you'll find Latcho Drom absolutely beautiful. <br /> <br />
