Bedrooms and Hallways are the kind of movie that makes you realize that the drama of life is hidden in detail. And that drama can also be fun! Although your own love life and your own friends may not be as unique as the characters in this movie, each one is obliged to recognize themselves and others in the colorful protagonists of this movie. And this is the great power of Bedrooms and Hallways, that the irony of life is that the most confusing things that happen to you can be the most interesting / hilarious / funny / sexy / ... stuff! Hope you will enjoy the movie as much as I have!
