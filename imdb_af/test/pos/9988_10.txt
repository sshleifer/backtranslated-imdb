It also didn't matter that others praised the boy in exuberant celebration. What matters most is that he saw his father's hand and then his face of approval. The real life challenge for both father and son has been met. <br /> <br /> Since in real life the hero of the story kept his amateur status and became a businessman, it virtually confirms that everything that went before the match has the practice field for a valid father and son relationship .
