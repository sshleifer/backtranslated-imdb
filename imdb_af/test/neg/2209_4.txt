MELTDOWN is pretty interesting SCI-FI. No big budget, very few special effects; But decent acting and a storyline of global punishment are enough to watch. An Asteroid reflects the atmosphere and pushes Earth in orbit closer to the sun. Global warming is becoming unbearable. A certain LAPD police spokesman (Casper Van Dien) is all out to save the world from certain extinction, as the rising temps are devastating. The pressure is to save mankind from this solar disaster; as well as protecting his daughter, nurse, ex-wife and TV reporter friend. The film includes: Stefanie Von Pfetten, Venus Terzo, Amanda Crew and Vincent Gale.
