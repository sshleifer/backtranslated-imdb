An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a sloth and a mammoth, who saved a baby, who was murdered near a group of tigers during an ice age. The morality of the film shows that good behavior may benefit others. One of the tigers in the group ordered to finally catch the baby, which was barely saved by his mother when the tigers attacked her community. The baby was then rescued by the sloth and the mammoth, but the tiger brought them to the goal of finally removing the baby. They traveled through very difficult roads with many dangerous roads, and immediately the tiger had to fall and be saved by the mammoth. In the end, the group of tigers tried to catch the baby, but the mammoth helped incredibly by his tiger colleague to overcome this attack and return the baby to his father and the community to which he belongs.
