Credited by Variety to be one of the biggest documentaries ever to come from Canada. All Pacino, Roger Ebert, Neil Simon, Matt Dillon and a constant dream of celebs make this film a Canadian classic. The film is best described as "Roger & Me" by "The Player". Look like Kenny Hotz and Spenny writing their script to the big boys of Hollywood. This film launched the Toronto Film Festival in 1997, Winner of the Best Indie Film Award Toronto. Europe premier at the prestigious HOF film festival in Germany. U.S.A. premier US comedy festival Aspen 1999. More information available at www.kennyhotz.com
