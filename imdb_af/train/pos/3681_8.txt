Paris Je T 'aime is a film that explores the various types and aspects of love and all the emotions it evokes. This film reunites some of the best directors from around the world like Gus Van Sant, Joel and Ethan Coen, Walter Salles and ... (read more) and Alfonso Cuaron to tell short stories about love in Paris, each with their specific direction. In this movie we also have one of the best films ever seen in a movie, including great actors such as Willem Dafoe, Steve Buscemi, Natalie Portman, Elijah Wood, Gerard Depardieu and many more each with good performances. Finally, this film is a compilation of stories of happiness, separation, unexpected encounters and love.
