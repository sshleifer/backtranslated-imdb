A brother's promise is a wonderful family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, a champion of Olympic speed skater. The film shows this athlete's life from young age to full maturity. The love and support of family members is everywhere in the hand. How Dan and the rest of his family handle and lose races is a life lesson for all of us. The dedication and determination of Dan's coach and his teammates show what is needed to make a real team. How Dan and his family deal with a devastating disease of a loved one is depicted without withdrawing sentiment or sugar cover. The faith of the family is shown in basic terms and is, of course, an important part of their lives. It is a powerful family movie that can make sense to a person of any age.
