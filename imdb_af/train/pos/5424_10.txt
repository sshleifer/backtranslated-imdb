"Gunga Din": One of the greatest adventure stories ever told! A story about the British Foreign Legion in the 19th century India and a low "aquifer" named Gunga Din, a local denizen who strives for his military counterparts; three British sergeants whose loyalty and companionship extend far beyond the limits of mere patriotism. They are a true and lasting friendship for each other and each one will be willing to sacrifice his own life for the good of the other. Gunga Din also wants to be a soldier, especially a Bugler, but can never reach that rank because of his subordinate social status. However, heroes are not made according to their social beliefs, they are made by their willingness to sacrifice for the greater benefit of others. Gunga Din tries every time to prove his mettle, but will he ever reach the rank he is so passionate about? .... "You are a better man than I, Gunga Din!" One of Hollywood's classic and a perfect 10!
