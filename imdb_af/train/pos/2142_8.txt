It's a very real and funny movie about a Japanese man who has a half-life crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessive with the encounter with the beautiful young girl, he sees in the window of a dance studio, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance classes. No one is more surprised when he begins to like it. But he has to keep his secret pleasure from his colleagues and family. When the truth comes out, it's pretty funny.
