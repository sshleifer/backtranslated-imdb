New York I love you, just like its predecessor (Paris Je T'Aime) is a compilation of different stories that reflect the different types and aspects of love, but otherwise the rhythm is much faster and the stories much shorter. The movie offers a unique view of New York City with its various and diverse landscapes. New York, I Love You offers a first-class film featuring Shia LaBeouf, Natalie Portman, Ian McKellen, Hayden Christensen, Chistina Rcci and Orlando Bloom, Ethan Hawke, James Caan and Robin Wright Penn among others and some excellent authors. and directors such as Brett Ratner and Anthony Mingella.
