In these glorious tales, a weekend is being shared under literary sizes. Mary and Percy Shelly, Lord Byron and others created an innovative group. Show their quest for sexual relief. Personal freedoms from political to moral. Liberal drug use for both stimulations and addiction. Their creative views of life and writing. Describes without bothering the viewer how each writer is looking for their moss. Along with the distractions and feelings, each one shares. With breathtaking scenery that doesn't detract but much enhances the story. Well created characters of fury to love, then angry for peaceful. With some of the most beautiful and scenic costumes.
