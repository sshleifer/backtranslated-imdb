After their daughter's brutal murder, Julie and Allen escape the city to find comfort in a lonely cabin on a remote mountain. Alen's intentions are good, he wants his wife to come out of her depression by resuming her photography. Julie beats over an ancient prison and sees the perfect, creepy, dilapidated environment for her photography. But when the photos were developed, they were full of dead people. And Allen quickly discovers the tragic history of suicide in their new mountain. "Dark Remains" is a pretty decent indie horror flick.It offers some real scares and lots of tension. The acting is pretty good and cinematography is great.7 out of 10.
