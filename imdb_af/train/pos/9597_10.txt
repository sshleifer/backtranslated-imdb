It's a must-see documentary for everyone who fears that modern youth has lost its taste for real life adventure and its sense of morality. Darius Goes West is an amazing roller coaster of a story. We live the life of Darius and the crew as they begin the journey of a lifetime. Darius has Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy, a disease that affects all the muscles in his body. He is limited to a wheelchair and needs daily attention. So how could this crew of young friends get him on a 6 000 mile trip to the West Coast and back? Watch the movie and experience the up and down of this great adventure - laugh and cry with the crew, as they face unimaginable challenges and enjoy the final triumph when they return home in their home town three weeks later. a striking reception and some great surprises!
