"Stanley and Iris" is a heart-warming film about two people who find each other and help each other overcome their problems in life. Stanley's life is difficult because he never learned to read or write. Iris is a two-year-old widower who works in a bakery where she meets Stanley. She decides to teach Stanley how to read in her free time at her home. Over time, they become romantically involved. After learning to read, Stanley goes to Chicago for a good job, just to return to Iris and marry her. <br /> <br /> It's a very good movie without nudity, violence or curse. which is rare in today's movies.
