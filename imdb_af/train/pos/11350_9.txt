Steven Rea plays a forensic scientist at work in Soviet Russia in 1982. In the first hour of his work, a body of a murdered girl is brought in. His workers go back to search for evidence and they bring back five more bodies. It begins the story of the hunt for one of the worst serial killers in contemporary history. It is a dark dark film that explores how the bureaucracy of the old Soviet Union indirectly contributed or caused the death of many. of the murderers victims. It also examines in Donald Sutherland's character how the proper use of bureaucracy in a communist government can help achieve the ultimate goal of finding a monster. A gripping movie not for everyone, but for those who want a good detective story Your interest is definitely a must on a scale of one to ten. 9
