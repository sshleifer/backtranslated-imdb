Twisted Desire (1996) was a TV movie featuring Melissa Joan Hart. Melissa's character, Jennifer Stanton, a 17-year-old, seduces her current boyfriend, Nick Ryan, to kill her two parents. The film is based on the 1990 murders of parents of 14-year-old Jessica Wiseman. Jessica shot her 17-year-old boyfriend, Douglas Christopher Thomas, and shot her parents! Thomas was executed in 2000! Jessica was released from prison when she was 21 years old. Evidence now suggests it was Jessica who fired the fatal shot her mother had killed. Jessica is known to live somewhere in the state of Virginia.
