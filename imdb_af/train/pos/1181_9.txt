Rachel Griffiths writes and directs this award-winning short film. A heart-warming story of dealing with sadness and the memory of those who have loved and lost us. Although, just 15 minutes, Griffiths managed to capture so much emotion and truth in the short time on film. Bud Tingwell touches Will, a widower who struggles to handle his wife's death. Will is confronted by the harsh reality of loneliness and helplessness while continuing to care for Ruth's pet cow, Tulip. The film shows the sadness and responsibility one feels for those who have loved and lost them. Good cinematography, good direction and great action. It will bring tears to all who have lost and survived a loved one.
