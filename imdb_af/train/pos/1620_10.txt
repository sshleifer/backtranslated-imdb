So wonderful, so peculiar, so romantic, so Italian. The movie is so feathery, you drift into its broken reality and you never want to return to the humdrum. A kitchen sink world of bakeries, and hairdressers, and plumbing, but one that shines with soft luminescence. Should the credit go to the script or the direction? Take your pick - they are flawless. Let me return to the New York City just outside the search glass.
