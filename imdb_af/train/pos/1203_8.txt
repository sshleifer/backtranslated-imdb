A Hilariously accurate caricature that tries to sell a script. Documentary hits knock, plot points, character bows, temptations, moments of seduction and disappointments and the allure but ugly prospect of selling a screenplay or getting an agent in Hollywood and all the fleeting, fantasy-realizing but ultimately empty rituals of passage closer to socializing in "the system." Hotz and Rice catches the moment you think you're finally a player, just to find out what's coming up fast and blindly, for inexplicable reasons, Hollywood has overtaken and you've checked your heart. dreams and your pockets. Pitch is a must-see for students in the film school to taste the mind and ego-bashing gantlet which is mostly the way to travel to sell you and one's projects in Hollywood. If your teacher or guru has never been there, they cannot tell you what to prepare for this gantlet. To enter the "biz", talent is needed, but far from sufficient
