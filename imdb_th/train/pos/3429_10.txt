I saw the movie premiere Friday (1/19) night in Park City for Sundance and was incredibly impressed. Sitting in a theater and listening directly to a soldier who had to go through more than I could bear. Other people in the audience were moving equally and while we wanted to turn away, at least we Can do so as a witness when these men and women share their experiences with them. Robert Acosta, Paul Rieckhoff, Sean Huze and Herold Noel, all veterans of the war in Iraq and featured in the film this While they may be returning home now, you can tell that this war is still in them and may always be Whether you support the war or not, it is our duty to support the troops with anything other than bumper stickers. Watch this movie!
