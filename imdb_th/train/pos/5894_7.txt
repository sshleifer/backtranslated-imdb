Brother's promise is a great family movie. This is the biography of Dan Jansen, an Olympic speed skater. This film shows the life of this athlete from an early age to full age. Love and support from family members are clear throughout. The way Dan and his family deal with winning and losing races are life lessons for all of us. The determination and intention of Dan's coach and his teammates shows what will make the team a reality. The way in which Dan and his family deal with the terrible illness of loved ones, being transmitted without feeling bad or making sugar The family's faith shows in basic terms and is clearly an important part of their lives. This is a powerful family movie that is meant for people of all ages.
