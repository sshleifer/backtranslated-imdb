A film about nostalgia that is quiet, cute and fascinating about how to confront old friends and the environment from your youth with your memories and problems and sorrow today. Movies that make you feel good All the ingredients are here: old, competition, friendship and loyalty. Mischief, refrigerator, night attack and other fun things we remember from our summer camp. Now all of them are in their thirties and middle ages.
