The great comedian from Dudley Moore was supported by Liza Minnelli and John Gielgud who spoke well. Moore was Arthur, the man in the wealthy family, who was close to earning 750 million dollars as he married a woman (Susan) from another million millionaire family In principle, Arthur accepted the terms. But he eventually refused when he met Linda Marrol (Lisa Minnelli) Poor, Arthur is just a parasite because he doesn't work. He enjoys drinking heavily and enjoying prostitutes. After many serious thoughts in his life and for the first time Arthur decided not to marry Susan just a few minutes before the wedding. Finally, there is happiness for Linda and Arthur, although the latter will know that his life will change in the future. This comedy is a good lesson for life for everyone. Rich people are not always happy with their way of life.
