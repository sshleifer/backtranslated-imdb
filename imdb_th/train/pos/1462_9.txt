In this documentary, we met Roger, the famous factory manager in China who produced beads and small jewelry, sold and traded at Mardi Gras in New Orleans. Roger claimed that the girls in the factory loved their work and were grateful for the opportunity. Received But the interview with the four of them told him another story. The cold life of the girls shows a completely different approach to Mardi Gras. The filmmaker David Redmon should be praised for the excellent and rare images of everyday life in the Chinese factory. This film is clearly told and intended to be the window to what is behind the "Made in China" label that is prevalent.
