A very good horror movie presents the greatest story, Boris Karloff and Bela Lugosi. Dr. Janos Rukh (Karloff) is traveling in Africa to find an ancient meteorite. After finding it, Rukh received poison from its radiation. The things that he touched death and the dark side of Rukh made him a selfish killer. His friend, Dr. Felix Benet (Lugosi) has found a way to solve a limited problem and at the same time realized that radiation can be used for the benefit of humanity by treating disease. Both humans will fight the possibility of radiation. Other special effects on the team: Frances Drake, Frank Lawton, Beulah Bondi and Frank Reicher
