Kathryn Bigelow and Mark Boal are preparing a sequel about a young boy in the US in Afghanistan. He is also a highly qualified surgeon and can travel freely around Kabul by working with injured NATO soldiers. In a particularly difficult mission, he accidentally picked up a rifle and shot Osama Bin Laden from a distance of about 3000 yards. But could not decide between a sharpshooter career with a surgeon, so he completely quit the army A year later, frustrated with civilian life, he joined the navy and the final scene showed that he was proudly wearing a white uniform
