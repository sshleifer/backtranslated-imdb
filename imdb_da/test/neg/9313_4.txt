A young man falls in love with a princess, but then has to fight to save her father's kingdom. While waiting, he kills an enchanted animal that brings a curse upon him. He becomes an animal and starts killing himself his own comrades. When no one returns to the kingdom from the battle, the king curses the crime country and forbids anyone to get there. One day, a rebel who wants to marry the princess, decides it's time they woke up in the cursed land to claim it for the king and the king agree when they come to the country, the king is caught by the animal, and the rebel returns home to the kingdom, the king has been taken and killed. He takes over the throne and prepares to marry the princess, but the night before her wedding escapes the princess escapes to the land to go and fight the animal herself. It is only when she comes to the damned land that she begins to realize that her father is still alive and that the animal may not even be evil after all. Unfortunately, her discoveries lead her to pay the ultimate price in their revelation.
