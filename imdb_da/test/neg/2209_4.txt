MELTDOWN is pretty interesting SCI-FI. No big budget, very few special effects; But decent acting and a story of global judgment are enough to sustain viewing. An asteroid grazes the atmosphere and evokes the ground to a path closer to the sun. Global warming quickly becomes unbearable. A particular LAPD politician (Casper Van Dien) goes out to save the world from certain extinctions, as the rising temples are devastating. The pressure is on to save humanity from this solar disaster; as well as protecting his daughter, nurse ex-wife and television reporter boyfriend. The game includes: Stefanie Von Pfetten, Venus Terzo, Amanda Crew and Vincent Gale.
