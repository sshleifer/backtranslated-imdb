Joan Fontaine is swept off the feet of the suave Cary Grant. After their marriage, she realizes that her husband is very irresponsible and owes a great gambling debt. It seems that Grant is trying to fix his best friend, Nigel Bruce, out of some of his life savings. Bruce ends up being murdered, and Fontaine suspects that her husband will try to kill her for the insurance money. This drama draws on an abrupt and flat final.
