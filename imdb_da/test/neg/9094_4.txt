A chilling and gory tale of a couple inherit an Italian castle of 150 rooms while still providing for the loss of their young son. The couple's marriage seems to be on the rocks because of a car accident that killed their son and left their daughter blind. Taking stock of the castle for a future sale, a fierce, tortured and creepy creature loses break from the intestines of the 12th century castle. Quite gory with great scary atmosphere and some sexual overtones. Starring Jeffrey Combs, Barbara Crampton, Jessica Dollarhide and Elisabeth Kaza.
