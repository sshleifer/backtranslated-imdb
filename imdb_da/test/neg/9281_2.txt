This was one of the shows I wanted to follow up on. But I just couldn't bring myself to devote my time to this show. To have a show that focuses on the theme of politics, you really need a strong plot of twists and turns to enhance the mood of the show, something like "The West Wing" or "Commander-in Chief". Rob Lowe was ok, but actors like Kyle Chandler just couldn't act (he was horrible in "Early Edition"). It was a pain to sit through this show. With its lack of excitement, urgency and characters that can actually work, I just had to give up on this show and am glad it was canceled so I wouldn't have more to miss. Degree D-
