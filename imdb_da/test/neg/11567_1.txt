What a desperate movie. Dress actors in furry funnels, place in suburban areas, destroy cameras and hope for the best. One can only imagine the thanks that the cast gave when their characters were killed by the sockpuppets, saving them further humiliation in this dull party. This rivals Monster a go-go as the best cure for insomnia ever made. Oh God - how can I fill out 10 lines explaining how overwhelmingly bored everyone looks in this movie? Whiney crappy plastic bungling robot that annoys all on and off the screen, giant spiders reduced to a single giant hair leg pulled by string, actors desperately not looking at the camera while mumbling out of dialogs ...
