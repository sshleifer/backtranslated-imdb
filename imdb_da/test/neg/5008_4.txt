"Curacao" is a foreign intrigue drama on the title Caribbean Island, involving a retired sea captain and bar owner (Scott) and a demoted CIA field operation (Petersen). The movie has several villains, foreign agents and bullies who talk about the couple of protagonists, all of whom are calling for something that Scott has and wants to kill. A lukewarm low budget tv flick, "Curacao" is seasoned with a few babes and use some Carnival street parades as window dressing. A little more than feed for the bored sofa potato. C-
