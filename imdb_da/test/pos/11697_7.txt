Crackerjack is another classic Aussie movie. Like so many Australian films like The Castle, The Dish and Sunday for Father Away, there is a place that has not been much explored in movies before, this time it is Lawn Bowls and bowling clubs. Crackerjack is a much slower paced sports movie than many you will find as Remember the Titans or Million Dollar Babybut the characters involved are athletes in their own right. This film is a showcase of a large area of ​​Australian culture and has a sport that is popular and increasingly popular in Australia. Mick Molloy presents a classic, unforgettable character. It really is a must see.
