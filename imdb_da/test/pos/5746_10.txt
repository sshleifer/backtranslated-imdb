A young man discovers that life is valuable after being seriously injured in a racing accident. His father is a pastor who changes his outlook on life, and he spends the whole movie sharing his newfound love for others in this shocking and heartwarming movie. The theme is built around street car racing, and it is an excellent movie for people who are in it and movies like 'the fast and the furious'. This shows how people can overcome their problems and give the general message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
