This film is one of those that cannot be considered extrovert. In fact, at first glance, it seems that history focuses only on the desire to get more money. But .. We take a look at the other side ... What do you see? You see that the money is only a metaphor for the ambiguous feelings that man has: Should I do the right thing or should I? And ... what is the right thing? Le makes a deeper analysis ... -What does that mean for a small town in the border? - That means that we can sometimes go too close to the border to do something we thought we couldn't ... - What does the dry land that appears in this image mean? - It means the dryness that sometimes takes possession of our offended hearts ... - What does it mean by the character (CHRIST): "Now I belong to him"? - It means the loss of our free will because of our unforeseen actions. In my statement, the author of history is trying to "tell" us. By the way ... remember what another character (JACK BARNES) said: "Nothing is so simple ..."
