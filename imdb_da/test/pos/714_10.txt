I've never given a movie a ten out of ten before, but that's the closest I've ever come to (I gave it a 9). There are very few movies that I really love, but one of them. With the gritty realism, fantastic on-site placement and it's great soundtrack, it literally blew my young mind when I first saw it in 1979. At that time, I didn't know about prisons, violence, racial tensions or the struggle to survive & Live freely. I doubt anyone who is an adult or, for that matter, anyone who grows up in today's world could be affected by this movie just as I was many years ago, but I will say this: "if you haven't seen this movie I envy you "; This is because you have the chance to watch this amazing movie for the deadline. For each of us they are different, but here are the rarer films of them all: those we actually love ...
