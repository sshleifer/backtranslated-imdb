Critters 4: This film was continued after the 3rd critters movie. This was released in the same years as critters 3 were released in 1991. Critters 4 takes you into space as they hunt for people in a spaceship. I doubt if there will be a Critters 5, because the end to the fourth was a pretty good ending, which brought the end to the critters as there was no more left. I give this movie 10 out of 10.
