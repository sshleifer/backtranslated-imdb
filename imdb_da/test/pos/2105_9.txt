This is one of the best martial arts movies (Kung-fu) from time to time. If you love martial arts movies this is one not to be missed. From flying nuns to training monks this movie has top kungfu styles and a good story line. Its about a priest from the wu dang clan trying to remove all shaoulin fighters claiming the title of being the best in martial arts. After killing key people in the shaoulin temple, the belief in shoulin has remained in the hands of two boys secretly under a shaoulin monk. The white abbot or priest of the wu dang clan develops new techniques that make him iron. Well this one not to miss the classic. It involves ninjas, shaoulin and nuns fighters. It's a good classic
