One of the best romantic classics, teen deviyaan is a movie that is about choosing from like-minded your most soft feelings, love. This is a simple story of a middle-class man who entered the limelight of his poetic talent and charming personality. He is attracted to three women and vice versa, a Hindi movie star, the other a rich lady and the third living in his neighborhood. The situation puts him in confusion as he has to decide one of them as his life partner. He asked for a few days from all of them. Finally, he goes to an astrologer and asked him who he loves most. His confusion is no longer, and he came to know the lady who lives in his neighborhood who really cares for him and loves him is his true love.
