If you want an excellent study of Byzantine history done in colorful fashion, this is for you. This documentary would also be excellent for teachers who learn about Roman or medieval history. This documentary is divided into three portions, first dealing with the fall of the Western Roman Empire, the rise of Christianity, and the beginning of the Byzantine Empire. The second video deals with Byzantine diplomacy and the iconoclastic controversy. The third and final video explains the fall and the fall of the Byzantium. The series is shot in several countries and beautifully integrates Byzantine history into reality in the modern world and shows the place of this civilization as part of human civilization in general. Do, take the time to look.
