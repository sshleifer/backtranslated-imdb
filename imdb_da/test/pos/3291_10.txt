The most comfortable pet movie since Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story is about a 23-year-old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems at his boss's company seem to be wrong, Brian is also the dating boss's daughter Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robot dog named CHOMPS modeled on his own dog Rascal. But CHOMPS is not the usual dog, he is as fast as a cheetah, he has x-ray vision, can jump about 6 meters and has a strength of 20 men. The new invention impresses its boss and makes its business a success. But when the company's rivals hear about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to catch him. May CHOMPS overcome them before it's too late. This movie is a classic for all ages.
