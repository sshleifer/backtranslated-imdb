In this emotional flame we are introduced to several different high school students during an eventful day in school. All of them struggle to belong, find themselves, and keep their painful secrets revealed. It is the feeling of a forthcoming documentary film focusing on the students' inner landscape and their way of dealing with everyday challenges. Goes with a powerful opening and development As a hymn requiem.Thalluri moves the camera with great skill and invites us slowly into the youth's mind and hearts. A fictitious reconstruction that informs us all and reminds us of the youth's weakness. One of the most important movies today. All teenagers should be given the opportunity to see this moving piece of film.
