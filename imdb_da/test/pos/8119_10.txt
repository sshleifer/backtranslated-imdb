Bedrooms and Hallways are the kind of movies that make you realize that the drama of life is hidden in the details. And that drama can also be fun! Although your own love life and your own friends may not be as unique as the characters in this movie, everyone has to recognize themselves and others in the colorful protagonists of this movie. And it is the great strength in bedrooms and hallways that irony in life is that the most confusing things that happen to you can be the most interesting / hilarious / fun / sexy / ... also things! Hope you will enjoy the movie as much as I have!
