What do you do if you are Aishwarya Rai, come out of a movie like 'Devdas', with some skeptical critics who are still relentlessly dissatisfied with your amazing performance or convinced by your strong screen presence and star play, what do you do? Go home, sit down and pout? No. If you are Aishwarya Rai, sign yourself for the next strong episode that comes together and continues to prove yourself worth all the praise, kudos, amazing scripts and equally great roles. And that's just what she did with and in 'Chokher Bali - a passion game' where she stars and shines like Binodini, a young widow who causes controversy far ahead of her time. Directed by Rituparno Ghosh [who later turns to her in the straight stellar 'Raincoat'}, Prasenjit Chatterjee {Devdas in Bengali} costars.
