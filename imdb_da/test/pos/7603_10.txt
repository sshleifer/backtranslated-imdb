This film may appear superficially flaky and to teenie-boppers. It seems to be the stereotypical high school movie. But actually it takes an honest and touching look at the high school experience. It does not suggest an unrealistic end where the geeks and the popular children reconcile. It doesn't change what happened in adolescence; It just tries to count on it.
