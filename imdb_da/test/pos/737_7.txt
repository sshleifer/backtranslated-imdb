Widely known as "Don't Look In the Basement" - it's Crying 70's Cry, B-movie goodness that could actually pass as the genre version of "One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest". Although the movie seems to go anywhere during the first hour + of it's runtime, I enjoyed this particular group of quirky crazies and their various personalities and deficiencies - like the former army officer, a chicken obsessed with nurturing a plastic doll , a lovable man - source and a whimsical nymph. After their doctor has been murdered by a patient, a small sanitarium hires a new nurse on their sub-staff who are immersed in the resident's various "ticks" and outbreaks. Things are becoming alien when patients start acting much more abnormally than normal. You never really know, or care where the film goes, because it is still bred until it is completely knocked out! Several of the "twists" felt a little too forced, and I could have used a bit more blood, but I really dug this over-undervalued mix of humor and horror. Check it out ...
