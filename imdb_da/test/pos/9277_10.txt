"Watch the Skies" (2005 - 60 minutes) is an outstanding documentary on film from Science Fiction. It was produced and led by critic Richard Schickel, author of more than 20 books on this theme. Mark Hamill is the documentary narrator. Schickel joins directors such as Steven Spielberg, James Cameron, George Lucas and Ridley Scott to complete a time and space tour to show some of the 50's most memorable science fiction films and also some recent classics. The documentary shows six different approaches: Paranoia in the nuclear war; The amazing trips to the moon; The enigmatic planet Mars; Good and evil aliens; The after apocalyptic world; and the future of humanity. It presents comments and scenes of the following classics: Flying Saucer, Rocketship XM, Destination Moon, Space Children, Animal of 20,000 Fathoms, Godzilla: Monster King, Incredible Shrinking Man, Attack of Killer Tomatoes, A Journey to the Moon, the Evil Red Planet , the forbidden planet, things from another world, the ground against the flying saucers, I married a monster from outer space, the invasion of body matches, bloben, the world war, the day Earth still stood, ET: The Extra Terrestrial, Omega Man , Apes Planet, Terminator, 2001: A Space Odyssey, Things to Come and Metropolis. Highly recommended for science fiction fans!
