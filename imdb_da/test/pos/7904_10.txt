"on their own" is a touching story of four children running away from the foster home after their mother dies, with only one suitcase, their dog Ralph and $ 9 they sat down in their mother's old car in search of their uncle Jack . On their way their car breaks down and they meet Peggy Williams, a vacation teacher and she offers to help them out. Together, she and the kids are looking for Uncle Jack, but are forced to hide from the authorities who are planning to take them back to nursing homes as they do. This movie tells a story of love, friendship and how important it is to hold on to your family as the kids go on their amazing journey.
