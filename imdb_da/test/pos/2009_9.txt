This new movie by Jeskid is amazing! Check it out and you'll be amazed. The story of Emily Waters, once a girl from a broken home, whose only means to escape from a violent father was through her sketchbook. Until one night, his drawings manifested themselves and rescued her, and now they use this power she is fighting against those who would hurt. Both live action movies and hand-drawn animation blend together to create a unique and original experience that will shake your soul and blow you away. The music is also incredible, it really intesifies the emotional experience and draws you deep into the conflict. Directed by Jesse Cowell and animated by Erica Langworthy starring in beautiful Marissa Parness, with music by Nico Audy-Rowland, Daniel Collins, Jeff Strathearn, Matt Sisco and Selcuk Bor.
