Fine dialogue and first-rate casting propeller this low key, noirish journey into the girl's search for the meaning behind the word puzzle that still appears in her life. Helped by her, anything but enthusiastic, male friend, when they two to the end of their quest, but with a price to be paid. The film never intends to answer all its mysteries, but does an excellent job in the exhibition of multiple plot twists
