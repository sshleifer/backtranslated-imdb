Thirty years later and the current president is launching a re-election campaign, but he is receiving death threats; and Horrigan has been called upon to help with what should be a routine research operation. John Malkovich plays the professional assassin and disguise champion who tracks the president, and with the help of the past he begins to torture Horrigan in a psychological duel of cat and mouse. <br /> <br /> Malkovich, Eastwood, and Rene Russo all provide great performance in this premium thriller.
