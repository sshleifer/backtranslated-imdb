This movie is absolutely cinematic genius. It has a well brought together cast that gives an almost magical performance. The effects are nothing but amazing, and the story keeps you hanging out of your chair right through the movie. Jack Long plays the part of abbot white exceptionally well, he gives an incredibly exciting portrayal of absolute evil. If you're a kung fu fan or just an action movie enthusiast, this movie is an excellent choice for anyone lucky enough to find a copy. For any great kung fu fans, this movie gives a convincing insight into the Shaolin world. This movie is absolutely 10/10 quality and should be considered as one of the greatest Eastern movies of all time.
