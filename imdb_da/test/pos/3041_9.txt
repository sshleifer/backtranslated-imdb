John Voight plays the title character in this movie based on the autobiography of author Pat Conroy (Prince of Tides). A fine teacher's film, tells the story of a naive Pat Conroy, a young English teacher, whose first task is in a primary school on a rural island. The only white man on the island, he must fight internal and external pressures as he tries to instill education and values ​​in children who have been systematically denied such things for generations. A solid performance that really makes you think.
