This British psychological thriller is known in the United States as CLOSING. Utilization of X-Files' Gillian Anderson, playing an attractive middle-aged businesswoman of substance named Alice. She will attend a business party and invite Adam (Danny Dyer), who has just installed a security system for her, to be her escort. On the way home, jumping through the forest on a narrow path, Alice's car collides with a deer. After pulling the wounded animal away from the road, the couple are violently attacked by a drunken gang of bullies. Adam is beaten to a lot; Alice is gang raped and both are emotionally and physically destroyed by the ruthless attack. When the identity of their attackers is discovered, Alice and Adam designated exact revenge ... brutal revenge. The couple sometimes find themselves in conflict with how to deal with the ruthless attackers. Their final decision is to avenge without mercy. Let it not be a mistake, payback is hell. Also in the cast: Anthony Calf, Ralph Brown, Francesca Fowler and Antony Byrne. Brutal violence, disturbing images, nudity and graphic rape.
