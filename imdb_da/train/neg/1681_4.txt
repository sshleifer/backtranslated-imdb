Freeway Killer, is a Madman who shoots people on the highway while yelling a bunch of mysterious chant on a cell phone. Police believe he is a casual killer, but Sunny, the blonde heroine played by Darlanne Fluegel, discovers a pattern. Also ex-police, as James Russo plays, and they gather and bodies in search of the villain who has gone away with their spouses. Also starring Richard Belzer, this movie has its moments, especially if you like car chases, but it's really not a good movie for the most part, check it out if you're really bored and have already seen The Hitcher, Joy Ride or Breakdown, otherwise keep away from the highway.
