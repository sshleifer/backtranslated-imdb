Bela Lugosi plays Dr. Lorenz who loves his wife so much that he will do everything to keep her young. This movie starts with a wedding as the bride is taking her promises that she suddenly collapses. She is pronounced dead and taken by the citizens. Problems are that these are not real subcontractors, but body cleaners. A wave of bridal death at the altar and their body disappears confronts the police. Enter reporter Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She tracks Dr. Lorenz, but he also decides to use his youth to also keep his wife young.
