Well, just watch the movie. <br /> <br /> This one deserved and got the full MST3K sendup. As the boys and the various reviewers have pointed out, the movie "Fargo" had more Hawaii locations than this movie. Apparently shot on a tight budget, this movie highlights Hawaii's disintegrated dive shops, petrol stations and cheapo hotels. Zulu - later to the star like Kono in Hawaii-Five-O - appears as Thinne's lumpy, creepy sidekick, while France Nguyen models the Jenny Craig diet that has gone terribly wrong. Others who share the flickering screen include a drunken Richard Harris knockoff, a George Takai imitator, a not-so-smart hitman with splashed sensual slacks, and the villain "Tree" who has a beloved British accent.
