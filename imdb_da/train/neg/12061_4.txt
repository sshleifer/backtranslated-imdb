Charles Bronson stars as Lt Crowe a police detective who declares war against a pimp named Duke (Juan Fernandez) who kidnap the daughter of a Japanese businessman who is the man who sexually harassed Crowe's daughter (Amy Hathaway) in this cunning, but stylish hell avenged thriller. Kinjite may not be for everyone with it's somewhat disturbing plot threads, but it's well made and actually entertaining.
