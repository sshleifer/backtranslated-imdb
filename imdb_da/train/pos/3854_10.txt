When it comes to Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think of "Basic Instinct" and some of "Showgirls". But Verhoeven has made his best erotic thriller year before these two films: "The Fourth Man". This film is fascinating and mindblowing - and above all, the story is absolutely plausible, making the whole experience even more intense. The performances of Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thom Hoffman are extraordinary, and Verhoeven has the right direction. "The Fourth Man" makes even "Basic Instinct" look pretty pretty ... It can't get much better than this, a true classic of erotic nightmare cinema. 10 out of 10, at least ...
