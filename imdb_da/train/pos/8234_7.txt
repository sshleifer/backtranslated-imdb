"Fido" is praising for taking a tired genre, zombies and making it a most original movie experience. The atmosphere of the early 50's is fantastic, the spectacle amazing and the whole production shows very careful planning. Suddenly, the viewer is immersed in a world of beautiful classic cars, "Eisenhower era" dress, art deco furniture and zombie waiters. It would be very easy to reject "Fido" as a cartoon-like fluff, similar to "Tank Girl", but the two films are very different. "Fido has structure, a script that tells a story and appears to be superior. Don't make a mistake, it's a bold black comedy that succeeds where many others have failed.
