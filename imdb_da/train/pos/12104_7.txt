Well done old and very dark house cloud. Good setup, which includes Poe's character, even referring to the history of a London pub. Even from here it is pretty much the one guy who has taken the courage to visit the house on a particular evening that runs from room to room, either looking for or avoiding people, it is still most enjoyable. Plus we have the lovely and enigmatic Barbara Steele. There is some wood dialogue and some inexplicable bits and bobs, but it is the super creepy atmosphere maintained throughout it and the super musical score that keeps it moving nicely together. DVD comes from the US and has a few extra features
