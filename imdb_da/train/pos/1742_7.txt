Perhaps one of the most important and funniest Greek movies I've seen in the last ten years. Excellent performances (especially yiannis zouganelis are great), well written script and effective direction from a very special to the Greek very average standards, author. A film that is obviously influenced by Sam Peckinpah's straw dogs could be a masterpiece if it ultimately avoided some clear and exaggerated situations and symbolizes. Nevertheless, it is a film that deserves our attention and belongs to the rare category of Greek films that should be seen outside of Greece. It is a shame that in Greece there was no commercial operation, in addition to other fake and clever big productions like Politiki Kouzina ..
