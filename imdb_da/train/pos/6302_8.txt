Went to see this as me and my lady had a little else to do on a Sunday afternoon I like movies dealing with sleazy, losing characters and this is full of em. After a slow start we get some good turns from the cast, but it is the actual 'Bellini' that both makes and lets the movie down. 'The Bellini is one of the funniest scenes I've seen in a movie for a long time but is too short and could have made this a masterpiece totaling 71/2 out of 10
