Once again, the two bickering professors must join together to save the lost world. The five members of the first expedition return (see The Lost World, 1992, for a list of actors). A man seeking oil brings a drill crew to the plateau. Instead of striking oil, they tap into an underground volcano that threatens all life in the lost world. The oil crew collides with the natives and the scientific expedition. Although the situation looks hopeless .... (I'm not going to tell you the end).
