This may be the best short crime drama I've ever seen. The action is amazing, especially Amanda Burton, whose character goes from scary to sweet to disturb to sad and then some ... She does a fantastic job balancing Rachel's / Carla's feelings and working out the pain of someone who has lost one child, it is so credible that it feels more like a reality story then a drama. The other actors are, of course, also good, as they normally are in British television / film. The end, which I will not give away, is great, mainly because you do not really get a ... (you get what I mean after you have seen it). It is worth buying and looking over and over and if you are not touched by this, you are a cold hearted person.
