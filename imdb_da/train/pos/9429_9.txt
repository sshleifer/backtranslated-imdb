This movie is a classic in every sense of the word. It is very entertaining and also very disturbing. The action in this movie is well done. The story itself is credible, exciting and well thought out. Character development is also done well, the audience can clearly see how each of the characters is emotionally tested through this film. The villain of this movie is very threatening, from the first moment the audience sees them, they can tell that they are up to something. This film shows how a human being, when taken from civilization and in the midst of the forest, faces a life or death situation, slowly becomes almost as wild and wild as the animals that inhabit it. Not everyone will like this movie, which is very disturbing content that can make some viewers unpleasant. It is definitely worth a look. This film is a masterpiece.
