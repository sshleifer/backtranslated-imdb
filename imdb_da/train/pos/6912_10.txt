Superb comic farce from Paul Mazursky, Richard Dreyfuss, plays Jack Noah a rather successful actor - who is On location shooting a film in a fictional Latin American banana republic of Parador, led by the fascist, Alfonse Sims, who unfortunately has been subjected to a heart attack after indulging in for many local cocktails! Raul Julia plays the oily policeman who forces the restrained Noah to imitate the just-dead dictator Noah has a remarkable resemblance to, Sonia Braga plays the dictator's glamorous amusing mistress, who gives Noah a few lessons on how to 'act' as a dictator, Jonathan Winter literally bets the role of a CIA man in the Parador posing as a hammock seller. Can Noah win over Paradoras people? and stop the rebels? And give life's performance without losing it?
