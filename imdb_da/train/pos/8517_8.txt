Father Harry is in the rental business, and he gets the excellent idea to "rent" some of the orphanage for couples like Russ and Valerie. Harry, who becomes aware of the couple's dilemma, offers a family sibling for a 10-day rental period! Brandon, Kyle and Molly move into the apartment with their interim parents with entertaining consequences as the new carers are inexperienced with children. But where is the opportunity for a good ending? This is a treasure family movie.
