A nice piece of cinema history in a simple but effective way. After all, cinema is a visual medium, and Igor used his full potential. A young restless man controls a train without destination in mind. In one of the rooms he meets with a girl. Words are not exchanged, but their laundry is and from there we are taken on tour with other peculiar characters and situations. The two wires are the perfect cast, as their unique features tell you a story that doesn't require words.
