"Europe" (or, as it is known, 'Zentropa') is one of the most visually stunning films I've ever seen. The blend of grayscale and color photography is almost seamless ... a real feast for the eyes. The picture was a struggle for a 1991s Golden Palm in Canners. The award went to Barton Fink (of Coen's brothers); a film that is stylistically similar to Zentropa. Here is an exercise in the class: Rent both movies and be a judge for yourself.
