Uncompromising look at a suburb of 21st century Vienna blends the stories of six groups of characters by former documentary producer U.Seidl is a provocative, minimalist and intensive observation object. Following the worldwide spread of Big Brother reality shows, Dog Stage takes modern voyeurism to a disturbing, deep level. Hard to like, but insignificant piece of European art film may seem cruel and seedy, but manages to convey the nihilistic, alienated sense of modern society in a commendable way. <br /> <br /> A must for lovers of the world cinema.
