Danny DeVito only looks six minutes on the screen, but countless VHS broadcasts give him false credit for the star. The movie is a relatively sexist moral story - will Bobby find sexual satisfaction through the one night stand that his custom van facilitates, or must he wait for Tina, the dream girl, to give him time in the daytime? Yet, it is representative of the prevalent carnal dream of today's American American high school students, and on this basis alone, VANEN has almost become an artifact of the period to be referred in any honest retrospective of the period's popular American cinema ...
