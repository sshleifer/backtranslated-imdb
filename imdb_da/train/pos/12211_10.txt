By Hook or By Crook is an incredibly innovative movie from a couple of extremely clever and talented filmmakers, Harry Dodge and Silas Howard. They manage to tell an original story in a distinct film style, and it is beautifully shot by Ann T. Rosetti, and wonderfully written - truly poetic. <br /> <br /> The main characters are true heroes and serve as a rare kind of role model / inspiration to butch dykes and trannies everywhere. This movie has so much energy, so much exciting passion and scruffy San Francisco heart to it. I cannot recommend it highly enough! <br /> <br /> The best butch buddy movie all the time!
