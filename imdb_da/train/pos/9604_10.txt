"Darius Goes West" is the touching story of a brave teenager with Duchenne's muscular dystrophy and his personal mission to see the Pacific. He receives help and encouragement from a group of young men who love and care for him while on this mission. The story has a natural drama and an honest portrayal of the young people's commitment to helping one of their own suffered from this incurable disease. <br /> <br /> Anyone who thinks that young people are self-centered and narcissistic will find this movie to turn the stereotype upside down. It is the power of young people and their commitment to Darius' situation, which is very convincing in this documentary.
