Rob Roy is and underestimated epic of passion and action! Some MILD SPOILERS IN. Liam Neeson provides a high-performance performance like Rob Roy MacGregor, one of the best in his career. Jessica Lange is literally perfect as her wife Mary. They have the most passion and chemistry I've seen in a screen saver. John Hurt gives his best snotty aristocrat performance.Tim Roth portrays one of the big-screen villains. The best swordfights ever.Well Scripted ans scored and Michael Caton-Jones direction is flawless. 10 out of 10
