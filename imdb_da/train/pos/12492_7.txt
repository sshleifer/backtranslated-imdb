After their daughter's brutal murder, Julie and Allen avoid the city to find comfort and mourn in a lonely hut on a remote mountain. Allen's intentions are good, he wants his wife to get out of her depression by resuming her photography. Julie stumbled upon an old prison and sees the perfect, scary pastime for her photography. But when the images are developed, they are full of dead people - and Allen quickly discovers the tragic story of suicide in their new mountain. "Dark Remains" is a pretty decent indie horror flick.It offers some real scares and lots of excitement. The action is pretty good, and the cinematography is great.7 out of 10.
