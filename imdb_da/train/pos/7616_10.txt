Here is an innovative TV drama; who so easily mixes a compelling story, brilliantly drawn character development, humor, romance and drama into each episode. Here is a show that sings to its own tune whether it is the audience who chooses to follow or not. How many other shows on television these days are so boldly changing in tone from one season to the next? Where most of the top two shows on this site have found a formula that works in viewers and dollars and has stuck to the formula (Jail, 24 and Desperate Housewives come to mind) - LOST takes one Another route, where even after achieving that plateau and winning formula, the team of executive producers is brave enough to fully reinvent the show to serve their higher goals with compelling storytelling. This is where LOST distinguishes from normal TV. This is so tired of traditional television. And therefore, LOST is one of the most groundbreaking and innovative creations of modern television. Forget the naysayers - LOST is, has been and always will be there to appease it's cool after the first and the public next. But it is a testament to the ingenuity that it has got a fan base consisting of the best of both audiences.
