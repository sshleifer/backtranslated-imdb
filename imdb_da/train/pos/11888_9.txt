It was very warm. As a foreigner French, I especially enjoyed this "TV" movie which I think deserves a wider distribution. The action was good, and the ties between each member of this rural French family seemed so strong and so natural. Without a doubt, a fantastic film about the concept of family and integration in the light of homosexuality and AIDS. One of the strongest aspects of the film is this privileged relationship between the oldest son (Leo) who has contracted HIV and the youngest son (Marcel) who the rest of the family is trying to hide the situation.
