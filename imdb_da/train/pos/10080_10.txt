In the rapid economic development of the 1990s in China, there is a resurgence of traditional Chinese culture, partly because of the rise of nationalism accompanied by the rise of wealth and, more importantly, due to the sense of spiritual belonging after the collapse of it old socialist ideology in the post-cultural revolutionary era. But the revival of the Chinese traditional culture, namely Confucianism, was not without disasters because the Chinese have adopted the whole tradition without removing the bad part, and the discrimination of girls demonstrated in this film is an excellent example. Moreover, not only the part to be discarded, the good part to be inherited, such as the traditional opera, and its technique of changing face, must be ignored in the resurgence and outward extinction. <br /> <br /> The director used this movie to criticize the problem of transforming the tradition of today's China a d this is the deeper meaning behind the movie.
