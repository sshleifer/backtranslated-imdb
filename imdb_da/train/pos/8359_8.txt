Bertrand Blier is really a terrible French cinema, and in the 70's he could always shock the public. Filmed with his fave duo (Depardieu and Dewaere) and the usual dose of sex (Miou-Miou plays his typical role, at least one in the seventies so little we could know that a decade later she would be the best French actress ever ). In the first "Les Valseuses" is also one of the first roadmovies, since the viewer has just been taken to some trips by two small criminals. Those who are only happy with family life or just do not know anything more, the movie would be quite shocking, but this movie is more than just what it just lets you think of all the usual things in life (working for the car, being delimited at work etc.). It is a kind of criticism of the hypocritical society we live in. Good work, and it just makes you two things: Dewaere died just as young as he was a top actor and of course Depardieu, he should rather be stuck with French film as he proves here that no one can beat him. Timeless classic and 20 years later it will still shock some ...
