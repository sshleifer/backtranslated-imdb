Twisted Desire (1996) was a television film with Melissa Joan Hart. Melissa's character, Jennifer Stanton, a seventeen year old seduces her current boyfriend Nick Ryan to murder her two parents. The film is based on the 1990 killings of 14-year-old Jessica Wiseman's parents. Jessica had her 17-year-old girlfriend Douglas Christopher Thomas shoot and kill her parents! Thomas was executed in 2000! Jessica was released from prison when she was 21 years old. Evidence now indicates that it was Jessica who fired the fatal shot that killed her mother. Jessica is known for now residing somewhere in the state of Virginia.
