A brother's promise is a wonderful family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, a champion of Olympic speed skates. The film depicts this athlete's life from a young age through full adult life. Love and support from family members is evident throughout. How Dan and the rest of his family handle winning and losing races is a life lesson for all of us. The commitment and determination of their coaches and his teammates show what is required to do a real team. How Dan and his family deal with a devastating illness in a loved one is depicted without regretting feeling or sugar coating. The family's faith is shown in basic terms and is, of course, a big part of their life. This is a powerful family movie that can be meaningful to a person of any age.
