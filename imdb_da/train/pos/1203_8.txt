A hilarious exact caricature to try to sell a script. Documentary hits all beats, plot points, character arches, delusions, moments of elation and disappointments and the allure but insane prospect of selling a script or getting an agent in Hollywood, and all fleeting, fantasy-realizing but ultimately empty rites of passage companion to be socialized in the "system". Hotz and Rice grab the moment you think you're finally a player, just to find out what's going on is fast and blind, it's unexplained reasons that Hollywood has gone on and on Let yourself control your heart, your dreams and your pockets. Pitch is a must-see for students in film school to taste the mind and ego-bashing gantlet, which for most is the way to travel to sell themselves and one's projects in Hollywood. If your teacher or guru has never been there, they cannot tell you what to prepare for this gantlet. To get into the "biz" talent is needed, but far from sufficient
