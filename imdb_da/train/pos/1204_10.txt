Credited by Variety to be one of the greatest documentaries to get out of Canada. Al Pacino, Roger Ebert, Neil Simon, Matt Dillon and a constant killing of celebs make this movie a Canadian classic. The movie is really best described as "Roger & Me" meets "The Player". Watch as Kenny Hotz and Spenny PITCH their script for Hollywood's big boys. Called the only American movie ever to come out of Canada. This film opened the Toronto Film Festival in 1997, Winner of the Best Indie Film Award Toronto. Europe's prizes were at the prestigious HOF film festival in Germany. US Leading American Comedy Festival Aspen 1999. More information available at www.kennyhotz.com
