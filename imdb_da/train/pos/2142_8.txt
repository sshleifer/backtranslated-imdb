This is a very real and fun movie about a Japanese man with a mid-life crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved by. But when Shohei Sugiyama is obsessed with meeting the beautiful young girl, he sees in a dance studio window, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance classes. No one is more surprised when he starts to like it than he does. But he must keep his secret joy from his colleagues and family. When the truth comes out, it's pretty funny.
