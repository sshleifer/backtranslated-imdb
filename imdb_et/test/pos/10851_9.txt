A young woman leaves her provincial life a new city and she meets another woman with whom she falls in love. Their relationship quickly becomes physical and they both believe they are soul mates, until one day a provincial girl gets home to find a man in her bed. Her lover reveals to her that their relationship was just an experiment and she really likes men. Um, like Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. So, a provincial girl who is broken, torn and broken because of this discovery, is starting to discover what is in the real world, because she gets into the hands of all kinds of revenge and shiny people in the 19th century England.
