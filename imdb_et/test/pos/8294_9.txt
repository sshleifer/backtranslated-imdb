CHIKAMATUS MONOGATARI the only edition I've been able to find was abysmal - I almost couldn't see it. What a shame, because it is one of the biggest Mizoguchi films. The story that I believed other Japanese leaders had done before and since is a bit more straightforward than my favorite Mizoguch films (SANSHO BAILIFF and UGETSU MONOGATARI) and is essentially a tragic romance, in this case a transcendental romance that goes beyond the strict class boundaries. As always with Mizoguchi, I have been very pleased with the exclusivity and the bad print aside. As with all Mizoguch movies, I look forward to a DVD release - if it is possible ...
