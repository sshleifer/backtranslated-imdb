"Celebrity" is a very well-made portrait of students living in the New York Art School. The film focuses on a group of students who dream that it is great when they improve their craft at their famous school. Director Alan Parker allows every highlighted student to mature on the screen, allowing you to feel connected to everyone. Here's music infectious and fun. Dancing is exciting and fresh. The film finally became the basis for the Emmy-winning television series featuring starring Debbie Allen and some other film actors. One of the most enjoyable dance films of the 1980s.
