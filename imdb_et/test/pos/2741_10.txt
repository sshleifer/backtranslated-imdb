If there's one comic that helped put UPA on the map more than any other, it's Gerald McBoing-Boing. This story of a small store that only talks about sound effects has retained its charm over the last 57 years. In addition to the effects, Marvin Miller loved music, abstract animation and backgrounds, pretty much everything. And it won the 1950 Oscar for best animated short time.
