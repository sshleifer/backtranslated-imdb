The young man falls in love with the princess, but then he has to fight to save his father's kingdom. While away, he accidentally kills a fascinated animal that brings him a curse. He gets a beast and even kills his comrades. If no one returns from the battle to the kingdom, the king turns the land into a land of battle and forbids anyone to go there. One day, a rebel who wants to marry a princess, it is time that they went to the cursed lands to demand that king and the king agrees when they reach the land, the king tries to catch the beast, and the rebels return home to lie to the king to have the king caught and killed. He takes the throne and prepares to marry the princess, but in the evening before the wedding the princess arrives to the land to go and fight the beast. Only when he goes to the cursed land will he realize that his father is still alive and that the beast may not even be evil. Unfortunately, his discoveries made him pay the final price in his revelation.
