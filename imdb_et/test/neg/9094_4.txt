Frozen and gory story of a couple inheriting a 150-room Italian castle, while lamenting the loss of their young boy. Couples' marriage seems to be on the rocks caused by a car accident that took their son's life and left his daughter blind. Taking a castle inventory for future sale loses a terrifying, tortured and insecure creature from the intestine of the 12th century. Pretty horrible with horror and some sexual manifestations. Starring Jeffrey Combs, Barbara Crampton, Jessica Dollarhide and Elisabeth Kaza.
