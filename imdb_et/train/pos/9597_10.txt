It is a must-see documentary for anyone who is afraid that today's youth has lost their taste in real adventure and its moral sense. Darius Goes West is the story of an amazing roller. We live in the life of Darius and the team as they live all their lives. Darius has Duchenne muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects all of his body's muscles. She is confined to a wheelchair and needs round-the-clock attention. So how could this young friends team travel to the West Coast and back 6,000 miles back and forth? Watch the movie and experience the ups and downs of this great adventure - laughing and crying with the team as they get on the road with unimaginable challenges, and enjoy the ultimate triumph when they get back in their home country three weeks back to a vigorous reception and some great surprises!
