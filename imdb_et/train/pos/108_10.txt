"Europa" (or, as we know, Zentropa) is one of the most visually stunning movies I've ever seen. The mixture of grayscale and color photography is almost smooth ... a real feast for the eyes. The picture was a nominee for the Golden Palm in Canners 1991. The award was given by Barton Fink (Coen Brothers); a film that is stylistically very similar to Zentrop. Here's an exercise in the classroom: rent both movies and be a judge yourself.
