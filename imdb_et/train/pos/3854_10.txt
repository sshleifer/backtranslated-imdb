When it comes to Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think of "Basic Instinct" and some "Showgirls". But Verhoeven has made his best erotic thrillers before these two films: "De Vierde Man". This film is mesmerizing and mindblowing - and above all the story is absolutely plausible, which makes the whole experience even more intense. Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thom Hoffman's performances are extraordinary and Verhoeven is heading for the rest. "De Vierde Man" even makes "Basic Instinct" apparently annoying ... It doesn't get much better than this, the classic of the erotic nightmare. 10 out of 10, at least ...
