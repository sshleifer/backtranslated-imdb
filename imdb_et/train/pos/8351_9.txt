I remember going to Spain in the 25's, getting two lifts out of Italy, small Italian attacks. They did a lot outside the law, but on the other hand a little bit of a portrait of Jesus was protecting them ... Just and unfairly, good and bad, criminal, and correcting where in this new combination, outside the categories I knew earlier. "Les Valseuses" gives me a picture, albeit a movie and not a real life, that is close to my own experience: the intensity of every moment as you leave "everything behind" and you go great, whatever your way, it's another mood and also " a dangerous form of life, because, as we all know, there are people who are not ready for it and want to persecute you and so on ... This film is about values, it is the story of "what is right and wrong": morality. It is the resurrection of an individual who fights freely against "false morals" and conformism ... There is a danger all the way, because how far can you go with your "freedom" and overcome your moral limits and other people? What to do with people who are ready to hurt you, imprison you or even shoot what you do, like "steal" gasoline from an international oil company for you for a fifth-hand car? In Les Valseus these questions wake up again in me, because morality, which is contrary to the ordinary media message, is quite complicated ...
