Bela Lugosi plays Dr. Lorenz, who loves his wife so much that he will do anything to keep her young. This movie begins with a wedding as the bride is about to take their vows, suddenly collapses. She is declared dead and taken by the undertakers. The problem is that these are not true undertakers but body thieves. A wave of deaths of brides on the altar and the disappearance of his body confuses the police. Enter reporter Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She tracks Dr. Lorenz but he also decides to use his youth to keep his wife young too.
