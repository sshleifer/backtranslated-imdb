This psychological thriller from the United Kingdom is known in the United States as CLOSURE. Exploitation of Gillian Anderson of X-Files, who plays an attractive middle-aged businesswoman named Alice. She must attend a business party and invite Adam (Danny Dyer), who has just installed a security system for her, to be his companion. On the way home, speeding through the woods on a narrow road, Alice's car collides with a deer. After removing the wounded animal from the road, the couple is savagely attacked by a gang of drunken thugs. Adam is beaten to a pulp; Alice is raped by a gang and both are emotionally and physically devastated by the ruthless attack. When the identities of their attackers are discovered, Alice and Adam rush to revenge ... brutal revenge. The couple sometimes disagrees about how to deal with ruthless attackers. His final decision is to take revenge without mercy. Let there be no mistake, the reimbursement is hell. Also in the cast: Anthony Calf, Ralph Brown, Francesca Fowler and Antony Byrne. Brutal violence, disturbing images, nudes and graphic violations.
