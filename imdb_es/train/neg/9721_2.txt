Who could sympathize with these two hateful protagonists? What pretends to be a light and frothy comedy about neighboring children who can not give up their child's game of challenge even as they grow old to become an act of adultery, manifests itself more as an exercise in cruelty and petulant self-indulgence. As children, the couple is unbearably precocious; As adults they are intolerably immature. It is a bad combination.
