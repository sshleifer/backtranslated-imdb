Acclaimed director Mervyn LeRoy puts the drama in a film that competes with the best telenovelas. The great drama is found in the loves and infidelities in the social group of New York. Oh, yes, do not forget that jealousy can lead to contaminated hearts and murders. The cast characteristics of all the stars are: Barbara Stanwyck, Van Heflin, James Mason, Ava Gardner, Cyd Charisse and Nancy Davis.
