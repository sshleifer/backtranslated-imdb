'Stanley and Iris' is a moving film about two people who meet and help each other overcome their problems in life. Stanley's life is difficult, because he never learned to read or write. Iris is a widow with two teenage children who work in a bakery where she meets Stanley. She decides to teach Stanley how to read at home in his spare time. Over time they get romantically involved. After Stanley learns to read, he goes to a good job in Chicago, only to return to Iris and ask him to marry him. <br /> <br /> It's a really good movie without nudity, violence or blasphemy, which is rare in today's movies. A good movie for everyone. <br /> <br />
