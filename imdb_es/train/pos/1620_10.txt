So wonderful, so peculiar, so romantic, so Italian. The film is so soft that light floats in your refracted reality and you never want to go back to monotony. A kitchen sink for the world of bakeries, hairdressers and plumbing, but that shines with a soft luminescence. Should the credit go to the script or the address?
