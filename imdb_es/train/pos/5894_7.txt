A Brother's Promise is a wonderful family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, an Olympic speed skating champion. The film describes the life of this athlete from an early age to full adulthood. The love and support of family members is evident at all times. How Dan and the rest of his family manage winning and losing races is a life lesson for all of us. The commitment and determination of Dan's coach and teammates shows what it takes to make a real team. The way in which Dan and his family deal with the devastating illness of a loved one is described without undoing the feeling or layer of sugar. The faith of the family is shown in basic terms and, obviously, is an important part of their lives. This is a powerful family movie that can be meaningful to a person of any age.
