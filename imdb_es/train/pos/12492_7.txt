After the brutal murder of their daughter, Julie and Allen escape the city to find comfort and pain in a lonely cabin on a remote mountain. Allen's intentions are good, he wants his wife to get out of her depression by resuming her photography. Former prison and see the perfect and spooky scenery for your photography. But when the photos are developed they are full of dead people, and Allen quickly discovers the tragic story of suicide on his new mountain. "Dark Remains" is a pretty decent indie horror movie. It offers some genuine fears and a lot of tension. The acting is pretty good and the cinematography is excellent.
