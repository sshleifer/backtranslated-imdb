An interesting look at Japan before opening to the west. John Wayne, the first US consul in Japan, arrives in accordance with the agreements resulting from Perry gunboat diplomacy. He is not welcome. Wayne finally wins his meeting with the Shogun after bringing an epidemic of cholera, introduced by an American ship, under control. Follow a colorful procession to the capital with gifts for the Shogun, which includes a bottle of Old Tanglefoot. The meeting with the Shogun, the debates between Japanese nobles and a murder during an archery show present an interesting look at the politics of the period. In general, a very nice movie and also, how often can you see the duke lose a fight with a guy half his size?
