"Dressed to Kill" is undoubtedly one of the best suspense and horror films ever made. It is a tense, elegant and extremely exciting mix of sex and violence. The performance is quite good, the orchestral score by Pino Donaggio is unforgettable and there are many surprises. to keep thrillers fans intrigued. "Dressed to Kill" is a murder mystery that involves a sexually frustrated housewife (Angie Dickinson), her teenage son (Keith Gordon), her psychoanalyst (Michael Caine) and a high-priced prostitute (Nancy Allen) . The killer in the movie is a transsexual named Bobbi, who is also one of Caine's patients. The movie is full of breathtaking moments: the infamous murder scene of an elevator is extremely elegant and quite bloody as well. Highly recommended
