THE INVADERS IS A SCI-FI RETROCATOR OF FAST MOVEMENT THAT IS COMING TO BEN CROSS AND SEAN YOUNG. BEN PLAYS RENN, A TRAVELER FROM ANOTHER GALAXY WHO TRIES TO FIND ANNIE (PLAYED BY SEAN) THAT IS PHYSICALLY IDEAL TO HAVE HIS SON. THIS CHILD, IF ALLOWED TO BORN AND RETURN TO HIS PLANET, CAN BE THE ONLY OPPORTUNITY FOR HIS CAREER TO SURVIVE. THE ENEMY, A FOREIGNER WHO HAS DESTROYED THE PEOPLE OF RENN, ALSO HAS FOLLOWED THE RENN THROUGH THE STARS TO STOP THIS BIRTH. CROSS AGAIN SHOWS A GREAT RANGE OF COMIC BEGINNINGS WHEN THE FOREIGNER ENTERS A LOCAL BAR AND ASKS FOR THEIR FIRST FOOD COCKTAIL, HIS CAREER WITH THE ENEMY AND THE DRAMA WHEN SHE CAN KEEP ANIE AND THE CHILD HE IS LIVING.
