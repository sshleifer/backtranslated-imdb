['In the rapid economic development of the 1990s in China, there is a resurgence of traditional Chinese culture, partly due to the increase of nationalism accompanied by the increase of wealth, and more importantly, due to the sense of spiritual belonging after the collapse of the old Socialist ideology in the post-cultural-revolutionary era. <br /><br /> However, the resurgence of traditional Chinese culture, namely, Confucianism, was not without its disasters, since the Chinese adopt the whole tradition without eliminating the bad part, and the discrimination against girls shown in this film is an excellent one. example. <br /><br /> In addition, not only was inherited the part that should be discarded, but also the good part that was supposed to be inherited, such as traditional opera, and his technique, such as changing his face, was ignored in the resurgence and faced extinction. <br /><br /> The director used this film to criticize the problem of re-embracing the tradition of contemporary China and this is the deeper meaning behind the film.']
