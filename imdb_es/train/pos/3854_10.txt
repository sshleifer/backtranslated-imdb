When it comes to Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think of "Basic Instinct" and, perhaps, "Showgirls". But Verhoeven has done his best erotic thriller years before these two films: "De Vierde Man". This film is fascinating and amazing, and above all the story is absolutely plausible, which makes the experience even more intense. The performances of Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thom Hoffman are exceptional, and the Verhoeven management takes care of the rest. "De Vierde Man" makes even "Basic Instinct" seem quite docile ... There's nothing better than this, a true classic of erotic nightmare movies. 10 out of 10, at least ...
