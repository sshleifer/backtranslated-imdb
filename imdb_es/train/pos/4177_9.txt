A delicious piece of cinematographic narration in a simple but effective way. The cinema is, after all, a visual medium and Igor used all its potential. A restless young man gets on a train with no destination in mind. In one of the compartments he meets a girl. The words are not exchanged, but their clothes are washed and, from there, we get to other characters and peculiar situations. The two drivers are perfectly fused as their unique characteristics tell you a story that does not need words.
