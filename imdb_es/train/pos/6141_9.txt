Angela Lansbury plays Eglantine, a middle-aged lady in war-torn England, during the First World War. She has been studying witchcraft by mail, and has been secretly learning it at home, she is doing well, until three children who have been separated from their parents from the last air attack, are sent to stay in Angela's huge house Lansbury. She is not happy to have them, because of her secret. They just want to go home. It is very difficult to get along with the eldest son, he is a brat. The other two, a boy and a girl, are simply curious about their new place to stay and the lady who is their hostess. The children discover their secret and that is when the fun begins. Fun and more of that. Family fun. Laughter, party, adventure, something for everyone. The special effects are pretty good. This is a musical that the melodies are so catchy to sing later like any of the other Disney movies. Add this to your family video library. You will not be disappointed.
