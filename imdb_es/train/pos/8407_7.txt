The successful Sammy John record in 1975, "Chevy Van", is heard repeatedly on the soundtrack (this film has even been reissued with the title CHEVY VAN), despite the fact that the title vehicle of the film is a Dodge. Danny DeVito just six minutes of screen appearance, but innumerable VHS re-releases accredit him as the star of the film. The film is a comparatively sexist moral tale: will Bobby find sexual satisfaction through the night that his personalized van facilitates, or should he wait until Tina, the girl of his dreams, gives him the time of day? Still, it is representative of the prevailing carnal dream of the American male high school students of the time and, on that basis, THE VAN has become, almost in spite of itself, an artifact of the period to which reference should be made in any honest retrospective of the most popular American cinema of the time ...
