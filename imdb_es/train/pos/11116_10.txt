"The Godfather", "Citizen Kane", "Star Wars", "Goodfellas" None of the above is compared to the complex brilliance of "The Sopranos". Each and every one of the characters have layers on layers of absolute truth, completely and completely three-dimensional. We worry about Tony Soprano wholeheartedly, even though in the simplest model of good versus evil, he is evil. Soprano is the most provocative, intricate and fascinating protagonist ever created until this moment in history. If you are in the mood to be openly challenged as a spectator and to be forever altered in your feelings towards entertainment, look at "The Sopranos." I challenge anyone to sit down and watch the first episode of season 1 and not want to continue with the series. Each season is completely brilliant in its own way.
