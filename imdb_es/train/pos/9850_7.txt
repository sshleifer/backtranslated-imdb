An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a sloth and a mammoth, who saved a baby, who was about to be killed by a group of tigers during the ice age. The moral of the film shows that good behavior with others can bring benefits in the end. One of the tigers in the group received an order to finally capture the baby, which was barely saved by his mother when the tigers attacked his community. The baby was rescued by the sloth and the mammoth, but the tiger joined them with the aim of finally removing the baby. They went through very problematic roads with much danger, and immediately the tiger fell and was saved by the mammoth. In the end, the group of tigers tried to capture the baby, but the mammoth helped incredibly by his tiger colleague was able to overcome this attack and return the baby to his father and the community to which he belongs.
