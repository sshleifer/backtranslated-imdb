Based on a real mining disaster, this former German speaker (with English subtitles) remains one of the most effective docu-dramas ever filmed. With many non-professional actors, "Kameradschaft" offers a chilling vision of the friendship that unites the miners, no matter which side of the French / German border they are. A fatal accident reveals the best of all, nullifying the orders of superiors. A fellow miner in need will receive the help of his companions, even in the face of the threat of great losses, including life. <br /> <br /> This film recalls the selfless heroism displayed by the NYFD after the 9/11 attacks. . Leaving formal rules and regulations aside, these men and women in uniform knew only one cause: save lives and find their firefighters. - More than 70 years later, "Kameradschaft" still has a strong and timeless message: a friend in need is a made friend.
