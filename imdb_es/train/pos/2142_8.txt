This is a very real and funny movie about a Japanese man who has a middle-aged crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting the beautiful young woman he sees in the window of a dance studio, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance classes. Nobody is surprised more when he likes him. But he must keep his secret pleasure from his co-workers and his family. When the truth comes out it's pretty funny.
