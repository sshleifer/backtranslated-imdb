Twisted Desire (1996) was a television movie starring Melissa Joan Hart. Melissa's character, Jennifer Stanton, a seventeen-year-old seduces her current boyfriend Nick Ryan to murder her two parents. The film is based on the murders of the parents of Jessica Wiseman, 14, in 1990. Jessica had her boyfriend Douglas Christopher Thomas, 17, shoot and kill his parents. Thomas was executed in the year 2000! Jessica was released from prison when she turned 21. The evidence now suggests that it was Jessica who fired the fatal shot that killed her mother. It is known that Jessica now resides somewhere in the state of Virginia.
