This image came out in 1975 and was the second in the three-part series of the life of Sheriff Buford Pusser. Bo Svenson takes on the role of Sheriff Buford Pusser, and Luke Askew plays the role of Mobster Pinky Dobson. The last time we saw Sheriff Pusser, he was lying in a hospital bed behind him and his wife, who was killed in an ambush on Sunday morning. After Pusser recovers, he goes after the men who killed his wife. Can Pusser complete the revenge he pursues or does the mafia try to get him out before he succeeds?
