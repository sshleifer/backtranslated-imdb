Bertrand Blier is, in fact, the terror of French cinema and in the seventies he was always able to surprise the audience. Filmed with his favorite duo (Depardieu and Dewaere) and the usual dose of sex (Miou-Miou plays its typical role, at least that of the seventies, we can know that a decade later it would be the best French actress in history). First of all, "Les Valseuses" is also one of the first roadmovies, since the viewer has just been taken to some trips by two petty criminals. Those who are only satisfied with family life, or just do not know anything else, the movie would be quite shocking, but this movie is more than that, it allows you to think about all the usual things in life (work for the car, be bounded at work etc.). It is a kind of criticism towards the hypocritical society in which we live. Good job and it only makes you want two things: Dewaere died too young because he was a topactor and, of course, Depardieu, should have stayed with the Frenchman. Movies like him prove here that nobody can beat him. Classic timeless and 20 years later will still surprise some ...
