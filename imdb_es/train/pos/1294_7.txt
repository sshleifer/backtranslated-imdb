Strictly gloomy and depressing, the film evokes and could wish for the legendary misery and emptiness that characterized the controversial novel by Houellebecq of the same name. Like many French films, his style is of deep depth, but it is painfully slow, or should that be, slowly painful? While this is an excellent and challenging film, it is not pleasant and it is difficult to think of it any time you can be in the "right" mood to see it.
