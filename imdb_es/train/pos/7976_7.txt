Revenge is the theme of this Denzel Washington thriller that offers its share of action, chaos, murders and bloody bloodshed. The essential thing is the mission of search and destruction of a bodyguard, since his position, the young Dakota Fanning, is kidnapped from the school, which is exactly what Washington hired to prevent. The locations of Mexico City are so chaotic as the plot moves towards its foreseeable and violent conclusion, with plot twists on the way. Washington, a former CIA agent with a drinking problem, receives a good reference from a former agent who sets in motion the scheme of the plot. Washington and Fanning have a great chemistry between them and after a problematic start, the bodyguard and his position become best friends. Christopher Walken, Rachel Ticotin, Radha Mitchell, Giancarlo Giannini and Mickey Rourke make up the good cast in support of the two stars.
