A hilarious comedy of action in which Damian Szifron takes over the lives of Diaz, a policeman whose wife has cheated him. He is living in a hotel feeling guilty for his wife's infidelity, falling into depression, stop worrying, go red lights just for fun, feeling sorry for himself. And Silberman, a Jew cowering on parole, a left liberal who does not trust the police at all. He is told to accompany Diaz in his daily tasks, unable to refuse due to the terms of his probation. Soon, the situation is reversed when Silberman himself discovers that his wife is cheating him and ends up being consoled by the same person he was supposed to help, a person he did not trust at all at first. A nuclear conspiracy, car thieves, international spies, a hysterical wife. Strange characters in a charming comedy about friendship and heroism.
