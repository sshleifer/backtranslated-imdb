Richard Attenborough, who already gave us magnificent films like "A Chorus Line" and "Gandhi", once again surprises us by making a beautiful hymn to nature. In fact, the vast and unexplored territory (at that time) of Canada helps to compose the astonishing beauty of the landscapes captured by the cinematographic camera. If the film really is based on a true story, it is once again evident that the "men of vision" are, in truth, men who live beyond their time, with a historical perspective that only Time will give them reason . The cinematography is magnificent, like the cast led by Pierce Brosnan, whose performance is due to the masterful hands of Attenborough. A pleasant surprise is the appearance of Annie Galipeau in the role of Archie's beloved. Film that should appear on a list of those who really love nature ...
