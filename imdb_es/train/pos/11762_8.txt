I think many of us have had that moment in our lives. To achieve this, he must convince the great football field marshal, Reno Hightower (Kurt Russell) to return. For Reno, who is satisfied with his current batch in life, there is no need to change the previous record, which improves as he ages. It is not easy when his father-in-law (Donald Moffat) habitually reminds him of the biggest drop. However, Dundee is persistent and will do anything to try to correct the biggest mistake of his life.
