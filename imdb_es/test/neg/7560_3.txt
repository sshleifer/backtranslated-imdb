The movie has no connection to real life in Bosnia in those days. It should be more realistic and show the viewer the real traumas that were happening to ordinary people during the war. Please see some films by Yugoslav authors (Emir Kusturica, Ljubisa Samardzic, ... eg Bure baruta (A barrel of gunpowder), Tito i ja (Tito and me), Lepa sela lepo gore (beautiful villages arden beautiful ), etc. This is the true way of knowing the so-called Bosnian problem, Hollywood is the definitive discourse and not the right one to make films about the Balkan Peninsula, maybe Vietnam, World War II, ... but not the Slavs living in the former Yugoslavia.
