File this in the `How are movies made like this? 'column. An indie 1970s version of "Macbeth" adapted quite faithfully (but meaninglessly) in a conventionally unconventional black comic style. The cast gives it a shot, with Christopher Walken calling the eccentricity McDuff, and with Maura Tierney rising above the boring script as Pat McBeth. The other actors are wasted, as is the public's time. However, fans of this brand of peculiarities may like it. 4 out of 10
