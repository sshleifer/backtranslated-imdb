"The Danish Bladerunner" is boldly indicated on the box. Are you kidding me?! This movie is a complete drag. When I'm thirsty and I'm going to have a drink in the kitchen, I usually pause the video, so I will not miss anything. Not this time. Actually I found myself looking long and hard in the fridge, only to not have to go back. Why the hell is not there a science fiction screenwriter out there who has the slightest idea how computers work? It is amazing. One of the premises of the film is that our hero (who is a hacker) has a bit of a computer assistant to help him (the Microsoft Office clip finally became popular in the future). When he loses the assistant in the movie, he is helpless and can not get into any computer. IT'S A HACKER! It's like saying, you can not drive your car, if you do not have your lucky "driving cap" on. I will not even get into the effect of lightning when receiving electroshock ...
