"Curaçao" is a drama of foreign intrigue set in the Caribbean Island title that involves a retired sea captain and bar owner (Scott) and a CIA field agent (Petersen). The film has many bad guys, foreign agents and thugs, who hide on the pair of protagonists who covet something that Scott has for which they want and are prepared to kill. A warm low-budget television movie, "Curaçao" is spiced up with a couple of babies and uses some Carnival Street parades as a showcase. Little more than the forage for the boring armchair. DO-
