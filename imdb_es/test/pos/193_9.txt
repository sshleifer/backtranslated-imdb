Life establishes its own rules, from which no human can escape and which we can not change, not even making a supreme effort. Veronica initially resists, but in a terrible night of bombing she finally falls victim to her charms. A marriage is carried out, which is never accepted by the family. <br /> <br /> Soon, feelings of guilt seize Veronica and she realizes that Boris's return is the only thing she really craves.
