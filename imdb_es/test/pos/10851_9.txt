A young woman leaves her provincial life for a new one in the city and there she meets another woman whom she falls in love with. Their relationship quickly becomes physical and both believe they are soulmates, until one day, the girl from the province comes home to find a man in his bed. His lover reveals to him that their relationship was just an experiment and that he really likes men. Um, a bit like the thing about Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. So, anyway, the little girl of the province, broken, torn and destroyed by this discovery moves and begins to discover what the real world is about when it falls into the hands of all kinds of vengeful and salacious people in England. XIX century.
