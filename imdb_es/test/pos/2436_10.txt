I almost lost it. While visiting my friends in Philadelphia sometime in the early 1980s, I was navigating the canal after everyone else went to bed. He was not only obsessed with Bogart; but rather the whole era of those old movies that those of my age know so well. Add to that a plot similar to The Maltese Falcon, where so many different characters interacted with Sacchi, and you have a work of art as far as I'm concerned. About ten years later he appeared on television and I recorded it. >
