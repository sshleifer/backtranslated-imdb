Basil Rathbone and Nigel Bruce return as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson in this higher story of Holmes fighting the 3rd Reich and the intellectual genius of Professor Moriarty. The film opens in Switzerland, since Holmes is disguised as an "old book seller". You must bring Dr. Tobel and the Tobel Bomb Site to England before the Germans can kidnap Tobel. Holmes succeeds and the Germans receive the evil professor. Moriarty manages to evade Scotland yard and LeStrad "Dennis Hoey" kidnapping Tobel. The only clue left by Tobel is a list of "men dancing". Who will first break the hidden code of the dancers, Holmes or Moriarty? Can Holmes prevent the bomb site from falling into German hands, thus saving England from the precision bombing techniques developed by the Tobel bomb site? Look and enjoy.
