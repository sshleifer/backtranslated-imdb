A young man discovers that life is precious after being seriously injured in a street racing accident. His father is a pastor who changes his vision of life and spends the whole movie sharing his new love for others in this shocking and moving film. The theme is based on street car racing and it is an excellent film for people who are interested in that and in films like "the fast and the furious". This shows how people can overcome their problems and gives the general message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
