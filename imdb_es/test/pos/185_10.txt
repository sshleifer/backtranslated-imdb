If you think any war movie can really make you feel war, you should see "Letyat zhuravli" (in English, "The Cranes are Flying"). It tells the story of Veronika (Tatiana Samoylova) and Boris (Aleksey Batalov), who are in love on the verge of World War II. They are walking along the coastline, watching the cranes fly, when the war begins. Boris is immediately sent to war. Veronika hides with a family and ends up marrying the son, whom she does not love. Boris, meanwhile, continues trotting through the countryside, fighting the Nazis and experiencing all the horrors of war, until he runs out of energy. When Veronika, who works in a military hospital, receives this news, she refuses to accept it, until the body of Boris arrives home on one of the trains.
