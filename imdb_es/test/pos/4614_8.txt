In this flame of emotion we are introduced to several different high school students during a day full of activities at school. Everyone struggles with belonging, they find themselves and they avoid revealing their painful secrets. It has the feeling of a documentary coming of age focused on the internal landscape of students and their way of dealing with everyday challenges. Get a powerful opening and development. like a replica of him. Thalluri moves the camera with great skill and invites us slowly to the minds and hearts of the young. A fictional reconstruction that enlightens us all and reminds us of the fragility of youth. One of the most important films of today. All teenagers should have the opportunity to see this moving film.
