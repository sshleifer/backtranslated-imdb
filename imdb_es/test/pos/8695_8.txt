This movie has an excellent premise and is really asking to be turned into a Hollywood blockbuster. As I remember (and a few years ago I do not see the movie), the action starts with a crowded London bus. There is a horrible shock and a passenger dies. The rest of the film is told in flashback, with 13 characters who were on the bus exploring their recent lives with intricate details. At the end of the film, we return to the accident and discover which of these happy and vivid characters has had an appalling ending. Great things, a bit like good tabloid news developed with precise and even details. If only it were available in video ...
