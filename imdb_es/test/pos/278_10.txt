This is a very moving film that gives a new twist to a place where we have all been: a relationship that is about to end. The performance of Kristen Thomson as Tessa, desperately trying to maintain her connection with Bobby for just one more day, is extremely compelling and moving, and takes you straight to the story. Who has not faced the end of a relationship and at least wanted to scream with tears, "Just one more night!"
