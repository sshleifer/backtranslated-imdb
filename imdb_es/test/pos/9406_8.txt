This great television movie told the 1975 murder of Martha Moxley in Greenwich, Connecticut, by a nephew of Ethel Kennedy. The use of Martha's "ghost" to provide some of the details was very effective and added a lot of heart to the story. Christopher Meloni seemed to capture the personality of Mark Fuhrman very well.
