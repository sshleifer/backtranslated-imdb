Uzumaki succeeds in immersing you in a strange surreality where the forms of Uzumaki persecute and curse a city. It fails to be a competent horror film. While it is certain that the film will attract attention mainly to its strange plot and some interesting visual details, it will be better as a dark comedy than as a horror movie. It is definitely a movie that you should see if you like the kind of things, but if you are looking for a fright or even a small chill, you will want to look elsewhere.
