The most entertaining pet movie since Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around a 23-year-old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems in his boss's company seem to continue to fail, Brian is also dating the boss's daughter named Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robotic dog called CHOMPS, inspired by his own dog Rascal. But CHOMPS is not a common dog, it is as fast as a cheetah, it has X-ray vision, it can jump about 6 feet and it has a force of 20 men. The new invention impresses your boss and makes your business a success. But when the company's rivals hear about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to capture it. Can CHOMPS cheat them before it's too late? This movie is a classic for all ages.
