This movie may seem superficially scaly and for teenagers. It seems to be the stereotypical high school movie. But, in fact, an honest and moving look at the high school experience is needed. It does not propose an unrealistic ending where geeks and popular children reconcile. It does not change what happened in adolescence; just try to keep in mind with it. This is a really great, funny and moving movie, ideal for teenagers and for those who can still remember being one ...
