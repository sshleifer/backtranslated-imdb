A game full of fun, full of silly jokes, but sometimes cruel. It's not the best of the movies, but it's definitely worth seeing. David Niven and Stewart Granger are his usual charm with Granger as an especially charming and witty gentleman. Ava Gardner is as wonderful as ever, with such a charming character that is absolutely irresistible. The story line is typical, but full of jocular surprises, especially in relation to the unconventional relationships between Granger, Gardner and Niven.
