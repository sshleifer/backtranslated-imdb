This is one of the best martial art movies (Kung-fu) of all time. If you love martial arts movies, you can not miss it. From flying nuns to training monks, this film has the best styles of kungfu and a good story. It is a wu dang clan priest who tries to eliminate all Shaoulin fighters to claim the title of being the best in martial arts. After killing the key members of the Shaoulin temple, the faith of the shoulin is held in the hands of two children who are secretly trained under a shaoulin monk. The white abbot or priest of the wu dang clan develops new techniques that turn it into iron. Well this is a classic not to miss. They are ninjas, shaoulin and fighting nuns. It's a great classic
