The film "Inhabited" is about a family of four who move to a new house without knowing that there are dangers lurking. The daughter befriends dangerous creatures as they gain their trust. When they do they use it against her. A man who used to live there as a child or adolescent knows about creatures and tries to warn them. The creatures steal and take bright objects to do new things, mainly weapons. They have a cat and it disappeared in a murderous way. The man who tried to warn the little sister was tricked by them and ended up in a psychiatric ward. She then helps them defeat the creatures and destroy the one man. After they do it, they leave forever with nothing but between them and something happens to the doctor.
