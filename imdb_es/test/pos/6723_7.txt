A young Korean artist lives in Amsterdam. She is a bit lonely and has never had a serious relationship, insisting that she is "waiting" for the right person. She works in the public square, drawing portraits for passers-by, but, by herself, she also paints her favorite flowers, daisies. But suddenly, she has a secret admirer. The flowers are delivered to your residence every day at 4:15, usually daisies, but you can not catch the sender on the spot. This is because, for her, her boyfriend is a Korean hit man, and he wants to stay hidden, for now. One day in the square, however, another attractive Korean man sits down for the artist and goes by to carry a pot of margaritas. She concludes that he is her shy hero and, also, the man she has been waiting to find. This second gentleman, too, has a secret; He is an agent of Interpol. The murderer can see everything that happens in the square, because he has an apartment nearby. Naturally, it bothers him that another man has entered the life of the young woman. How will this somber love triangle develop? This is a beautiful image to see. The environment in Amsterdam and the surrounding countryside is very, very beautiful. Add the three most attractive young Korean actors and, visually, any viewer has an impressive panorama in front of them. The story is also quite beautiful, as it is a mixture of drama and action, with a touch of the unexpected. The costumes are very striking and the production values ​​are high. In short, anyone looking for a quality foreign film with a compelling story and a wonderful setting will consider this film an excellent choice for a fun night. If you have someone's hand to hold during the hearing, much better.
