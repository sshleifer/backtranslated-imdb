This movie is an absolute cinematographic genius. It has a well-assembled cast that gives an almost magical performance. The effects are nothing but amazing and the story will keep you hanging from your chair throughout the movie. Jack Long plays the role of white abbot exceptionally well, providing an immensely exciting representation of absolute evil. If you are a fan of kung fu or simply an enthusiast of action movies, this movie is an excellent choice for anyone lucky enough to find a copy. For all the great fans of kung fu, this film provides a convincing vision of the shaolin world. This film is definitely of 10/10 quality and should be considered as one of the best Eastern movies of all time.
