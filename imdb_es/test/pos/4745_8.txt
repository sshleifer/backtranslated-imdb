A compact criminal drama with a good amount of action. The exclusive location shots of New York are added to this small and tough film. Technically well done with good direction and acting. It goes without saying that the wild car chase that seems to start in the center of the city, extends along the upper west side, Washington Heights, across the George Washington Bridge and into New Jersey, has to be one of the best movies of all time. for me it improves with time. 8 of 10 ... easy
