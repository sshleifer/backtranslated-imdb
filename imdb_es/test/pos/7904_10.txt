"on our own" is a moving story of four children fleeing the foster home after their mother dies, with only one suitcase, their dog Ralph and $ 9 leaving in their mother's old car in search of their Uncle Jack. On the way, her car breaks down and they meet Peggy Williams, a school teacher on vacation and she offers to help them. Together, she and the children look for Uncle Jack, but they are forced to hide from the authorities who plan to take them back to the foster home while they do so. This film tells a story of love, friendship and how important it is to continue with your family while the children venture on their incredible journey.
