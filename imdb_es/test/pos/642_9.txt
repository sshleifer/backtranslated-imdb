An incredible movie that was well conceived and kept me on the edge of my seat. Brilliantly orchestrated in its time, and the comedy began exactly when the tension needed to break free. The performance was generally well done (the "Director" should have asked Alec Guinness for the acting lessons), and the shot selections were impressive (as in the lengthening of the room when Billy tries to run to the door in the studio). This movie did not stop from the opening scene ...
