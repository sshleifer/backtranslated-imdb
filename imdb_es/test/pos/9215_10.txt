The worst movie in history? BARELY!!! This is one of the BEST animated movies I've seen! It has appearances of all the great characters of the animated television series Rainbow Brite. Very safe for small children and colors capture your attention like no other! There is an evil princess who tries to steal the diamond planet Spectra, which in turn would cause the cessation of all life on Earth. This tells the story of how Rainbow Brite and her new friend Krys are able to stop the evil plan of the Princess and restore life and color back to Earth. It is proof that children can make a difference! Rainbow Brite will live forever.
