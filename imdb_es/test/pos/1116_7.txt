This is a great family movie that deals with land people who enjoy their local interests that are mainly about horse racing. Lloyd Bourdelle, (Walter Matthau) is a farmer who also breeds poultry and has a small son named Casey Bourdelle, who loves horses. Lloyd is able to raise an adult horse called "Shadow of Casey" after his son and this horse break all speed records and is a possible winner of a million-dollar race. Sarah Blue, (Alexis Smith) is interested in this horse and offers to buy the horse for $ 500.00 dollars, however, this horse receives serious injuries in the legs and Lloyd receives a serious setback that bothers the whole family. Walter Matthau gave an outstanding performance and this is a great movie to watch and enjoy.
