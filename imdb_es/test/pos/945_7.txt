The Japanese probably have the most sadistic movies in the world, and this is one of the most solid examples. With an execution time of approximately one hour, it contains enough sexual violence and gore to displease all healthy people on earth (even those who are hunting such films). Three men and a woman are making a porn movie. After some normal shots (which are pixelated), the girl is tied and the crazy people cut their food, arm and clothespins. After that, they make a hole in his abdomen and a man has sex with his intestines. He also becomes unconscious and his penis is cut off. The special effects are good for an obviously low budget production (only the pincers scene seems false), and I can not talk about the acting, the direction or the script. After hearing a lot about this movie, I felt very happy when I finally found it. The first part is pretty boring, but the second part is totally fucked up. The scenes of torture and killing are some of the most extreme and disturbing that I have seen. The gore hounds will be satisfied with "Tumbling Doll of Flesh", but an unknown viewer should not read the synopsis.
