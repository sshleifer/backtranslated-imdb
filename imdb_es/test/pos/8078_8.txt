Being a fan of the Bills, I originally found it annoying that they made a movie about the Bills and the loss of four Superbowls. But once I started looking, I felt really connected. Actually, it was nice to see the "Bills" winning the Superbowl, and I must say that for a television movie it was very well done. Gil Bellows as quarterback and Jon Voight as the old school coach did an excellent job. 8 out of 10
