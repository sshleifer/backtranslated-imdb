This miniseries is iconic of the Australian spirit. While there may be what may be considered obvious inconsistencies, the film portrays a spirit that is unique to Australia and one that should be appreciated. In any case, this miniseries demonstrates the Australian sense of humor. The ability to laugh at the supremely ridiculous. Our willingness to laugh in the most terrible situations. While a large part of this series focuses on fellowship and how humor can be used as a means of survival, it also has dramatic elements that evoke real emotions. The main actors that appear are absolutely sensational and very convincing in their different roles. Perfectly capturing the essence of his characters. This miniseries must be obligatory for all Australians.
