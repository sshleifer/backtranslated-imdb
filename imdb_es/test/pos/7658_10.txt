Latcho Drom is a film survey of gypsy music from several countries. It is moving, sad and joyful. Most segments seem to be completely stageless, not rehearsed. The music, which ranges from the sensual flamenco music of the Spanish gypsies to the melancholic music of the Central European Gypsies, is exquisite. If you like gypsy music, you will find Latcho Drom absolutely beautiful. <br /> <br />
