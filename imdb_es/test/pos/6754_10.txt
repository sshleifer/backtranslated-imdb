I still can not believe that Starevich made this film in 1934. The animation is just perfect, and what is surprising, with all the advanced technology we have today, there are few animation studios that are capable of producing a little gem like is. one. It has everything: a great story, beautiful characters (although in some cases it's a kind of morbid beauty), special effects ... well, it's definitely not a children's movie, but it's a must for anyone who is really interested. animation.
