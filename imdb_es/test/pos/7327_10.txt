Yes, it's "Flipper." But writer / director Alan Shapiro has created a version jewel. The spectacular underwater and past photography of "Jaws" veteran Bill Butler and the unforgettable music of Joel McNeely and Crosby, Stills and Nash make this family entertainment truly memorable. Shapiro, whose last film was improbably "The Crush", is once again shown as an extremely talented and versatile filmmaker. Rent it
