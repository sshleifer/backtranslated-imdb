Good dramatic film about a case of child custody with excellent performances by all the actors. A good example of what an excellent script can do to push a simple story to a much higher quality. However, the script was quite normal and this is what kept the film from the list of the best dramas of all time. Still, the great performance makes this film good to see if you are a fan of court dramas or a big fan of the main actors. The movie really should have been a little longer.
