"Sorte Nula" is the Portuguese box office number 1 of Box Office 2004. This low budget production (USD $ 150,000) was opened during Christmas along with American box office successes such as National Treasure, Polar Express, The Incredibles and Alexander, but he quickly captured the adulation of the Portuguese. movie buffs Despite the tough competition, the small movie did surprisingly well, beating all other Portuguese films of the last two years in its first weeks. The film is a mystery / murder with a humorous tone written and directed intelligently by Fernando Fragata, who has become a solid reference in the field of independent European cinema.
