Gary Cooper, (Michael Brandon) played the role of an American millionaire who had seven bad marriages, but always divorced his wife with a lot of money to live. Michael is in Paris on business and goes to a French department store to buy a pair of pajama tops and sellers refuse to sell only the tops, he has to buy the funds or there is no sale. Nicole DeLoiselle, (Claudette Colbert) listens to this conversation and offers to buy the bottom of these pajamas. Michael is very interested in Nicole and they have the opportunity to meet and leave. It is not long before Michael proposes marriage to Nicole and she is very surprised with her marriage request since she really does not know him very well. However, once she discovers that she will become Michael's eighth wife, she begins to change her mind and this story becomes quite entertaining and fun. Do not miss this movie, it is a great entertainment of great veteran actors. Enjoy.
