The story of an obsessed lover (Shahrukh Khan) and the efforts he makes to obtain his true love (Juhi Chawla) who is already married to her husband (Sunny Deol). The film is considered one of the best performances of Shahrukh Khan and earned him recognition from critics and the public. The fear that your love is not reciprocal, the fear of losing the person you love, the fear that your beloved may change your heart. In short, fear is the villain in every love story. <br /> <br /> But in 'Darr' fear is the ultimate expression of passion, obsession and sacrifice. 'Darr' is the story of Rahul (Shahrukh Khan) whose love and obsession with Kiran (Juhi Chawla) frees him from all the fears of life and death. 'Darr' is the story of Sunil (Sunny Deol), whose love and passion for Kiran give her the courage to face the fear of death. <br /> <br /> And finally, 'Darr' is the story of Kiran who is caught between them. The love of man and the obsession of another man. She fears one and fears for the other. One represents love, the other for life. In this battle between love and life, the supreme winner is love, because love always wins, in life and in death. simply "Darr" is one of the best Indian films ever made.
