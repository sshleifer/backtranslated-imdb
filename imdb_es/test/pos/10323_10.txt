"Gargling with old blades, can I help you if I'm not a cousin Basil? I think the piano is not tuned in. Ginger Gray, this is your little snookum." They laugh throughout the 20 minutes that last, while the boys cheat the gold prospectors and opera singers. They even manage to show us how to properly demonstrate to some attractive women how to handle both a rifle and a bear trap. I wonder how many times they rehearsed the scene with the telephone booth. Adding Christine McIntyre and Emil Sitka, 2 frequent contributors, to the mix makes it even better. Only missing Vernon Dent. The Stooges did some great individual scenes, but this was their best overall.
