The art of the absurd is alive and prosperous in contemporary Danish cinema! Well, at least it's in this movie. Nobody in this movie has fun. Everyone is upset or in shock, and if they are not, soon they will be! It is a story of mistakes, murders, shame, dignity and, in the end, love and redemption. The chilling and uncomfortable style of humor is idiomatic and will not attract everyone, but I personally felt that I had the fascinating combination of the strange and the absurd. You grieve the characters from a distance, even when you do not like them up close and personal. But their story is so tragic that you find yourself forgiving them and rejoicing over them, even when they get away with it. <br /> <br /> This is, in my opinion, definitely the best Danish film of recent years. <br /> <br /> 9 out of 10.
