"Head" is a film that has held up well since its original release date in 1968. The film is a complete contradiction of the image of Monkees. Present the Monkees in a way that their fans never perceived them; Men with real thoughts Fully controlled by their producers, the Monkees had the opportunity to tell their side of the story. The film makes fun of its image, the industry of the entertainment and the American corporations. The soundtrack contains some of its best music. It is a movie that is worth seeing again and again.
