This is a very real and funny movie about a Japanese who has a mid-life crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting the beautiful girl he sees in the window of a dance studio, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance lessons. No one is more surprised when he starts to like him. But he must keep his secret pleasure from his colleagues and family. When the truth comes out it's pretty funny.
