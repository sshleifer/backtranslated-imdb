A beautiful horror film with the greatest, Boris Karloff and Bela Lugosi. Dr. Janos Rukh (Karloff) is on an expedition to Africa to look for an ancient meteorite. After finding it, Rukh is poisoned by his radiation. Everything he touches dies and Rukh's dark side makes him a selfish murderer. His friend, Dr. Felix Benet (Lugosi) finds a remedy limited to the problem and at the same time realizes that radiation could be used for the good of humanity by curing diseases. The two demons will fight over the possibilities of radiation. Pretty good special effects. Others in the cast: Frances Drake, Frank Lawton, Beulah Bondi and Frank Reicher.
