Unbearably depressing and depressing, the film also evokes the legendary misery and emptiness that characterized the controversial Houellebecq novel of the same name. Like many French films, his manner is of melancholy depth but is painfully slow - or should it be, slowly painful? Although this is an excellent and stimulating film, it is not pleasant and it is difficult to think of any moment in which one can be in the "right" mood to see it.
