Accredited by Variety for being one of the greatest documentaries ever released by Canada. Al Pacino, Roger Ebert, Neil Simon, Matt Dillon and a constant string of celebrities make this film a Canadian classic. The film is much better described as "Roger & Me" meets "The Player". See how Kenny Hotz and Spenny PITCH put their script to the big Hollywood guys. Called the only American film ever released from Canada, this film opened the Toronto Film Festival in 1997, winner of the "Best Indie Film Award Toronto". The European premiere was at the prestigious HOF film festival in Germany. USA premier U.S. Aspen Comedy Festival 1999. More information available on www.kennyhotz.com
