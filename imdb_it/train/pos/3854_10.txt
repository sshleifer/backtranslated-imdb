When it comes to Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think of "Basic Instinct" and perhaps "Showgirls". But Verhoeven made his best erotic thriller years before these two films: "De Vierde Man". This film is mesmerizing and intelligent - and above all the story is absolutely plausible, which makes the experience even more intense. The performances of Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thom Hoffman are exceptional, and Verhoeven's direction does the rest. "De Vierde Man" even makes "Basic Instinct" a little domesticated ... It can't get much better than this, a true classic of erotic nightmare cinema. 10 out of 10, at least ...
