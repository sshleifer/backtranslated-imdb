After the brutal murder of her daughter, Julie and Allen flee the city to find comfort and pain in an isolated cabin on a remote mountain. Allen's intentions are good, he wants his wife to get out of his depression by taking his picture again. Julie stumbles into an ancient prison and sees the perfect disturbing scenery for her photography. But when the photos are developed they are full of dead people - and Allen quickly discovers the tragic story of suicide in their new mountain. "Dark Remains" is a pretty decent horror flick indie. It offers some authentic bravado and lots of tension. The acting is quite good and the cinematography is excellent.7 out of 10.
