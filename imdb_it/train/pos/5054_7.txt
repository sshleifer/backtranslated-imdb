Richard Attenborough who has already given us magnificent films like "A Chorus Line" and "Gandhi", once again surprises us to make a beautiful hymn to nature. Indeed, the vast (at that time) unexplored territory of Canada helps to compose the extraordinary beauty of the landscapes captured by the film camera. If the film is really based on a true story, once again it becomes clear that "men of vision" are, in truth, men who live beyond their time, with a historical perspective that only Time will give them reason. Cinematography is magnificent, like the cast of Pierce Brosnan, whose performance is due to Attenborough's expert hands. A pleasant surprise is the appearance of Annie Galipeau in the role of Archie's beloved. Film that must appear on a list of those who really love nature ...
