At first, I thought that the Ring would be a more than normal film with its normal plot. How surprised I was! Of course, the plot is simple - a girl is in love with two men - but Hitchcock brings it to us on a silver platter, with laughter and fear, with compassion and anguish. The way in which he describes the popular crowd of the fair, the strength of the attraction of the girl for both men, the tragic elements that are combined with techniques that open the mind to most of his bigger films (North of North-West Rope, etc. The master did it very well even before his thirty years!
