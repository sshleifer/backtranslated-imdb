Steven Rea plays a forensic scientist engaged in the work in Sovie Russia in 1982 ... in the very first hours of his work he is taken inside a body of a murdered girl ... his workers go back to look for evidence and bring five other bodies back. .. begins the story of hunting for one of the worst serial killers in modern history. It is a dark and depressing film that explores how the bureaucracy of the old Soviet Union indirectly contributed or caused the death of many of the killers' victims. Also explore in the character of Donald Sutherland how the correct use of bureaucracy in a communist government can help you reach the ultimate goal of finding a monster A compelling film not for everyone but for those who like a good police story that will keep your interest is to be seen on a scale of one to ten. 9
