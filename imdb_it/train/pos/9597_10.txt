This is a documentary must for anyone who fears that modern youth has lost its taste for the adventure of real life and its sense of morality. Darius Goes West is an incredible roller coaster of a story. We live the lives of Dario and the crew as they embark on the journey of a lifetime. Dario has Duchenne muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects all the muscles in his body. He is confined to a wheelchair and needs attention 24 hours a day. So how could this crew of young friends manage to take him on a journey of 6,000 miles to the west coast and back? Watch the movie and experience the highs and lows of this great adventure - laugh and cry with the crew as they face unimaginable challenges along the way, and enjoy the final triumph when they return three weeks later to their hometown at an ecstatic reception and some great surprises!
