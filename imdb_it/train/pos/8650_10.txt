Kalifornia is the story of a writer and his girlfriend's photographer who is looking for someone to help pay the gas money and concentrate on driving for an off-road trip to famous attractions for murder. Ironically, a serial killer and his girlfriend respond to the post. Kalifornia is a rough diamond and a very intriguing journey with a serial killer. Great shows all around from the leads, particularly with Pitt who is exceptional. Check it out!!
