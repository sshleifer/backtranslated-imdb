A Brother's Promise is a wonderful film for families. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, an Olympic champion. The film describes the life of this athlete from a young age through full adulthood. The love and support of family members is evident throughout. As Dan and the rest of his family manage to win and losing races is a life lesson for all of us. The commitment and determination of Dan's coach and his teammates shows what it takes to form a real team. The way in which Dan and his family deal with a devastating disease of a loved one is portrayed without giving up feelings or sugar. The faith of the family is shown in basic terms and is obviously an important part of their life. This is a powerful family movie that can be meaningful to a person of any age.
