THE INVATORS ARE A QUICK-MOVING SCI-FI FAST THAT SETS BEN CROSS AND SEAN YOUNG. BEN PLAYS RENN, A TRAVELER OF ANOTHER GALAXY LOOKING TO FIND ANNIE (PLAYING FROM SEAN) THAT IS PHYSICALLY IDEAL TO HAVE HER CHILD. THIS CHILD, IF PERMITTED TO BE BORNED AND RETURNED TO HIS PLANET, CAN BE THE ONLY POSSIBILITY OF SURVIVING HIS RACE. THE ENEMY, AN ALIEN WHO DESTROYED THE PEOPLE OF RENN, HAS ALSO FOLLOWED RENN IN CORRIDO TO THE STARS TO STOP THIS BIRTH. THE CROSS ADDS A LARGE RANGE FROM THE COMIC BEGINNERS AS THE ALIEN ENTERS A LOCAL BAR AND ORDER ITS FIRST EARTH COCKTAIL, TO HIS RACE WITH THE ENEMY AND DRAMA OF SE CAN CAN MAINTAIN ANNIE AND THE CHILD IS TRANSPORTING ALIVE.
