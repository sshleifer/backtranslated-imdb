An interesting animation on the fate of a giant tiger, a sloth and a mammoth, which saved a child, who was close to being killed by a group of tigers during the ice age. The film's morale shows that good behavior with others can ultimately bring benefits. One of the tigers in the group was ordered to finally capture the child, who was saved by his mother when the tigers attacked his community. The child was later rescued from the sloth and mammoth, but the tiger joined them with the aim of finally taking the child away. They crossed very difficult paths with great danger and immediately the tiger fell and was saved by the mammoth. Eventually the group of tigers tried to capture the child, but the mammoth incredibly helped his fellow tiger was able to overcome this attack and return the child to his father and the community to which he belongs.
