A group of friends decide to camp in the desert and find themselves chased and murdered by a mysterious killer in a black pickup. "Mirage" is obviously inspired by "Duel" by Spielberg and "The Hills Have Eyes" by Craven. "This slasher yarn offers a lot of violence and blood. The highlight of the film is a sequence involving a man holding his arm and leg chained around a tree and then having his limbs dismembered when the chain is pulled by a truck. also a bit of suspense and some exciting stalk-and-slash sequences. The acting is rather lame and the script is rather weak, but the film is fast and shocking. BGSteers who plays the villain is quite threatening. they provide an atmosphere and the blood is pretty strong.Overall, if you like low-budget horror movies, take a look at this out of 8.
