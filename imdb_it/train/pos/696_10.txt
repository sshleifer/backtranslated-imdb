Surprisingly well done for an independent film, An Insomniac's Nightmare paints a surprising picture of what it would be like to suffer from insomnia. Wonderfully well written and directed, it creates the atmosphere of a dream as the spectator goes through a night in the life of a sleepless person. <br /> <br /> Starring Dominic Monaghan as Jack, we can see everything he sees as they spend the long hours of a lonely night. The narration is almost mesmerizing and it is impossible to go away from the opening lines. Fascinating and slightly disturbing, shows how someone faces a lack of sleep, poised on the brink of sanity and madness. <br /> <br /> With twists behind each corner, An Insomniac's Nightmare is provocative and engaging. It comes highly recommended.
