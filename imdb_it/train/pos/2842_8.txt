New York I love you just like its predecessor (Paris Je T'Aime) is a mixture of various stories that reflects the different types and aspects of love, but unlike the rhythm it is much faster and the stories much shorter. The film offers a unique view of the city of New York with its various and diverse landscapes. New York, I Love You offers a first class cast, with actors such as Shia LaBeouf, Natalie Portman, Ian McKellen, Hayden Christensen, Chistina Rcci and Orlando Bloom, Ethan Hawke, James Caan and Robin Wright Penn among others and some excellent writers and directors like Brett Ratner and Anthony Mingella.
