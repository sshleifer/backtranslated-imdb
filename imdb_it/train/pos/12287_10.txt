Although the story is fictitious, it draws on the reality not only of the history of Latin American countries, but of the entire third world. This is the recent, true and pure history of these countries summarized concisely in this novel / film. The unusual supernatural stuff lightens the intensity of the historical events presented in this film. After all, supernatural things are part of third world culture. Although he is not critically acclaimed (probably due to supernatural things), this is an excellent film, with a great story and great acting.
