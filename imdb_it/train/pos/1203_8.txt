An exhilarating and accurate caricature of trying to sell a script. The documentary affects all the beats, plot points, character strings, seductions, moments of euphoria and disappointment and the fascination but the insane perspective of selling a script or getting an agent in Hollywood and all the fleeting rites of passage fleeting, who realize the fantasy but ultimately the companion of being socialized in the "system". Hotz and Rice capture the moment to think that you are finally a player, only to discover that what happens goes down quickly and in a blind way, that for unexplained reasons, Hollywood has gone ahead and let you control your heart, your dreams and your pockets. Pitch is a must for film school students to sample the ego mind and gantlet which is, for the most part, the way to go to sell themselves and their projects in Hollywood. If your teacher or guru has never been there, they can't tell you what you need to prepare for this gantlet. To enter the "biz", talent is necessary but not enough
