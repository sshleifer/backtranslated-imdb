Beautiful story of the Wisconsin native, Dan Jansen, and his real life, agonizingly struggling to win the 1994 Olympic gold medal in Speed ​​Skating, despite his overwhelming emotional loss with the death of his beloved best friend and family member; his sister, Jane. <br /> <br /> The main objective of this story is to sensitively represent the emotional turmoil of real life, what it feels like to lose a special Beloved and the struggle to face and overcome the great loss in a productive way! It is the incredible story of Dan Jansen's heartbreak in losing his beloved Sister in Leukemia, in his struggle to face the intensity of his pain, while maintaining his Dream to win the Olympics, and his last triumph in winning the Medal gold in speed Skating for America, and in honor of the memory of his sister; thus fulfilling his childhood promise to Jane! <br /> <br /> After the extraordinary performance of Dan Jansen's Gold Medal at the Lillihamer Winter Olympics in Norway, in 1994 he founded a Foundation to combat Leukemia, which claimed the life of his sister and supported programs of youth sports, education awards and scholarship. His Dan Jansen Foundation promotes the philosophy that: "as his father always told him at a young age: 'there is more to life than skating in circles!' Maintaining a proper perspective is fundamental, so set goals and realize your dreams through perseverance, overcome adversity and never give up! <br /> <br /> Recommended to anyone who has ever suffered from the great loss of someone very much special and loved in your life! And, for those who still believe in achieving dreams and never give up!
