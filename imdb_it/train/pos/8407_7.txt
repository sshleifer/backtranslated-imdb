The 1975 Sammy Johns hit album "Chevy Van" is heard repeatedly on the soundtrack (this film was also reprinted under the title CHEVY VAN), despite the film's title being a Dodge. Danny DeVito makes only six minutes of screen appearance, but countless VHS reissues falsely credit him as the star of the film. The film is a story of comparatively sexist morality: will Bobby find sexual satisfaction during the night when his customized van will facilitate, or will have to wait until Tina, the girl of his dreams, gives him the time of day? However, it is representative of the prevailing carnal dream of American male high school students of the time, and on this basis alone LA VAN, almost in spite of itself, becomes an artifact of the period to which one must refer in every honest retrospective of the famous cinema American of the period ...
