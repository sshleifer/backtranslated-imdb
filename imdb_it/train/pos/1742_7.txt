Perhaps, one of the most important and amusing Greek films I've seen in the last ten years. Excellent performances (in particular yiannis zouganelis is exceptional), a well-written script and effective directing by a very special, by the very average standards of Greece, author. A film, obviously influenced by Sam Peckinpah's Straw Dogs, could be a masterpiece if it avoided some obvious and exaggerated situations and symbolizations in the end. However, this is a film that deserves our attention and belongs to that rare category of Greek films that should be watched outside of Greece. It is a pity that in Greece it did not work commercially, in addition to other large false and superficial productions such as Politiki Kouzina ..
