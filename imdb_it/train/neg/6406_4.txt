An update of the scenes and jokes you would have seen on a Burlesque stage in the first half of the 20th century. It is a series of recited lines. Some of them could tell your grandmother, others don't, but it's a pretty safe bet that you've heard them before. For what it seeks to be, it is not bad. Before hiring it, remember that it is an older style of entertainment and has more value than history or comedy or titillation. Robin Williams has a couple of bits, but it's interchangeable with other players.
