This psychological thriller from the United Kingdom is known in the United States as CLOSING. Exploitation of Gillian Anderson of X-Files, who plays a very interesting middle-aged businesswoman named Alice. He has to attend a company party and invites Adam (Danny Dyer), who has just installed a security system for her, to be his escort. On the way home, hurtling through the woods on a narrow street, Alice's car collides with a deer. After removing the injured animal from the road, the couple is savagely attacked by a drunken gang of thugs. Adam is beaten to a pulp; Alice is raped by a gang and both are emotionally and physically devastated by the ruthless attack. When the identities of their attackers are discovered, Alice and Adam set off to take revenge ... brutal revenge. The couple sometimes disagrees on how to deal with the ruthless aggressors. Their final decision is to avenge without mercy. That there is no mistake, the refund is hell. Still in the cast: Anthony Calf, Ralph Brown, Francesca Fowler and Antony Byrne. Brutal violence, disturbing images, nudity and graphic rape.
