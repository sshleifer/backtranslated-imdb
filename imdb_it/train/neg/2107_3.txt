Bela Lugosi plays Dr. Lorenz who loves his wife so much that she will do everything to keep her young. This film begins with a wedding while the bride is about to take her vows that suddenly collapses. She is declared dead and taken away by gravediggers. The problem is that we are not dealing with real entrepreneurs, but with wounded people. A wave of deaths from the bride on the altar and their body that disappears confuses the police. Enter the journalist Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She tracks down Dr. Lorenz but also decides to use his youth to keep even his young wife.
