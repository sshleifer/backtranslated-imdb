Ali G was fun at the beginning. His interviews were fresh and original. At first the idea of ​​a fake gangster wearing OTT clothes and using street jargon was tempting. <br /> <br /> But this film is just a remake of old jokes, humor was mostly childish and revolved around the male sexual organ for the most part.
