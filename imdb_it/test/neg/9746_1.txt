This film was chosen, frankly like a pig in one shot from our local video store. It turned out to be quite a pig. The plot - as it is - was disjointed, inconsistent and predictable. The actors seemed constantly embarrassed to put badly written lines in their mouths. The only funny moment of the whole film revolved around the dangers of smoking and the involuntary deployment of an airbag. The rest of the film lacked the punch to amuse my two daughters (under the age of ten), me, my wife, my mother-in-law, or even the cat or dog. My advice, if you are tempted to borrow this turkey, is to save your money and your time. Search for something else ...
