Surrender Cinema is known for its extremely erotic and almost explicit science fiction films. While they generally do very well with these (Femalien 1 & 2, Virtual Encounters), this particular item seems to be just a remake of the old tape. Any number of girls - some recognizable and others not - are in this film at all stages of nudity. There are also several clips of other films that are exceptional, unless you've seen those other films. There are a number of solo nude scenes that do a number of things: some play basketball, others talk, others still play with themselves. The only thing of value in this tape is the last scene - a short but erotic girl-girl scene with a very enthusiastic and pleasant Sandy Wasko and a more subdued Tammie Hainum. Not highly recommended.
