The word Ghilli actually means a small wooden game instrument that is used in a game called ghilli-danda (a forerunner of Cricket) in India. The use of the word as a nickname for the main character is elegant, as it means one that is strong, fast and can seriously injure when rubbed in the wrong way. <br /> <br /> Ghilli is one of the best films for Vijay and has no rivals in its rhythm and action. The film never loosens for a moment, and always keeps you with some exciting action. The film is set in Madurai and Chennai and its story is simple. A wealthy owner tries to desire a beautiful girl in his city, and his undisputed power and authority prevents the girl from seeking justice. The hero tries to save her and most of this film portrays the week in which all this action takes place. <br /> <br /> A very exciting film and even if the story is not new, the director and the actors receive praise for the action at full speed.
