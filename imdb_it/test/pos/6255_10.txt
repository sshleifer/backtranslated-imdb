The "Inhabited" film is about a family of four who moves into a new home without knowing that the dangers are lurking. The daughter befriends dangerous creatures as they gain her trust. When they do they use it against her. A man who lives there as a child or as an adolescent knows the creatures and tries to warn them. The creatures are made of steel and take shiny objects to get new things, mainly weapons. They have a cat and murderers have disappeared. The man who tried to warn the little sister was deceived by them and ended up in a psychiatric ward. Then he helps them defeat the creatures and destroy the man.
