"Beyond the Clouds" is an artistic group of four exaggerated vignettes that offers a glimpse of a man-woman relationship from tenuous to turbulent. Although the film offers superb cinematography, exquisite visual beauty and a cast of refined artists, there is little flesh on the bones of this fragmented work. A taste of a relationship cannot impart its fullness and synergy suggests that much more can be achieved with a two-hour history than four. Nevertheless, "Beyond the Clouds" will be forage for amateurs and a visual feast for the whole, even if superficial, prodded and without substance.
