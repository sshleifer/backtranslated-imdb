This film is an absolute cinematic genius. It has a well combined cast that gives an almost magical performance. The effects are nothing but amazing and the story will keep you hanging on the chair during the movie. Jack Long plays the part of the white abbot exceptionally well, offering an immensely exciting representation of absolute evil. If you are kung fu enthusiast or just an action movie fan, this movie is an excellent choice for anyone who is lucky enough to find a copy. For every great kung fu fan this film offers a compelling view of the world of Shaolin. This film is certainly quality 10/10 and should be considered one of the greatest oriental films of all time.
