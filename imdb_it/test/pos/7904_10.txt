"Alone" is a moving story of four kids who run away from the adoptive home after their mother died, with one suitcase, their dog Ralph and $ 9 left with their mother's old car in search of their uncle Jack. In the way their car breaks down and they meet Peggy Williams, a teacher on vacation and offers to help them. Together she and the children look for Uncle Jack, but are forced to hide from the authorities who plan to return them to foster care while they do it. This film tells a story of love, friendship and how important it is to stay with your family while the children venture into their incredible journey.
