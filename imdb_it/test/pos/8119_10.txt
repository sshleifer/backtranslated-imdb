Bedrooms and corridors is the kind of film that makes you understand that the drama of life is hidden in the details. And even this drama can be a lot of fun! Even if your love life and your friends may not be as unique as the characters in this movie, everyone is required to recognize himself and others in the colorful protagonists of this film. And this is the great strength of Bedroom and Hallways, which the irony of life is that the most confusing things that happen to you can be the most interesting / hilarious / funny / sexy / ... things too! I hope you enjoy the movie as much as I do!
