This film may appear superficially brazen and for teen-boppers. It seems to be the stereotypical high school movie. But in reality, it takes an honest and touching look at the high school experience. It does not propose an unrealistic ending in which the fanatics and popular boys are reconciled. It does not change what happened in adolescence; just try to deal with it. This is a really nice, funny, heart-warming movie, good for teenagers and for those who can still remember being one ...
