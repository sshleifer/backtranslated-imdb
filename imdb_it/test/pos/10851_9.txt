A young woman leaves her provincial life for a new one in the city and there she meets another woman with whom she falls in love. Their relationship quickly turns into a physical and both believe they are soul mates, until one day the provincial girl returns home to look for a man in their bed. Her lover then tells her that their relationship was just an experiment and she really likes men. Um, kind of like the thing about Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. So, however, the provincial girl, destroyed, torn apart and destroyed by this discovery moves away and begins to discover what the real world is as it falls into the hands of all kinds of vengeful and salacious people in 19th century England.
