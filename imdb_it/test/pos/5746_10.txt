A young man discovers that life is precious after he was seriously injured in a car accident. His father is a pastor who changes his outlook on life and spends the entire movie sharing his new love for others in this shocking and moving film. The theme is built around street motor racing and is an excellent film for the people who are part of it and for films like "the fast and the furious". This shows how people can overcome their problems and gives the general message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
