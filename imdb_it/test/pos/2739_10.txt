If I remember correctly we had to watch it on a UHF station and this meant using a UHF converter in those days that was not part of normal TV to tune in to the local station to see the cartoon a big problem in those days that made the show even more mysterious. I remember all the sound effects that Gerald was talking about. A great memory over 50 years ago. I will have to see what other memories could be hidden on the web.
