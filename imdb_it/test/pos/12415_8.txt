Two college classmates - one a tense nerd, the other a crude idiot - embark on a journey across the country. Along the way they encounter a vicious vehicle that looks like an army tank combined with a monster truck, which tries to run its own car. They escape, but only annoy the mysterious and dangerous driver when one of them takes a leak in the upper hatch while he stops. Later, they pick up a sexy hitchhiker who ends up being involved in their life-threatening situation. MONSTER MAN is an extremely funny horror comedy that has some good moments of suspense and also some of the best ones. The two main characters and their constant quarrel are fun to watch alone and eventually take a turn similar to that of a TCM that has been very well done. Absolutely to try
