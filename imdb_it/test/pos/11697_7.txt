Crackerjack is another classic Australian film. Crackerjack is a sports movie that is much slower than many of those you will find as Remember the Titans or Million Dollar Babybut, the characters involved are full-fledged athletes. This film is a showcase of a vast area of ​​Australian culture and presents a sport that is popular and growing in popularity in Australia. Mick Molloy presents a classic and unforgettable character.
