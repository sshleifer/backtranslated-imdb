From the late Sidney Pollack comes a love story about human nature, pain, passion and betrayal. Dutch police sergeant Van Den Broeck (Harrison Ford) is devastated when he learns that his beloved wife was killed in a plane crash, he is even more upset to learn of his relationship with the husband of a famous congressman Kay Chandler (Kristen Scott Thomas). He organizes to meet her and see if he has known or had no idea. They begin to make friends with each other, then from their mutual pain and anguish they begin an intense relationship (which could be seen as a cure for both). Do the memories of the dead and the pressures of their respective jobs drive them away and cause more harm than good or can they escape from the past? Well, you'll have to watch Random Hearts and find out. A moving and well acted film with an exceptional cast.
