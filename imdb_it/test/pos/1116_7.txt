This is a great film for families that deals with people who love their local interests and who are mainly involved in horse racing. Lloyd Bourdelle, (Walter Matthau) is a farmer who also raises quarter quarter and has a young son named Casey Bourdelle, who loves horses. Lloyd is able to raise an adult horse he calls "Casey's Shadow" after his son and this horse break all speed records and is a possible winner of a million dollar race. Sarah Blue, (Alexis Smith) is interested in this horse and offers to buy the horse for $ 500.00, however, this horse suffers serious leg injuries and Lloyd receives a serious injury that upsets the whole family. Walter Matthau gave an exceptional performance and this is a great film to see and have fun.
