So, Americans do t.v. film-based series, while we Brits make films based on t.v. shows. It should never work, but on this occasion it does so because of a sublime meeting of character and actor. Cliches are sometimes rightly so, and Leonard Rossiter was born to play Rigsby. This is one of the great comic creations, a bit like Norman Bates would have found out if he had been melancholy instead of a murderer.
