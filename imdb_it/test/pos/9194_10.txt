As in "Les amants du Pont-Neuf" two strangers live a love story without concessions. The film consists of many interesting conversations and many sweet moments. The best comes in a listening booth. They listen to a record together and sometimes they look at each other. They talk, they like each other. She suggests a change in their lives, but he is out of hope. Realistic style destroys realistic (but) emotional dialogues. The work of a truly mathematical writer for this film. The Spanish novelist Jesus Ponce creates one of the most perfect galleries of the last year of Spanish cinema.
