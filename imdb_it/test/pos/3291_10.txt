Scooby Doo and Garfield's most entertaining animal film. The story revolves around a 23-year-old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems in his boss's company seem to continue to fail, Brian is also dating the boss's daughter, Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robotic dog named CHOMPS, modeled on his Rascal dog. But CHOMPS is not a normal dog, it is as fast as a cheetah, has an X-ray view, can jump about 6 feet and has a strength of 20 men. The new invention impresses his boss and makes his business a success. But when company rivals hear about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to capture it. CHOMPS can beat them before it's too late. This film is a classic for all ages.
