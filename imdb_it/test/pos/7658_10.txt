Latcho Drom is a cinematographic survey of gypsy music from different countries. It is moving, sad and joyful. Most segments seem to be completely unpayed, not included. The music, which ranges from the sensual music of the flamenco of the Spanish gypsies, to the melancholy music of the Central European gypsies, is exquisite.
