A young Korean artist lives in Amsterdam. He is a little lonely and has never had a serious relationship, insisting that he is "waiting" for the right person. He works in the public square, he draws portraits for passers-by but, for himself, he also allows himself to paint his favorite flowers, daisies. But suddenly he has a secret admirer. The flowers are delivered to his residence every day at 4:15 am, usually daisies, yet he can't capture the sender red-handed. This is because, unknown to her, her boyfriend is a Korean killer, and for the moment she wants to remain hidden. One day in the square, however, another charming Korean man sits for the artist and is holding a vase of daisies. He concludes that he is his shy hero and, moreover, the man he was waiting to find. This second gentleman also has a secret; he is an Interpol agent. The killer can see everything that happens in the square, due to the proximity of an apartment. Of course, he is troubled by the fact that another man has entered the girl's life. How will this dark love triangle develop? This is a beautiful image to look at. The setting in Amsterdam and the surrounding countryside is very, very beautiful. Add three more attractive young Korean actors and, visually, each viewer has a breathtaking view before them. The story is also very beautiful, being a mixture of drama and action, with a hint of unexpected. The costumes are very interesting and the production values ​​are high. In short, anyone looking for a quality foreign film with a compelling story and fantastic scenery will find this film a wonderful choice for a fun evening. If you have the hand of someone to hold during the visualization, so much the better.
