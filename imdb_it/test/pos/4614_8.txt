In this flame of emotions we are introduced to several high school kids during a busy day at school. All of them struggle with belonging, finding them and hiding their painful secrets from being revealed. It has the feeling of a documentary on growth focused on the inner landscape of the students and on their way of facing the daily challenges. It opens with a powerful opening and develops like a requiem hymn. Use the camera to move with great skill and slowly invite us into the mind and heart of young people. An imaginary reconstruction that enlightens us all and reminds us of the fragility of youth. One of the most important films of today. every teenager should be given the opportunity to see this moving film.
