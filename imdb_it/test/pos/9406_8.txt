This great television movie tells of Martha Moxley's 1975 murder in Greenwich, Connecticut, by an Ethel Kennedy nephew. The use of Martha's "ghost" to provide some details was very effective and added a lot of heart to the story. Christopher Meloni seemed to capture Mark Fuhrman's personality very well. Furhman, who got so little publicity in the OJ. The Simpson trial was certainly confirmed by his contribution in bringing the murderer to justice after about 27 years.
