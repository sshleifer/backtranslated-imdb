"Brotherhood of Satan" is one of the most underrated horror movies of all time. Because it has not reached a cult status it is beyond me. This is a chilling story of terror and sorcery that contains one of the most powerful and disturbing climaxes in the small American city some children disappeared and their parents were violently killed. What is the cause of hysteria? The film is really disturbing, some scenes are really unnerving and certainly not easily forgotten. The suspense never leaves up, the acting is really good, and the climax is bizarre and disturbing. Discover this forgotten gem of satanic horror. Absolutely recommended.
