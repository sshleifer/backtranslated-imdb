Too bad, nobody seems to know this little masterpiece thriller. Where films with higher budgets fail, "Terminal Choice" offers many thrills, shocks and bloody violence. A little-known gem that deserves to be sought in your local video store. That anonymous guy downstairs is right, when he says, you would never trust hospitals again ... it's so effective! Good ending, too, not really a coup de theater, but it does not end as previously thought. Yes, that is Ellen Barkin in an early role ...
