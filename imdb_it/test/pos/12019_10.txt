Richard Norton really turns the screen on in this martial arts masterpiece based in Portland, Oregon. Norton, an Australian heartthrob, plays the evil Mr. Milverstead, who runs an important import / export business by smuggling weapons and participating in the female meat trade. Usually women are torn from their favorite nightclub with the help of a team of goons, the best known of who is Bolo Yeung, playing the role of Ice. The troubles come for Milverstead when a new policeman in the city John Kim (Britton Lee) is ready to avenge his murder of dead partners at the hands of the Milverstead organization. If you have time to see only a martial arts film this year, don't miss this classic.
