A good drama about a case of custody for children with great performances of all the actors. A good example of what an excellent script can do to push a simple story to a much higher quality. The script was just average and this is what kept the film from the list of the best dramas of all time. Still, the big acting makes this movie a good movie to see if you are a fan of court dramas or a big fan of the main actors. The film should have been a little longer for more excellence but that would really be the fussy ......
