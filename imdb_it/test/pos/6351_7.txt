A touching love story that you remember ?? In the Mood for Love '. Drawing heavily on Chinese poetry and how it is used by people from the east to communicate feelings, the story focuses on a teacher who wants to be a model teacher so much as a good husband and father. An elderly student is very attracted to him. As the story unfolds, we see the emotions below the surface in his 20-year marriage and how he is grappling with the moral dilemmas that confront him. A beautiful and moving story.
