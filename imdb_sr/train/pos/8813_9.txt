Although the pieces are uneven, this collection of 11 short films is truly a mobile and human experience. There were some who, after emotions at the anniversary of the bombing, thought it was antiameric. I think that's not the case, although some parts can be taken in this way if you do not look beyond the obvious. In the end, the film is nothing but an attempt by people to express their confusion, sympathy and feelings about what happened. These are stories of people who are shocked by what happened on Tuesday in September. As I said this movie will move you, probably to tears. It's not always easy to watch, for example, a movie from Mexico is more than a black screen with sound, but its effect is that it even sets the strongest people low. If you can be strong, you really should see this movie. It will comfort you and enlighten you and affect you ...
