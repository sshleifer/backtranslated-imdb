For lovers of Troma or Ciberpunk genre mixed with a little blood shed this film for you! There is a good amount of blood spilled over the boundaries of this film, and the effects can be impressive and terrible. The action is funny and refreshing, not related to what we as audiences expect from movies these days. All in all, it's a good movie for fans of this genre. Also recommended are films such as Tetsuo: Iron Man and Versus for this strange approach to a Japanese film, filled with wonderful ambiguity and bloodshed.
