This is a story of Australian commandos who were caught in the uniform after the raid. As they are out of uniform, they are properly treated as spies. As such, they were tried, convicted, and sentenced to death. The Japanese military court, out of admiration for their heroism, approves of their warlike death. Of course, under the Bushido code, that means they need to shave their heads. The fate for which, as Westerners, is unprepared.
