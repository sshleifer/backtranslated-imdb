The promise of a brother is a wonderful family movie. This is the biography of Dan Jansen, the Olympic Speed ​​Skaters Champion. The film shows the life of this athlete from his youth to the full mature age. The love and support of family members is obvious throughout the world. How Dan and the rest of his family manage victory and loss is a life lesson for all of us. The dedication and determination of Dan's coach and his teammates shows what is needed to make the right team. As Dan and his family are confronted with the devastating illness of a loved one, they are shown without undue feelings or sugars. The family's faith is shown in basic terms and apparently a large part of their lives. This is a powerful family movie that can be significant for a person of any age.
