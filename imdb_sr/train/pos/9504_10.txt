As many people know, Mexican cinema was very poor after the so-called Golden Age of Mexican film, fortunately, during the late 90s and early 21st centuries, great films such as La Lei de Herodes, Bajo California, Amores Perros, I There appeared Mom Tambien and, of course, El Coronel No Tiene Kuien le Escriba. El Coronel ... is a wonderful film that recounts the classic story of Gabriel Garcia Markuez, eliminating the elements of magical realism, and replacing them with the raw reality that lived in Mexico, not just of the people like Colonel who are waiting for their pensions, but more than half Mexican populations living in total poverty. The characters of the film satirically represent the classic characters found in a Mexican society, such as a nationalist colonel, a cold and even an ambitious priest, a hypocrite, but also a loyal partner, a tolerant and patient woman, a hidden gay, etc. film, it's mandatory to see if you want to know more about Mexican society, and especially if you want to watch a wonderful movie, one of the best Mexican directors
