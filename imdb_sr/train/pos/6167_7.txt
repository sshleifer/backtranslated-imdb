"Such a long journey" is a well-made movie, a good shot and a showcase for some good performances. However, the story is such a mess of subplots and peculiar characters that it becomes a kind of Jack of all entangled and master of none. Also, Western audiences will probably find the esoteric of the rather obscure Parsee culture a bit to gather in 1.7 hours. It is recommended for those who are interested in India.
