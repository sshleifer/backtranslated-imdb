In life we ​​first organize stones (Piedras in Spanish) such as career, family, friendship and love. In this way, we will find space between them to cost less stones, our little needs. If you are acting inversely, you will not have enough room for larger stones. The five protagonists in this film are women who could not organize large "stones" in their lives. Ramon Salazar, the Spanish film director, defines his first feature of Stones in this way. The film talks about a parallel, conflicting journey of five women: Anita (Monica Cervera, 1975-), Isabel (Angela Molina, 1955-), Adela (Antonia San Juan, 1961-), Leire (Najva Nimri, 1972-), and Maricarmen Vicki Pena, 1954) Everybody tries to remove stones that persistently appear on their way, or, worst, it's in their shoes. They are five Cinderella in search of Prince Charming and a new chance in life. The best story of these five Cinderella is Anita (Monica Cervera), who also stars in "20 centimeters", "Busco", "Ferpecto crime", "Entre Vivir and Sonar", "Hongos" and "Octavia".
