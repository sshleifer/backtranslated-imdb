"Fido" should boast of taking tired genre, zombies and turning into the most original film experience. The atmosphere in the early fifties is a stunning, brilliant acting, and the whole production shows a lot of careful planning. Suddenly the viewer is immersed in the world of beautiful classic cars, "Eisenhover era" dresses, art deco furniture and zombie officers. It would be very easy to dismiss "Fido" as a cartoon, similar to "Tank Girl", but two films are quite different. "Fido has a structure, a script that tells the story, and a performance that is superior. Do not make a mistake, this is a black comedy that thrives where many others have failed.
