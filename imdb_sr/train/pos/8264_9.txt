Based on a real mining disaster, this early German talkie (with English subtitles) remains one of the most effective docu-drama ever recorded. With many unprofessional actors, "Kameradschaft" gives a cool opinion of the friendship that connects workers in mines, regardless of which side of the French-German border they may be. A deadly accident provokes the best in everyone, overthrowing the orders of all superiors. A friend in need will receive the help of his comrades, even under the threat of great loss, including life. . Leaving aside all formal rules and rules, these men and women in uniform knew only one reason: save lives and find their fighters. - More than 70 years later, "Kameradschaft" still has a strong and timeless message: A friend in trouble is a friend in action.
