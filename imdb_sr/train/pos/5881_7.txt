This film was a fantastic comedy. There were a lot of comedy stars like Akshai Kumar, Rajpal Iadava, Paresha Raval and John Abraham. Mr Hot as Mac (Akshai Kumar) and Mr Cool as Sam (John Abraham) are two fashion photographers who love the same girl Maggie (Neha Dupia). When John Abraham is cheating on his work, Aksha Kumars becomes older and Akshai Kumar becomes really jealous because his apartment has to surrender to John Abraham, and Neha Dupia loves John more. Akshai Kumar wants to be better than John Abraham, finding the apartment and coming out with three different girls (Nit Chandra, Nargis Bagheri, Daisy Boppana).
