This is a documentary for anyone who fears that modern youth has lost the taste of real adventure and its sense of morality. Darius Goes West is a stunning story. We live the lives of Darius and the crew as they embark on a journey of life. Darius has Duchenne's muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects all the muscles in his body. He is limited to a wheelchair, and he needs attention around an hour. How could this team of young friends manage to take him on a return trip of 6,000 kilometers to the West Bank and back? Watch the movie and experience the ups and downs of this great adventure - laugh and pay with the crew while riding with unimaginable challenges on the road, and enjoy the ultimate triumph when they return three weeks later in their hometown to excited reception and some great surprises!
