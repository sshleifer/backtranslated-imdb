There is no romance or other side story for this movie, it's an action and an intrigue to the end, making it a real male kung fu movie. The elderly master sends his last student, Ian Tieh, to stop his five. Former students with styles represent five poisonous animals of stonegy, snake, scorpion, lizard and frog. Despite the word "Venom" in the title, none of these students use poison to kill their opponents. Ian Tieh, who was told by his teacher that he disagrees with five students, must find the one he can join in order to defeat the other four. As Ian Tieh and others find each other, it's an intrigue of the story, with a good kung-fu action that spreads through the story. Recognized as a cult classic, this film has already been established in the kung-fu movie action anali. It is known that other films relate to the five styles shown in this story. It is not an artistic masterpiece, with the usual bad synchronized sound, and stupid acting, but the movie is one of the best of its kind, because it is so focused on all the ingredients of the kung-fu action movie of its time, and gives an additional concentrated dose of them. One movie you have to watch if you are kung-fu movie fan.
