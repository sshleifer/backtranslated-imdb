Clara Bov (Hula Calhoun) is daughter of plantation owner Alberto Grant (Bill Calhoun), who is mostly interested in tickets and drunken friends. She is interested in riding in the countryside until engineer Clive Brook (Anthony Haldane) appears to build a dam. One of her father's friends, Arlette Marchal (Ms. Bane), then compete for his attention. His wife, Maude Trudel (Margaret Haldane), appeared on a fictional finale.
