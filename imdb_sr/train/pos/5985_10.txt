God, I love this movie. If you grew up in the '80s and you love Heavi Metal, this is a movie for you. They really do not get much better than this. The Fastvai soundtrack is one of the best soundtracks ever. When I first got out, I took the record and spent the next month learning each song on the guitar note. The action is your standard Heavi Metal horor movie. A childish favorite singer dies. Kid plays backwards. Hero returns in demonic form and stones the city. What more can you ask for? If you have not seen it, hurry up and buy it. You will not be disappointed. Metal Rules ...
