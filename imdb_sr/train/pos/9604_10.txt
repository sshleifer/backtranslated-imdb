"Darius Goes West" is a moving story about a brave teenager who faces Duchenne's muscular dystrophy and his personal quest to see the Pacific. He receives help and encouragement from a group of young people who love and care for him while going on this quest. The story has a natural drama and a sincere demonstration of the commitment of the young to help one of Everyone who thinks young are autocentric and narcissistic, will find this film to turn that stereotype into his head. It is the power of young people and their engagement in Darius's situation, which is very tempting in this documentary.
