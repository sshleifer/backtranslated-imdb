Based on the autobiographical novel, Kristi Braun, this sweet film talks about his life, he is affected by cerebral palsy, and they do not regard him, including his mother, as a person. Incredibly, he learns to draw and write using his foot, which is the only part of him that he can control. An incredible story of courage with the truly amazing and unforgettable Oscar winning Daniel Levis. 9 of 10
