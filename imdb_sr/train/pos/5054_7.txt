Richard Attenborough, who has already given us the films "Chorus Line" and "Gandhi", once again surprises us for making a beautiful anthem of Nature. Indeed, the vast and (at that time) unexplored territory of Canada helps to compile the staggering beauty of landscapes captured by the camera. If the film is truly based on a true story, it becomes obvious once again that "people of vision" are, in fact, people who live beyond their time, with a historical perspective that will only give them reason. Cinema is magnificent, such as the actor Pierce Brosnan, whose performance is thanks to the master's hands of Attenborough. Annoyingly surprising is the appearance of Annie Galipeau as Archie's beloved. A film that has to appear on the list of those who really love nature ...
