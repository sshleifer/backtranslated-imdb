Once again, two professors who argue must unite to save the lost world. Five members of the first expedition return (see The Lost World, 1992, for the list of actors). A man looking for oil brings the drilling team to the plateau. Instead of hitting oil, they release an underground volcano that endangers all life in a lost world. The oil team is confronting domicile people and a scientific expedition. Although the situation looks hopeless ... (I will not tell you the end).
