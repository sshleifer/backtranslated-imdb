His choice of films, the basic 'idea' of production (which puts him in sets or simulacres of the movies he is commenting on) and his delivery are brilliant! But if you want Freud, be aware that you get Zizek's version of Lacan, which must not be confused with Lacan itself. As usual, Žižek delivers complex ideas with pleasure and in a convincing way. The claim is that he is also quite lively, so in his enjoyment can be more than in actual content. Cinematic, it's a gem. Psychologically, this will have people of all conviction (Freudovci, Lakanci and Jungis) who will scratch their heads, but still reach for popcorn. Zizek is a phenomenon and pop icon.
