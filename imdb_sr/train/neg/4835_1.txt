Kathryn Bigelow and Mark Boal are already preparing a sequel to a young US Corporal in Afghanistan. He is also a highly qualified surgeon and freely wanders around Kabul, working on wounded NATO soldiers. In a particularly difficult task, he accidentally picked up a sniper rifle and fired at Usama bin Laden from a distance of about 3000 yards. He was eventually promoted to a sergeant, but he was not able to decide between the career of a sniper and a surgeon, so he completely abandoned the army. A year later, frustrated with civilian life, joining the Navy, and the last scene shows that he is proudly wearing a white uniform.
