The resurrected wrecked monster goes to murder. The craze is seeking revenge against the living members of the previous expedition. It seems that the universal program lacks packaging as well as new ideas. Most of the budget is probably spent on Lon Chanei Jr. to play Kharis, mummy. Other players include George Zucco, Vallace Ford, Turhan Bei, Dick Foran and Elise Knock. How long can this madness continue?
