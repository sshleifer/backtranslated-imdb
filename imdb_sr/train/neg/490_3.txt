Artemesia takes the usual story about the world of art, e.g. "You can not paint it! But I want!" and spray it with sex and scandal so the whole movie, good, interesting, but not remarkable. The story is about one of the first female painters in the area, Artemesia who of course, is fiercely independent, but just can not stop thinking about men, and their bodies? Of course for artistic purposes. Soon he gets private teaching of one of the famous artists, but soon teaching becomes much more than art, and shortly afterwards a scandal arises! It's strange how they could take a historical biography and turn it into a soft pornographic fantasy. I mean, was Artemesia such a person? Also, it's pretty funny when insisting that "painting for yourself!" still falls on the first person he sees. In fact, the story itself is fascinating and ends with the trial, which I always love.
