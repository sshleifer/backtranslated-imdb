White Lugosi plays dr. Lorenzo, who so much likes his wife that he will do everything to stay young. This film begins with a wedding, as the bride will vow to suddenly collapse. She was pronounced dead and taken away by the funeral. The trouble is that these are not real funerals but bodyguards. The death wave of young people on the altar and the disappearance of their bodies confuses the police. Enter the journalist Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She finds Dr. Lorenzo, but also decides to use her youth to keep her young wife young.
