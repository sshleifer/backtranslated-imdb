An interesting animation about a large tiger, a sloth and the fate of a mammoth that has saved a baby who was killed by a group of tigers in the ice age. The boldness of the film shows that the good behavior of others can bring benefits at the end. One of the tigers eventually came to seize the baby, and the tigers did not save his mother when she attacked her community. The baby was then rescued by sloth and mammoth, but the tiger finally met them with the intention of removing the baby. They went through very dangerous paths, and at any time the tiger fell and was protected by mammoth. Eventually, a group of tigers attempted to catch the baby but Mammoth helped his tiger colleague very much to overcome this attack and decided to give the baby back to his father and his belongings.
