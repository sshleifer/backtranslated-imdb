The most enjoyable pet movie since Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around a 23-year-old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems in his boss's company seem to continue to fail, Brian is also dating the boss's daughter named Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robotic dog called CHOMPS, based on his own Rascal dog. But CHOMPS is not an ordinary dog, he is as fast as a cheetah, he has x-ray vision, can jump about six feet and has a force of 20 men. The new invention impresses your boss and makes your business a success. But when the company's rivals hear about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to capture it. CHOMPS can fool them before it's too late. This movie is a classic for all ages.
