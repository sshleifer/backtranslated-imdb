For avid science fiction fans, this movie is exactly what you've been waiting for. Watching this movie leaves you lost in the characters, especially Riddick, the movie guy. This is the case where you cheer for the bad guy and want to see him live and win. As you watch the survivors struggle to stay alive, you see who lives and how they survive those unknown creatures that have taken over this planet. An excellent film, Vin Diesel did a wonderful job as convicted Riddick and the acting and thriller were breathtaking. A +
