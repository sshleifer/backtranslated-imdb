there is one of the best films directed by andrzej wajda, the story told about the young writer who is looking for his place after a second war (he survives the German camp) .excellent real atmosphere (action happens in the countryside for displaced persons), main hero (played by one of the best Polish actor daniel olbrychski) finally fall in love, but unfortunately his lady was dead. there was a beautiful scene, when he is talking to American soldier and says (about his girl's death) "nothing is happening, you are simply shooting at us now ... he is in the condition of soul destroyed.
