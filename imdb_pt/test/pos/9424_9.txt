Basil Rathbone and Nigel Bruce return as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson in this Holmes tale fighting against the 3rd Reich and the idealizing genius of Professor Moriarty. The film opens in Switzerland, while Holmes is disguised as an "old bookseller." He should bring Dr. Tobel and the Tobel Bomb Site to England before the Germans can kidnap Tobel. Holmes succeeds and the Germans recite the evil teacher. Moriarty manages to fool the Scottish shipyard and LeStrad "Dennis Hoey" kidnapping Tobel. The only clue left by Tobel is a list of "dancing men." Who will break the hidden code of men dancing, Holmes or Moriarty first? Could Holmes prevent the bomb site from falling into the hands of the Germans, saving England from the precision bombing techniques developed by the site of the Tobel bomb? Watch and enjoy.
