A young woman leaves her provincial life for a new one in the city and there she meets another woman she falls in love with. Their relationship becomes physical quickly and both believe they are soul mates, until one day, the provincial girl comes home to find a man in her bed. Her lover then reveals to her that her relationship was just an experiment and she really likes men. Um, like the thing about Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. So, anyway, the provincial girl, broken, ripped apart and torn by this discovery, begins to discover what the real world is when it falls into the hands of all sorts of vindictive and lascivious nineteenth-century England.
