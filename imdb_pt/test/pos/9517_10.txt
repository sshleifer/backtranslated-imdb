If you want an excellent search of Byzantine history done in a colorful way, this is for you. This documentary would also be excellent for educators, who are teaching about Roman or medieval history. This documentary is divided into three parts, first dealing with the fall of the Roman Empire of the West, the rise of Christianity and the beginning of the Byzantine Empire. The second video deals with Byzantine diplomacy and iconoclastic controversy. The third and final video explains the decline and fall of Byzantium. The series is filmed in several countries and beautifully integrates Byzantine history into the realities of the modern world, showing the place of this civilization as part of human civilization in general. Do, take the time to watch.
