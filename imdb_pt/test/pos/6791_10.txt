National Velvet (1944) The movie that put Taylor on the map. Costarring then number one boxing champion, Mickey Rooney. Taylor plays a girl in love with her horse and when the jockey Mickey Rooney is unable to walk in the Grand National race, Liz cuts off her hair, pretends to be a boy and runs the horse to ... well, I will not ruin the order for you . It's a quality melodrama that MGM was famous for releasing. And it's another initial color image to boot!
