"Alone" is a poignant story of four children fleeing a foster home after their mother dies with only one suitcase, her dog Ralph and $ 9 departing in her old mother's car in search of her Uncle Jack. On the way, their car breaks down and they find Peggy Williams, a vacation teacher, and she volunteers to help them. Together, she and the children search for Uncle Jack, but are forced to hide from the authorities who plan to take them back to foster homes while they do. This film tells a story of love, friendship and how important it is to stay with your family while the children venture on their incredible journey.
