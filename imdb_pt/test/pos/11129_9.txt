Of course this happened in the early 80's. Of course. Those theater students dancing down the street to Irene Cara's famous theme song, is an indelible image of the eighties. But there is more to the film than that. There is deprivation, and a man's struggle to learn to read, and a fight against sexuality, and an attack on a child, and a girl cheated while taking topless photographs and contemplating suicide. In the end though, it's also about that song.
