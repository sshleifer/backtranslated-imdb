An amazing piece of film that was well designed and kept me on the edge of my seat. Brilliantly orchestrated in its time, and the comedy came into action just when the tension needed a release. The acting was generally well done (the "Director" should have asked Alec Guinness for acting classes), and the shooting selections were impressive (as in stretching the hall while Billy tries to run to the door in the studio). This movie has not given up since the opening scene ...
