La Petit Tourette is a very funny episode of South Park.Cartman is in the toy store one day and here is a child cursing out loud but do not get in trouble for it.Your mother then tells everyone that the kid has "tourette syndrome." Cartman loves the fact that he can swear without getting in trouble, so he tells everyone that he has tourette syndrome. Kyle, however, finds that Cartman is lying and tries to tell people, but they feel that he is insensitive and is put on a sensitivity training. Cartman's tourette eventually puts him on a talk show, but he discovers he can not control his tourettes and starts saying embarrassing things that happen to him. Meanwhile, Kyle tries to sabotage the show in an interesting way.
