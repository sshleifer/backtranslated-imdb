This is one of the best martial art films (Kung Fu) of all time. If you like martial art movies, this is a not to be missed. From flying nuns to monks in training, this movie has kung fu styles and a good story. It is about a clan priest wu dang trying to eliminate all shaoulin fighters to claim the title of being the best in martial arts. after killing key members of shaoulin's temple, the faith of the shoulin remains in the hands of two boys secretly training under a shaoulin monk. The white abbot or the priest of the wu dang clan develops new techniques that transform it into iron. Well, that's not to miss the classic. Involves fighters Ninjas, Shaoulin and Nuns. it's a great classic
