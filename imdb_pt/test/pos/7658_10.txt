Latcho Drom is a film research of gypsy music from various countries. It is moving, sad and cheerful. Most segments appear to be completely stageless, not rehearsed. The music, ranging from the sensual flamenco music of the Spanish gypsies to the melancholy music of the gypsies of Central Europe, is exquisite. If you like gypsy music, you'll find Latcho Drom absolutely beautiful. <br /> <br />
