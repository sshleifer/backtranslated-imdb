A young Korean artist lives in Amsterdam. She is a little lonely and has never had a serious relationship, insisting that she is "waiting" for the right person. She works in the public square, drawing portraits for passers-by, but for herself she also devotes herself to painting her favorite flowers, daisies. But all of a sudden, she has a secret admirer. The flowers are delivered to her residence every day at 4:15, usually daisies, but she can not pick up the sender in the act. This is because, unknown to her, her boyfriend is a Korean killer, and he wants to remain hidden, for now. One day in the square, however, another attractive Korean man sits down to the artist and starts carrying a vase of daisies. She concludes that he is her shy hero and also the man she was waiting to meet. This second gentleman also has a secret; he is an Interpol agent. The killer can see everything happening in the square due to having an apartment close by. Naturally, he is disturbed that another man has entered into the life of the young woman. How will this sombre love triangle unfold? This is a beautiful image to watch. The scenery in Amsterdam and the surrounding countryside is very, very lovely. Bring together three young, handsome Korean actors, and visually any viewer has an amazing panorama ahead of them. The story is very good too, being a mix of drama and action, with a hint of the unexpected. Costumes are very attractive and the production values ​​are high. In short, anyone looking for a quality foreign film, with a compelling story and a fantastic setting, would find this film a wonderful choice for a relaxed evening. If you have someone's hand to hold during the view, so much the better.
