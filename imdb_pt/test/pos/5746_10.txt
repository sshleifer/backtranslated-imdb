A young man discovers that life is precious after he is badly injured in a street accident. His father is a pastor who changes his view of life and spends the entire movie sharing his new love for others in this shocking and reassuring movie. The theme is built around street car racing and is an excellent movie for people who are in it and movies like 'the fast and the furious'. It shows how people can overcome their problems and gives the overall message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
