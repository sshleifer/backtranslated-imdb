Frank Horrigan (Clint Eastwood) is a secret service agent plagued by guilt for the assassination of President John F. Kennedy while he was on duty. Thirty years later, the current president is entering a reelection campaign, but is receiving death threats; and Horrigan was called in to assist in what should be a routine research operation. John Malkovich plays the professional assassin and master of disguise who accompanies the president, and using the past he begins to torture Horrigan in a psychological duel of cat and mouse. <br /> Malkovich, Eastwood and Rene Russo all give wonderful performances in this top thriller. The direction is excellent and the whole image is loaded with tension and intrigue everywhere. A must see for suspense fans 8/10
