The MS 08 team presents two hopeful romanticists from different sides of the conflict. Aina, the official Zeon, and Shiro, the new pilot of the Federal Forces, find themselves in a battle in space, throughout the series of 12 episodes (and a "movie") the two debate their love for each other as they try deal with the war that surrounds them. It features a Romeo and Juliet novel and unbeatable animation. By far one of the best to hit American Shores. Dress up!
