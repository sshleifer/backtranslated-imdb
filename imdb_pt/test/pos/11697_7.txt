Crackerjack is another classic Australian movie. Like so many Australian films like The Castle, The Dish and Sunday Too Farway, it goes somewhere that has not been extensively explored in the movies before, this time it's the game of Lawn Bowls and bowling clubs. Crackerjack is a sports movie with a much slower pace than many you will find, such as Remember the Titans or Million Dollar Baby, but the characters involved are athletes in their own right. This film is a show case of a large area of ​​Australian culture and features a sport that is popular and rising in popularity in Australia. Mick Molloy presents a classic and unforgettable character. It really needs to be seen.
