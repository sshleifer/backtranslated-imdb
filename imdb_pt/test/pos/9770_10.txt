This mini-series is an icon of the Australian spirit. While there may be what may be considered glaring inconsistencies, the film portrays a unique Australian spirit and one that should be valued. If anything, this mini-series demonstrates the Australian sense of humor. The ability to laugh at the extremely ridiculous. Our will to laugh even in the most terrible situations. While a large part of this series focuses on friendship and how humor can be used as a means of survival, it also has elements of drama that evoke real emotions. The main actors that appear are absolutely sensational and very convincing in their different roles. Capturing the essence of your characters perfectly. This mini-series should be mandatory for all Australians.
