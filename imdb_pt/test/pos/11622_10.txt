I was very moved by the young life experiences of a man who has risen so high in the academic world. A difficult life, surrounded by the love of a close family and an extended family of co-workers, has created a person capable of succeeding in the world. For the most part, Hispanic culture is shown as I have always remarked and admired - hardworking, optimistic, and truly family oriented. The points of religious superstition were quite authentic for the Catholic church. Undoubtedly, the actress who played the mother deserves an Oscar. His prayers for his missing son brought me to tears. I will recommend this incredibly thoughtful film to my friends and family.
