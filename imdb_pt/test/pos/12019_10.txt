Richard Norton really illuminates the screen in this martial arts masterpiece based in Portland, Oregon. Norton, an Australian heartthrob, plays the evil Mr. Milverstead, who successfully runs an import / export business, smuggling arms and participating in the female meat trade. Usually women are uprooted from their favorite dance club with the help of a squad of henchmen the best known of whom is Bolo Yeung, playing the role of Ice. The problem comes from Milverstead when a new police officer in town, John Kim (Britton Lee), is avenging the murder of his dead partner at the hands of the Milverstead organization. If you have time to see just one martial arts movie this year, do not miss this classic.
