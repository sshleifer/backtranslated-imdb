This film is an absolute kinematic genius. It has a well-assembled cast that gives an almost magical performance. The effects are nothing short of impressive and the story will keep you hanging in the chair right in the middle of the movie. Jack Long plays the role of white abbot exceptionally well, it provides an immensely exciting portrait of absolute evil. Whether you are a kung fu fan or just an action movie enthusiast, this movie is an excellent choice for anyone who is lucky enough to find a copy. For all the great kung fu fans, this film provides a compelling insight into the shaolin world. This movie is definitely 10/10 quality and should be considered as one of the greatest Eastern movies of all time.
