In this blaze of emotion, we are introduced to several high school youths during a busy day at school. They all struggle with belonging by meeting and keeping their secrets painful to be revealed. It has the feel of a maturing documentary focusing on the inner landscape of the students and their way of dealing with the challenges of everyday life. It is with a powerful openness and develops as a requiem.Thalluri hymn moves the camera with great skill and slowly invites us to the minds and hearts of young people. A fictional reconstruction that enlightens us all and reminds us of the fragility of youth. One of the most important films of today. every teenager should have the opportunity to see this piece of film on the go.
