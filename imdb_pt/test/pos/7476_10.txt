How 'Thursday' managed to avoid attention remains a mystery. A potent mix of comedy and crime, this one rises where Tarantino plays the Hollywood formula. The risks are not always worth it: a character in a sequence becomes inadequately silly and falls face-first. In the lead role, Thomas Jane performs a wonderful and complex performance, and two brief appearances by Mickey Rourke suggest the high potential of this misused actor. Here's a director who should keep an eye out.
