If you're a Pauly Shore fan, you'll laugh at your butt. If not, this is a silly mess wasting a pretty good talent. A beautiful coed (Carla Gugino) from South Dakota invites her California college dorm advisor (Shore) home to share Thanksgiving. Notable members of the cast: Lane Smith, Cindy Pickett, Mason Adams and the beautiful Tiffani-Amber Thiessen. See where you tread.
