A young man falls in love with a princess, but then has to go to battle to save his father's kingdom. While he was away, he accidentally kills an enchanted animal that brings a curse on him. He becomes a beast and begins to kill even his own comrades. When no one returns to the realm of battle, the king causes the land of battle to be cursed and forbids anyone to go there. One day a rebel who wishes to marry the princess decides that it is time to venture into the accursed land to claim it for the king and the king agrees, when they reach the land the king is captured by the beast and the rebel returns home to lie to the kingdom that the king was captured and killed. He takes the throne and prepares to marry the princess, but the night before his wedding, the princess escapes to earth to go and fight the beast. It is only when she comes to the cursed land that she begins to realize that her father is still alive and that the beast can not even be that bad. Unfortunately, her findings led her to pay the final price on her revelation.
