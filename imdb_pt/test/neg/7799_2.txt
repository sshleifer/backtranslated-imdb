Surrender Cinema is known for its extremely erotic and almost explicit sci-fi films. While they generally do very well in these (Femalien 1 & 2, Virtual Encounters), this particular entry seems to be just a replay of the old tape. Any number of girls - some recognizable and some not - are in this movie at all stages of nudity. There are also several clips from other movies that are excellent unless you have seen these other movies. There are a lot of solo nudity scenes doing a lot of things - some playing basketball, some talking, some still playing with themselves. The only thing of value on this tape is the last scene - a short but erotic girl-girl scene with a very enthusiastic and pleasant Sandy Wasko and a more moderate Tammie Hainum. Not highly recommended.
