MELTDOWN is very interesting SCI-FI. No big budget, very few special effects; but decent acting and a plot of global doom is enough to sustain the vision. An asteroid grabs the atmosphere and places the Earth in an orbit closer to the sun. Global warming quickly becomes unbearable. A determined LAPD (Casper Van Dien) police officer does everything to save the world from a certain annihilation, as rising temps are devastating. The pressure is on saving mankind from this solar catastrophe; as well as protect his daughter, ex-wife nurse and girlfriend TV reporter. The cast includes: Stefanie Von Pfetten, Venus Terzo, Amanda Crew and Vincent Gale.
