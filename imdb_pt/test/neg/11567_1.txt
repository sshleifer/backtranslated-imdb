What a desperate movie. Dress the actors in fur rags, put them in a suburban wasteland, put moving cameras and expect the best. One can only imagine and the thanks that the cast gave when their characters were killed by sockpuppets, thus saving more humiliation in this monotonous party. This rivals the Monster as the best cure for insomnia ever made. Oh God - how can I fill 10 lines explaining how overwhelmingly everyone gets bored in this movie? Grumpy plastic robot that annoys everyone on and off the screen, giant spider reduced to a single giant hairy leg pulled by ropes, actors desperately trying not to look at the camera while grunting dialogues ...
