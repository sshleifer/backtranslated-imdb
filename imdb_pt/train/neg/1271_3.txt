This movie could be 15 minutes long, were it not for all the fights between father and son. Very predictable. Both male "stars" need a good slap in the face! Would you like some cheese with that "lament"? Two laughs ... and a headache. I can understand why the mother left her husband after 47 years ... I do not know how she lasted so long! The first 5 minutes made me want to turn off the movie, wishing I had never paid the $ 3.99 to watch it! The film did not flow well and was painfully long. I kept watching my watch waiting for time to fly faster ... It did not happen. The script had so much repetition that it had to be easy for the writer to fill the space. On a positive note ... the scenery was beautiful, being my favorite season. The car, the 40 Ford was also very good. This film gets a rating D approaching an F
