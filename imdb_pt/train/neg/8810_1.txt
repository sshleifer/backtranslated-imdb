This movie is one of those that I regret having invested 90 minutes of my life that I will never go back. The premise is really interesting - essentially it's a zombie movie from the perspective of the undead (we will not split the hair if they Unfortunately, they fail to present a convincing story within that framework. The almost unbearable monotony of the lives of the central characters may increase the realism of the film, but it sucks up the whole value of entertainment. If they had made a little more effort to keep the viewer involved, it would have been far more likely that they would take the social commentary home.
