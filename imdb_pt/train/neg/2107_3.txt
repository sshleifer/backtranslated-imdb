Bela Lugosi plays Dr. Lorenz who loves both his wife and will do anything to keep her young. This movie begins with a wedding while the bride is about to make the vows she suddenly faints. She is pronounced dead and taken away by undertakers. The problem is that these are not real funeral undertakers, but rather body robbers. A wave of bridal deaths at the altar and her disappearing body confuses the police. Enter reporter Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She traces Dr. Lorenz, but he also decides to use his youth to keep his young wife as well.
