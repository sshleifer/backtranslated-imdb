This British psychological thriller is known in the US as CLOSURE. Exploration of Gillian Anderson, from X-Files, who plays an attractive middle-aged businesswoman named Alice. She is due to attend a business party and invites Adam (Danny Dyer), who has just installed a security system for her, to be her escort. On the way home, rushing through the woods in a narrow lane, Alice's car collides with a deer. After pulling the injured animal from the road, the couple is wildly attacked by a band of drunken thugs. Adam is beaten in a pulp; Alice is raped by gangs and both are emotionally and physically devastated by the relentless onslaught. When the identities of her attackers are discovered, Alice and Adam set out to exact revenge ... brutal revenge. The couple sometimes find themselves at odds over how to deal with relentless attackers. His final decision is to avenge without mercy. That there is no mistake, the return is hell. Also in the cast: Anthony Calf, Ralph Brown, Francesca Fowler and Antony Byrne. Brutal violence, disturbing images, nudity and graphic rape.
