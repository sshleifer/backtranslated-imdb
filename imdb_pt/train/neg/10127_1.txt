This show made me feel physically sick and totally distant from British society as a whole. It was programs like this and Blue Peter that pretended there were no class divisions in Britain. They always say things like; "Go to your loft and you may find this .." or "Go to your yard tonight and ..." - what about us, working class kids who have never had a loft, and a "back garden" that was no more than 1 square meter of balcony on the 14th floor of a council block? Public broadcasting - yes right! And on top of that, it was terribly depressing to see those idiot, middle-class kids on your back, messing with pieces of old plastic having fun, doing me a favor and why not.
