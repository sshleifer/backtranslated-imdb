I was hoping to love this movie - film noir, serial killer, dark irony. I was puzzled by the many choices the characters made ("Hey, I know they're scary, but let's travel for a cross country trip too!"), I found the glacial pace, and the emphasis on moody lighting to take the place of original thought by the director and director of photography. Thinking about it now, this would be a much better movie if someone had just run the script through the common sense-o-meter (Model 1992) before starting filming ...
