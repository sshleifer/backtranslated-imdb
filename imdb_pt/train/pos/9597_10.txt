This is a must-see documentary for those who fear that modern youth has lost the taste for real-life adventure and their sense of morality. Darius Goes West is an incredible roller coaster of a story. We live the life of Darius and the crew as they embark on the journey of their lives. Darius has Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy, a disease that affects every muscle in the body. He is confined to a wheelchair and needs attention 24 hours a day. So how could this crew of young friends be able to take you on a 6,000 mile round trip to the west coast and vice versa? Watch the movie and experience the ups and downs of this great adventure - laugh and cry with the crew as you tackle unimaginable challenges along the way and enjoy the ultimate triumph when you return three weeks later in your hometown for a sweeping reception and some great surprises!
