Rachel Griffiths writes and directs this award-winning short film. A heartwarming story about dealing with pain and cherishing the memory of those we love and lose. Although only 15 minutes long, Griffiths manages to capture so much emotion and truth in the film in the short time. Bud Tingwell makes a moving performance as Will, a widower who struggles to deal with his wife's death. Will is confronted by the harsh reality of loneliness and helplessness as he takes care of Ruth's pet cow, Tulip. The film shows the mourning and responsibility that we feel for those we love and lose. Good cinematography, great direction and superbly acted. This will bring tears to all those who have lost a loved one and survived.
