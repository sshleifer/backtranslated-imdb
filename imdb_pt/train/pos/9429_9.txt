This film is a classic in every sense of the word. It is very entertaining and also very disturbing. The acting in this film is well done. The story itself is believable, full of suspense and well thought out. The character's development is also done well, the audience can clearly see how each of the characters is emotionally tested through this film. The villains in this movie are very menacing, from the first moment the public sees them, they can say they are up to something. This film shows how a human being, when taken out of civilization and placed in the middle of the woods, facing a situation of life and death, can slowly become almost as wild and wild as the animals that inhabit it. Not everyone will like this movie, there is a lot of disturbing content that can leave some viewers uncomfortable. It is definitely worth seeing. This movie is a work of art.
