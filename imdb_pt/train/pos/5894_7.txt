A brother's promise is a wonderful family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, an Olympic speed skating champion. The film portrays the life of this athlete from a young age to adulthood. The love and support of family members is evident everywhere. As Dan and the rest of his family deal with victories and defeats are a life lesson for all of us. The commitment and determination of the coach of Dan and his teammates, shows what it takes to make a real team. How Dan and his family deal with a devastating illness of a loved one is portrayed without undoing feelings or sugarcoating. Family faith is shown in basic terms and is obviously an important part of their lives. This is a powerful family movie that can be meaningful to a person of any age.
