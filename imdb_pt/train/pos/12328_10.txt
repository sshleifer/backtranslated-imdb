"Bread" very sharply snaps the conventions of horror films in general and "Night of the Living Dead" in specific and is constantly inventive. The production values ​​are a bit rough at times (it's a student film, after all), but it never misses its goal of entertaining. Hey - George Romero liked it enough to include it in the remastered videotape "Dead", laserdisc and DVD ... that should tell you something.
