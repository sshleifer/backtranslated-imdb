`Europa '(or, as it is also known,` Zentropa') is one of the most visually stunning films I have ever seen. The mixture of shades of gray and color photography is almost perfect ... a real feast for the eyes. The image was a contender for a 1991 Golden Palm in Canners. The award went to Barton Fink (of the Coen brothers); a film stylistically very similar to Zentropa. Here's a class exercise: Rent the two movies and be a judge for yourself.
