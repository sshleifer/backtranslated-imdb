Based on a real mining disaster, this old German talkie (with English subtitles) remains one of the most effective docu-dramas ever filmed. With many non-professional actors, "Kameradschaft" gives a frightening vision of the friendship that unites the mine workers, regardless of which side of the Franco-German border they may be. A fatal accident brings out the best in all, nullifying the orders of any superior. A fellow miner in need will receive the help of his comrades, even under threat of great loss, including life. <br /> <br /> This film recalls the selfless heroism shown by the NYFD after the September 11 attacks. . Leaving aside any formal rules and regulations, these uniformed men and women knew only one cause: saving lives and finding their fellow combatants. - More than 70 years later, "Kameradschaft" still has the strong and timeless message: A friend in need is a friend in action.
