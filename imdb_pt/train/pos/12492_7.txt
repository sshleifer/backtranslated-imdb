After the brutal murder of his daughter, Julie and Allen escape from the city to find solace and cry in a lonely cabin on a remote mountain. Allen's intentions are good, he wants his wife to get out of her depression by picking up her picture. Julie stumbles into an old prison and sees the perfect scary, decadent setting for her photograph. But when the photos are developed they are full of dead people and Allen quickly discovers the tragic suicide story on his new mountain. "Dark Remains" is a fairly decent indie horror film. It offers some genuine scares and lots of tension. The acting is pretty good and the cinematography is great.
