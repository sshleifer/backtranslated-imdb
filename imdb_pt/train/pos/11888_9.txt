It was very touching. As an expatriate French, I particularly enjoyed this movie "TV", which I think deserves a wider distribution. The performance was good, and the bonds between each member of this French rural family seemed so strong and natural. Undoubtedly a great film about the concept of family and inclusion in the face of homosexuality and AIDS. One of the strongest aspects of the film is this privileged relationship between the eldest son (Leo), who contracted HIV, and the youngest son (Marcel), whom the rest of the family tries to hide. The two characters explore each other progressively as time runs out for Leo, who is not willing to spend the rest of his life surviving under the drugs ...
