"Panic" is a captivating and obscure film about a reconciliation between passion and a younger uninhibited hairdresser, his love for his wife and son, his duty to his employer / father with his own identity. Although the film has a nebulous purpose and an ambiguous ending, it is a superb production in almost every aspect. The clarity and sincerity of the characters in such an improbable story can both fascinate and annoy the audience.
