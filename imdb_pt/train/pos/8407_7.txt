Sammy Johns' 1975 hit album, "Chevy Van," is heard repeatedly on the soundtrack (this movie was reissued under the title CHEVY VAN), although the title of the film is a Dodge. Danny DeVito only makes six minutes of screen appearances, but countless VHS reissues falsely credit him as the star of the film. The film is a comparatively sexist tale of morality - will Bobby find sexual fulfillment through a night that his personalized van makes easier, or should he wait until Tina, the girl of his dreams, gives him the time of day? Yet it is representative of the prevailing carnal dream of American high school men of the day, and it is only on this basis that the VAN, almost in spite of itself, has become an artifact of the period that must be referred to in any honest retrospective of the cinema popular American period ...
