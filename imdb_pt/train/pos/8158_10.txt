"Fear of a black hat" is all that (much weaker) "CB-4" should have been. Rusty Cundieff's satirical eye is relentless as it bends, renders, and mutilates every aspect of hip-hop trends and culture. Does "FoaBH" look like Spinal Tap? Yes a little. Is it derived from Spinal Tap? No, not really. The goal is more focused, the satire is more focused, and to be honest, it's funnier.
