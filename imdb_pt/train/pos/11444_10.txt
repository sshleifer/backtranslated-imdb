Go see this film for the stunning pictures of Andy Goldsworthy's sculptures and treat yourself to a breathtaking and relaxing experience. The song perfectly complements the footage, but never draws attention to itself. Some commentators have considered the excerpts from an interview with the artist as a weak point, but consider the following: Why do you expand this into a movie, can you read Andy's thoughts at length in your books, or attend one of your excellent lectures? This medium is much more suited to show the ephemeral nature of the artist's works, and is cleverly used in this regard.
