Twisted Desire (1996) was a TV movie starring Melissa Joan Hart. Melissa's character, Jennifer Stanton, a seventeen-year-old teen, seduces her current boyfriend Nick Ryan to murder her two parents. The film is based on the 1990 murders of 14-year-old parents, Jessica Wiseman. Jessica had her 17 year old boyfriend Douglas Christopher Thomas shoot and kill his parents! Thomas was executed in 2000! Jessica was released from prison when she turned 21. Evidence now suggests that it was Jessica who fired the fatal shot that killed her mother. Jessica is known to be residing somewhere in the state of Virginia.
