New York I Love You just as its predecessor (Paris Je T'Aime) is a composite of several stories that reflects the different types and aspects of love, but on the contrary, the pace is much faster and the stories much shorter. The film offers a unique view of New York City with its many different landscapes. New York, I Love You offers a first-class cast with great actors like Shia LaBeouf, Natalie Portman, Ian McKellen, Hayden Christensen, Chistina Rcci and Orlando Bloom, Ethan Hawke, James Caan and Robin Wright Penn among others and some excellent writers and directors like Brett Ratner and Anthony Mingella.
