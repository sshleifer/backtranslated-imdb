Based on Christy Brown's autobiographical novel, this captivating film tells the story of his life, being affected by cerebral palsy and being considered basically not a person by everyone, including his mother. Surprisingly, he teaches himself to draw and write using the foot, which is the only part of him that he can control. An incredible story of courage with a truly amazing and unforgettable Oscar winning performance by Daniel Day Lewis. 9 out of 10
