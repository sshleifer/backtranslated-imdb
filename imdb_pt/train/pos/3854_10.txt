When it comes to Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think of "Wild Instinct" and some maybe in "Showgirls." But Verhoeven did his best erotic thriller years before these two films: "De Vierde Man". This film is mesmerizing and hallucinatory - and above all the story is absolutely plausible, which makes the whole experience even more intense. The performances of Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thom Hoffman are exceptional, and the direction of Verhoeven does the rest. "De Vierde Man" makes even the "Wild Instinct" seem pretty tame ... It can not get much better than that, a true classic of erotic nightmare cinema. 10 out of 10, at least ...
