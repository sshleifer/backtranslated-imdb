The Invaders are a fast science fiction thriller that crosses the Cross and Young Young line. BEN PLAYS RENN, A TRAVELER OF ANOTHER GALAXY TRYING TO FIND ANNIE (REPRODUCTED BY SEAN) THAT IS PHYSICALLY IDEAL FOR HAVING HER SON. THIS SON, IF PERMITTED TO BORN AND RETURN TO HIS PLANET, MAY BE THE ONLY OPPORTUNITY FOR HIS RACE TO SURVIVE. The Enemy, a foreigner who destroyed Renn's people, also followed Renn through the stars to stop this birth. CRUZ AGAIN SHOWS GREAT SCALE OF BASIC BEGINNINGS AS THE ALIEN ENTERS A LOCAL BAR AND ORDERS YOUR FIRST COCKTAIL OF EARTH, FOR HIS RACE WITH THE ENEMY AND THE DRAMA OF IF YOU CAN KEEP ANNIE AND THE CHILD IS LOADING ALIVE.
