As many people know, Mexican cinema was very poor after the so-called Golden Age of Mexican Cinema, fortunately during the late 1990s and early 21st century, great films like Herod's Law, Baja California, Amores Perros, Y Your Mother Also, of course, the Colonel Does not Have Who Scribe him, they appeared. El Coronel ... is a wonderful film that recounts the classic story of Gabriel García Márquez, eliminating the elements of magical realism and replacing them with the crude reality lived in Mexico, not only by people like the Colonel, who wait for their pensions, but for more than half of the Mexican population, who live in complete poverty. The characters in the film, satiriously represent classic characters found in Mexican society, such as the nationalist colonel, the cold and even ambitious priest, the hypocrite, but at the same time loyal comrade, tolerant and patient wife, hidden homosexual, etc. film, is a must-see if you want to know more about Mexican society and especially if you want to watch a gorgeous movie by one of the best directors in Mexico
