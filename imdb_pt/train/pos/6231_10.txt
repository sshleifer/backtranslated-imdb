This is just a great movie, fun and charming. It captures the true essence of the decade and the people, and tells a beautiful love story of two sisters with two sailors. Although this film may only be in black and white, it definitely does not count against this nowadays. The main basic objective of the film is timeless. This film features great performances, beautiful numbers of music and dance, as well as great design work and film filming. Follow the Fleet is also comical, there are funny moments, moments that will make you laugh, but other times when the acting gets you so involved in the plot. It's amazing how this movie can be defined in a certain decade, as this can affect the current ones. If you want to see something great, check it out.
