Virgil Manoven is an old man who lives alone in his remote farmhouse. Facing his beloved cat one morning in the forest around his estate, Manoven glimpses what appears to be the murder of a child in the middle of the forest. He reports the crime. to the police, but there is no body to be found. Trained by disturbing visions, he investigates further and is eventually guided to a frightening orphanage where events take a supernatural course ?? "Soft for Digging" is a fantastic experimental horror with lots of scary atmosphere. This minimalist movie is almost completely devoid of dialogue. Some scenes are genuinely terrifying and the acting is excellent.
