Dressed to Kill (1980) is a mysterious horror film by Brian De Palma and really works. The atmosphere is right there. The atmosphere that leaves you scared. And that's not what a horror movie should do. All actors are in the right places.Michael Caine is perfect as Dr. Robert Elliott, the psychiatrist with a little secret.Angie Dickinson as Kate Miller, the sexually frustrated mature woman is great.Keith Gordon as her son Peter is brilliant.Nancy Allen as Liz Blake, the program girl, is fantastic. Dennis Franz does his typical detective role. Your detective Marino is one of the most colorful in this movie. There are many scary scenes in this movie. The elevator scene is one of them. comparisons between this and Alfred Hitchcock's Psycho (1960). There are some similarities between these two films. Both of these movies can cause some sleepless nights.
