Angela Lansbury plays Eglantine, a middle-aged woman in war-torn England during the First World War. She studies witchcraft in the mail, and is learning it secretly in her house, she is doing well, until three children who were separated from their parents from the last air raid were sent to stay in the huge house of Angela Lansbury. She is not happy to have them, because of her secret. They just want to go home. The older boy is very difficult to understand, he is a brat. The other two a boy and a girl are just curious about their new place to stay and the lady who is their hostess. Children discover their secret and that's where the fun begins. Fun and more of that. Family fun. Laughter, games, adventure, something for everyone. The special effects are very good. This is a musical that the songs are as captivating to sing later as any other Disney movie. Add this to your Family Video Library. You will not be disappointed.
