The teenager Eddie spends his life being bullied and humiliated because of his obsession with heavy metal music. One day he discovers that his hero Sammi Curr died, allegedly burned by the establishment that wanted to end his music. But Eddie has his latest record, never released, and when he plays, he starts getting messages telling him how to deal with his tormentors. Soon Sammi revealed that he intends to return to life at the local Halloween party to take revenge on the city that once mocked him. <br /> <br /> Full of humor and inner jokes, this is a really fun movie. Sammi himself is an original horror movie villain, touches on the obsessions of the "rock mores" of the 80's. Worth watching.
