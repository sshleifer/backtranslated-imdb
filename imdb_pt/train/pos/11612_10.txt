Most of them were planning to go back to their homeland, but few managed to do so. Also depicted as touching is the fate of Japanese women, some with fifteen or sixteen years of age, who were sent as brides promised to men they knew only by means of photographs that, at the age of ten or fifteen, were outdated or were from some other man younger. .
