An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a sloth and a mammoth, who saved a baby, who was close to being killed by a group of tigers during the ice age. The moral of the movie shows that good behavior with others can bring benefits in the end. One of the group's tigers was ordered to finally capture the baby, which was barely saved by his mother when the tigers attacked his community. The baby was then rescued by laziness and the mammoth, but the tiger joined them in order to finally take the baby. They went through very troublesome paths with considerable danger, and at one time the tiger fell and was saved by the mammoth. In the end, the group of tigers tried to capture the baby, but the mammoth helped incredibly by his fellow tiger was able to overcome this attack and give the baby back to the father and the community to which he belongs.
