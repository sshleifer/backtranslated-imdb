Antonio Margheriti's "Danza Macabra" / "Castle of Blood" is a mysterious atmospheric chiller that succeeds on all fronts. It looks absolutely gorgeous in black and white and has a wonderfully scary gothic vibe. Alan Foster is an English journalist chasing an interview with the visit of American horror writer Edgar Allan Poe.Poe bet Foster that he can not spend a night in the abandoned mansion of Poe's friend Thomas Blackwood. Accepting the bet, Foster is locked in the mansion and the horror begins! The film is extremely The sequence of the crypt is really sinister and the tension is almost unbearable. Barbara Steele looks incredibly beautiful as the sinister specter Elisabeth Blackwood. "Castle of Blood" is easily one of the best Italian horror films made in the early 60's. A masterpiece!
