Russ and Valerie are arguing about starting a family. The couple lives in an elegant apartment and manages an auction company that deals with valuable collections. At the same time, a dedicated adoption agency owner takes a vacation and leaves the orphanage under the responsibility of his father (Leslie Nielsen). Father Harry is in the rental business and he has the brilliant idea to "rent" some of the children from the orphanage to couples like Russ and Valerie. Harry, who becomes aware of the couple's dilemma, offers a family of siblings for a period of 10 days! Brandon, Kyle and Molly move into the apartment with their foster parents, with fun consequences, as the new caregivers are inexperienced with children. But where is the possibility of a happy ending? This is a beloved family movie. The actors, including Nielsen as the wheel salesman and Christopher Lloyd as the kind of apartment porter, are all wonderful. The script is fast and fun and the overall production figures are quite high. Yes, if life could be like this! Orphaned children everywhere deserve a chance to prove they are kind and can give so much joy to parents who are considering adoption. If you want to show a movie for your family that is rooted in good values ​​but is also highly entertaining, find this movie. It is guaranteed that everyone laughs, even when their hearts are melting.
