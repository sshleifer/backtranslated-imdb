This film is not only a great film for basic cinematography (script, acting, setting, etc.) but also for its realism. This movie could take place in any farm or rural setting. It makes no difference if the movie happens in Louisiana or if it happens in Kansas. The story and the messages included will remain the same. This film shows the values ​​and connections of the family to an older audience and, at the same time, shows young behavior for younger viewers. Everyone who watches this goes away with something that touched them personally, I know I did. The end comes very close to home so I do not start crying every time I watch. The ending emphasizes the importance of agricultural security, and everyone who has ever worked on a farm needs to see this film.
