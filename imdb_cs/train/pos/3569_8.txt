Creative use of modern and mystical features: 1956 Cadillac convertible for the transport of evil stepmother Kathleen Turner (John Waters "Serial Mom") and 2 twisted sisters; Queen Mab as godfather; David Warner (evil in "Time Bandits") in a red mantle in court; Cinderella (she's a babe) squeezes coal into a greedy kiln; Cinderella and her charming prince both look (and behave like) rock stars. Location on the island of Man.
