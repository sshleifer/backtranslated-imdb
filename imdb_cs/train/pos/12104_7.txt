Well done old and very dark home horror. A good setting that includes the character Poe, himself, who mentions the story of a London pub. Even though he is the one who dared to visit the house on a specific night that runs from room to room, whether he is looking or avoiding people, it is still the most enjoyable. Besides, we have the beautiful and mysterious Barbara Steele. There is some wooden dialogue and some inexplicable pieces and bobs, but it is a super scary atmosphere that is kept in the whole, and super music scores that keep this moving carefully. The DVD comes from the US and has several accessories
