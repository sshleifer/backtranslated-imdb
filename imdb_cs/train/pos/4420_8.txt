In two and a half hours, it is sometimes a slowly moving thoughtful film interrupted by huge swords. The battle between darkness and light is signaled by the constant motif of the blazing sun and is surprisingly demonstrated by a three-way battle between "demons," bandits and soldiers in the woods during the eclipse. Be Prepared: After a wonderful fight with the sword under a lightning filled sky, the end of this picture will have a scratching head in embarrassment.
