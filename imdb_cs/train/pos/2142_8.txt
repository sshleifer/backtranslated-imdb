This is a very real and entertaining film about a Japanese man who has a half-life crisis. In Japan, the ballroom is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed when he meets a beautiful young girl he sees in a dance studio window, he suddenly finds himself in dance classes. No one is surprised when he likes him than he does. But he has to keep his secret pleasure from his coworkers and his family. When the truth comes, it's quite funny.
