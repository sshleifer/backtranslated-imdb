An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a lady and a mammoth that saved a child who was killed by a group of tigers during the Ice Age. The morale of the film shows that good behavior with others can bring benefits in the end. One of the tigers in the group was ordered to finally capture the child his mother barely rescued when the tigers attacked her community. The child was rescued by lazy and mammoth, but the tiger joined them in order to finally take the baby. They traveled through very disturbing roads with great danger, and immediately the tiger fell and saved the mammoth. In the end, a group of tigers tried to capture a child, but a mammoth who incredibly helped his tiger colleague managed to overcome this attack and return the child back to his father and the community he belongs to.
