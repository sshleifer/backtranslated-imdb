There is no novel or any other side plot to this film, it's action and intrigue all the way, which is a real male kung fu movie. An aging master sends his last pupil, Yan Tieh, to stop his five former pupils who are styles, representing five poisonous centipedes, a snake, a scorpion, a lizard, and a toad. Despite the word "Venom" in the title, none of these pupils use poisons to kill their opponents. Yan Tieh, by his teacher, said he was not in agreement for the five former pupils, he must find one with whom he can create an alliance to defeat four more. As Yan Tieh and the others are, it is an intrigue of the story, with good action of kung-fu spread throughout the story. Recognized as a cult classic, this film has already taken the kung fu action movies. It is known that other films refer to the five styles shown in this story. It's not an artistic masterpiece, with usual bad dubbing and horror, but the film is one of the best movies. its kind because it focused on all the ingredients of kung-fu action film of its time and gives them an extra concentrated dose. One movie you have to watch if you are a kung-fu movie fan.
