"Darius Goes West" is a touching story of brave teens who cope with Duchenne's muscular dystrophy and his personal efforts to see the Pacific Ocean. He will receive help and encouragement from a group of young men who love him and care for him when you are going this way. The story has a natural drama and a fair display of the commitment of young people to help one of their own affliction with this incurable disease. Anyone who thinks that young people are self-sufficient and narcissistic will find this film to change this stereotype. It is the power of young people and their involvement in Darius' situation, which is very urgent in this document.
