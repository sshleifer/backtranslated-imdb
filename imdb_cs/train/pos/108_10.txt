'Europa' (or, as it is also known, 'Zentropa') is one of the most visually stunning films I've ever seen. The blend of grayscale and color photographs is almost seamless ... a real holiday for the eyes. The film was a candidate for the Golden Palm in Cannes in 1991. The award was won by Barton Fink (Coen Brothers); a style very similar to Zentro. Here is a classroom exercise: renting both films and a judge for yourself.
