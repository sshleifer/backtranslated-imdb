In our life we ​​first organize stones (Piedras in Spanish) such as career, family, friendship and love. In this way, we find these spaces to match the smaller stones, our small needs. If you act inversely, you will not have enough room for larger stones. The five protagonists of this film are women who could not organize large "stones" in their lives. Ramon Salazar, a Spanish film director, defines his first character Stones in this way. The film features the parallel, contradictory trajectory of five women: Anita (Monica Cervera, 1975-), Isabel (Angela Molina, 1955-), Adela (Antonia San Juan, 1961-), Leire (Najwa Nimri, 1954 -). Everyone is trying to remove stones that are urgently appearing on their way or the worst that are in their shoes. There are five Cinderella who are looking for Prince Charming and a new chance in life. The best story of these five Cinderella is Anita (Monica Cervera), also starring "20 cm", "Busco", "Crimen Ferpecto", "Entre Vivir y Sonar", "Hongos" and "Octavia".
