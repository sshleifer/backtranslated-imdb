"Fido" is to be praised for being a tired genre, a zombie, and turning it into the most original movie experience. At the beginning of the 1950s, the atmosphere was amazing, it worked amazingly and the whole production showed a lot of careful planning. Suddenly, the viewer is immersed in the world of beautiful classic cars, Eisenhower era dresses, artistic decoration furniture, and zombie servants. It would be very easy to refuse "Fido" as a cartoon-like "Tank Girl," but these two films are very different. "Fido has a structure, a script that tells the story and acting that is superior. Do not make a mistake, it's a brave black comedy that succeeds where so many others have failed.
