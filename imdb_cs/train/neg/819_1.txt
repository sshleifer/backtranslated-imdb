The "Domino Principle" is undoubtedly one of the worst thrillers ever to happen. No more sense can be made of intricate conspiracy and half way through, you'll want to throw your hands into frustration and shout "I give up !!!" As Gene Hackman and director Stanley Kramer have sometimes got involved in this mess, they have to be summed up by their paychecks. I hope they spent their money well.
