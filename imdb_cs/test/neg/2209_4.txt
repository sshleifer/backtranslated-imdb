MELTDOWN is quite interesting SCI-FI. No big budget, very few special effects; but decent behavior and the story of global destiny are enough to be seen. The asteroid creeps the atmosphere and throws Earth into orbit closer to the sun. Global warming is rapidly becoming unbearable. The determined LAPD (Casper Van Dien) is all in order to save the world from some destruction because the rising tempo is devastating. The pressure is to save humanity from this solar catastrophe; As well as protecting his daughter, sister ex-wife and television reporter girlfriend. The cast includes: Stefanie Von Pfetten, Venus Terzo, Amanda's crew and Vincent Gale.
