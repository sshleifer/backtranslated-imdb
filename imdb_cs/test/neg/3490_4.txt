I remember watching this movie when I was a kid. I remember that I was terribly frightened and stayed with me all these years. I spent a few years trying to find it online ... I didn't remember the name, just the storyline. After searching and searching, I came across a VHS that was sold on E-Bay. I was excited and when it finally arrived I got stuck in the VCR and couldn't help but feel a little nostalgic. Needless to say, I was a little disappointed. This wasn't a movie I remembered as a child. Sometimes it was boring, and Beryl Reid was constantly angry. Both Reid and Flora Robson's performances were good overall, but the movie wasn't scary. I think every movie is worth it to create your own opinion, but sometimes well ......
