A young man discovers that life is rare after being seriously injured in a street traffic accident. His father is a pastor who changes his view of life and spends the whole film sharing his new love for others in this shocking and gratifying film. The theme is based on street car races and it is an excellent movie for people who are into it and movies like 'fast and furious'. This shows how people can overcome their problems and give a general message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
