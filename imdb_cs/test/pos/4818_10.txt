Riding high on the success of "Rebel Without a Cause", came tidal wave 'teens' movies. It's probably one of the best. Very young McArthur stands out as a not too troubled teenager. The story focuses more on delinquency perception than any traumatic occurrence. Supporting the cast is unforgettable, Frankenheimer drives like an old pro. Just the story of a young man who discovers that others are taking his actions too seriously.
