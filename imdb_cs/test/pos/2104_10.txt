This film is an absolute film genius. It has well brought cast that give almost magical performance. The effects are nothing but stunning and the story will keep you in a chair right in the movie. Jack Long plays a part of Abbot White exceptionally well, providing an immensely exciting portrayal of absolute evil. If your fan is a kung fu or just an action movie enthusiast, this movie is an excellent choice for anyone who is lucky enough to find a copy. For all fans of big kung fu, this film provides a convincing insight into the world of shaolin. This movie is definitely 10/10 quality and should be considered one of the greatest Eastern movies of all time.
