Frank Horrigan (Clint Eastwood) is a secret service agent plagued by the murder of President John F. Kennedy while he was on duty. Thirty years later, the current president enters the re-elected campaign, but threatens to die; and Horrigan was called to help what should be a routine research operation. John Malkovich plays a professional killer and a disguise master who watches the president and, using the past, starts torturing Horrigan in a psychological duel of cats and mice. Malkovich, Eastwood and Rene Russo have a wonderful performance in this top notch thriller. The direction is excellent and the whole picture is loaded with tension and intrigue around the world. <br /> <br /> 8/10 is to be seen for thriller fans
