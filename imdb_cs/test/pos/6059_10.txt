In the fall of 1959, I was lucky to be on the island of Perris. DI showed one evening in the outdoor theater right in front of our barracks, Plt 162, B ​​Co, 1. Bn, 1. ITR. Although we weren't there long enough to think about seeing a movie, we could hear those who laughed. It's one of the many indelible memories of my thirteen weeks at PI. Sometime later I saw it in the theater. I am still convinced that it still remains the most realistic depiction of the experience of the late 1950s. No one did better than Jack Webb ...
