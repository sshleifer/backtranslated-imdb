This harsh drama based on a real incident concerns several ambitious African citizens who decide to leave their families temporarily by postponing them to an outgoing ship. They think that if they can travel successfully, they can improve their lives by making enough money in New York to send their families. Unfortunately, the ship they chose for them is a neglected Russian cargo ship that has already been heavily fined in the previous port for the dock. The Captain and First Officer are determined not to allow it to happen again because their jobs are on the line. A group of blacks begin their rough cruise in the hold and is finally discovered, forced out of hiding and murdered by a ruthless friend (Sean Pertwee's excellent performance). escape and are mercilessly persecuted by ships with their lives if they are caught. Overall, the riveting film, which will have a nail-biting viewer and crush teeth, wondered how such catastrophic events could take place in modern civilization.
