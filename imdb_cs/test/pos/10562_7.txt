London 1862, a young orphan named Susan Trinder (Sally Hawkins) grows among the petty thieves known as the Fingersmiths, under the direction of Mrs. Suckerby (Imelda Staunton). One evening, Richard "The Gentleman" Rivers (Rupert Evans) pays them a visit. Rivers have a sophisticated plan to deceive the rich heiress of Maud Lilly (Elaine Cassidy). Susan agrees to help reduce money and is quickly installed as a Muad maid. After his arrival, he finds out that Maud is basically a prisoner in his own home, as Uncle Chritopher (Charles Dance) controls every detail of his wife's life. As the plan begins to develop, Susan finds herself in an intimate relationship with the house lady. Adapted to Sarah Waters' novel.
