If you want an excellent overview of Byzantine history made in colorful fashion, it's for you. This documentary would also be excellent for educators who learn about Roman or medieval history. This document is divided into three parts, first dealing with the fall of the Western Roman Empire, the rise of Christianity and the beginning of the Byzantine Empire. The second video deals with Byzantine diplomacy and iconoclastic disputes. The third and final video explains the decline and fall of Byzantium. The series is shot in several countries and beautifully integrates Byzantine history into the reality of the modern world and shows the place of this civilization as part of human civilization in general. Take some time to watch.
