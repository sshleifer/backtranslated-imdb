The story of an obsessed lover (Shahrukh Khan) and his length to win his true love (Juhi Chawla), who is already married to her husband (Sunny Deol). The film is considered to be one of the best performances of Shahrukh Khan and won it from critics and audiences. Fear that your love may not be reciprocated, worry that you can lose the one you love, fear that your loved one may have a change of heart. In short, fear is a villain in every love story. But in 'Darr', fear is the ultimate expression of passion, obsession, and sacrifice. 'Darr' is the story of Rahul (Shahrukh Khan), whose love and obsession with Kiran (Juhi Chawla) frees him from all the fears of life and death. 'Darr' is the story of Sunil (Sunny Deol) whose enduring love and passion for Kiran gives him the courage to face the fear of death. Finally, 'Darr' is the story of Kiran who is caught between one. the love of man and the obsession of the other man. He is afraid of one and fear of another. One stands for love, the other for life. In this battle between love and life, love is the highest winner, because love always wins in life and death. Simply "Darr" is one of the best Indian movies ever made.
