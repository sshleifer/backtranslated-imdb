The most enjoyable pet movie by Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around a 23 year old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems in his company's boss seem to fail, Brian is also dating the daughter of a daughter named Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robotic dog named CHOMPS, modeled on his own dog Rascal. But CHOMPS is not usually a dog, it is as fast as a cheetah, it has x-ray vision, it can jump about 6 feet and it has a strength of 20 men. The new invention attracts its boss and his business is successful. But when company rivals learn about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to capture it. Can CHOMPS outsmart them before it's too late. This film is a classic for all ages.
