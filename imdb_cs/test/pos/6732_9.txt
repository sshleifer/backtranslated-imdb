This film is one of those that cannot be considered its outer. At first glance, the story seems to simply focus on the desire to have more money. But ... look the other way ... What do you see? Do you see that money is merely a metaphor for the ambiguous feelings that a human being has: Should I do the right thing or should I not? And what is the "right thing"? Le makes deeper analyzes ... - What does a small town on the border mean? - Does that sometimes we can go too close to the border to do something we thought we couldn't ... - What does the arid ground mean in this picture? - Does it mean a drought that sometimes controls our insulted heart ... - What does a character phrase mean (KRISTEN): "Now I belong to him"? - It means losing our free will through our unforeseen actions.
