Basil Rathbone and Nigel Bruce return as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson in this superior Holmes fairy tale fighting 3rd Reich and brilliant master Professor Moriarty. The film opens in Switzerland because Holmes is disguised as an "old bookseller". Must bring Dr. Tobela and Tobel Bomb Site to England before the Germans can kidnap Tobel. Holmes succeeds and the Germans recruit an evil professor. Moriarty manages to overcome the Scottish court and LeStrad "Dennis Hoey" kidnapped Tobel. The only trace left by Tobel is the list of "dancing men". Who breaks the hidden code of dancers, Holmes or Moriarty? Can Holmes prevent the bomb from falling into German hands and save England from the exact bombardment techniques developed by Tobel? Watch and enjoy.
