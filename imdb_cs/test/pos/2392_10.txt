I've been writing hard crime for years. When a writer develops a story, he always has in mind a character / actor to bring the story to life. Last weekend I found a new one in Paul Vario playing Uncle Benny at Eddie Monroe. This was a neat film accentuated by the presence of Varia on screen and off screen (as well as his voice narration). I also liked the actress playing Benny's niece and Eddie's ex-wife, although they all did a great job in this exciting movie about playing with the bad guys and the double crossing that goes. Nice work all around ... and Mr. Vario shone brightest. One day my Tony Gangi must be ...
