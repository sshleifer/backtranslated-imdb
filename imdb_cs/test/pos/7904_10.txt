"On Your Own" is the moving story of four kids running away from a children's home after their mother dies, with only one suitcase, their dog Ralph, and $ 9, who go to their old mom in search of Uncle Jack. On the way, their car breaks and meets Peggy Williams, a holiday teacher, and offers them help. Together with their children, they are looking for Uncle Jack, but they are forced to hide from the authorities who are planning to take them back to foster care as they do. This film tells the story of love, friendship and how important it is to stick to your family when they set out on their amazing journey.
