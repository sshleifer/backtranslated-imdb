My friend and I read the summer and watched the trailer and were very interested and excited to rent this movie. BAD IDEA. We thought a movie with actors was influential, but our expectations were extremely low. Especially the trailer and the summer are misleading to the lie. The movie started slowly for the first 1 1/2 hours (reminder, about two hours) and when it finally gained momentum, it sucked. Besides, the actions were very hard to follow. It confused us because it jumped from one story to another in random order. The characters were not very realistic in reality. Sure, mother and son could actually be real people, but everyone else seemed to be the one or the other extreme. If you are a person who likes sick, twisted, unusual movies, then do it. But we recommend not to waste two hours of your life that you can not get back. Unfortunately nobody told us that ...
