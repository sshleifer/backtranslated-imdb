If you're a Pauly Shore fan, laugh your butt off. If not, this is a silly mess that wastes a very good talent. A cute student (Carla Gugino) from South Dakota invites her Californian dorm (Shore) home to share Thanksgiving. Notable performers: Lane Smith, Cindy Pickett, Mason Adams and the dazzling Tiffani-Amber Thiessen. Watch where you step.
