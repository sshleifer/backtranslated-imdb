Sit in the cellar with one and a half hours without light. It's like watching this subterranean search for the devil's door. An American researcher Owen (Vincent Gallo) travels to Moscow and collects a rescue team to look for his friend Sergei (Rade Serbedzia), an archaeologist who has disappeared in the catacombs below the Russian capital. You will be shocked to discover underground inhabitants who thrive in the damp and dark complex of caves and tunnels. Seekers will encounter the Gatekeeper of Hell, Andrej (Val Kilmer), and make a deal to continue their venture; Just to be successful when you realize they are dead. Also in the cast: Joaquin de Almedia, Oksana Akinshina, Sage Stallone, Joss Ackland and Julio Perillan.
