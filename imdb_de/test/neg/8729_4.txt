"The Apartment Complex" is a comic comedy full of crazy characters created in lieu of a real story telling a young psychologist (Lowe) who takes on a job managing an apartment complex and gets caught up in a murder mystery. That's what you call it , This low cal watch contains no suspense, no thrill, no drama, no action, few funny moments, a touch of nudity and almost no romance. "The Apartment Complex" is passable, an unforgettable junk food only for the most urgently needed sofa potatoes.
