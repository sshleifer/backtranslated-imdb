Another example of the female-in-prison genre. This is not exactly a genre known for quality movies, but it is one of the highest among many. A few women are taken by the brothels to a prison on a rocky island. Once there, they are exposed to much misguided sex and cruel "dialogues". For a movie with an amazing amount of sex and nudity, there is nothing truly erotic about it. The sex scenes seem awkward, and some scenes, like the shower scene, seem to be moving ever further. However, there are at least some minimum production values. On the W-i-P scale, I would put it above women for cell block nine, but under Chained Heat.
