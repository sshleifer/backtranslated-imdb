Although he hates field work, he finds himself in a truck with a rescue unit in the open desert, fights sandflies and whips and blows sand. There are four diamond prospectors ... Well, what's left of their scattered bones is discovered. In a long, cold desert night, a form-changing monster appears. Zach and his colleagues are scared when their truck is stranded and members of the unit are dying terribly. There is no character development. let alone to speak the dialogue. The desert, set in its own way, is beautiful and betraying ... The creature is as old as the desert sand. The cast includes Rachel Shelley, Warrick Grier and Patrick Shai.
