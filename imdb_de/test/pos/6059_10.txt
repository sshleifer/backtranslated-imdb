I was fortunate to be on Perris Island in the fall of 1959. The DI showed one evening in the open air theater right in front of our barracks, Plt 162, B ​​Co, 1st Bn, 1st ITR. <br /> <br Although we had not been there long enough to even think about watching a movie, we could hear the laughter. It's one of the many indelible memories of my thirteen weeks with PI. At a later date, I actually saw it in a theater. I still believe that to this day it is the most realistic portrayal of the experience of the late 1950s that has ever been made. No one did it better than Jack Webb ...
