The beautiful Annie Belle in her best stars in this adventure / sex movie. She wears her hair in a buzz cut bleached with platinum. She and her boyfriend visit a tropical Asian paradise. They have decided to nurture an "open" sexual relationship that takes place on their journey to a secret club / tribe where people live one year and are then reborn in a kind of ceremony. The landscape is beautiful, deep green gorges and jungles are explored. The pictures are very similar to those of the movie "Black Emanuelle". It is rich and colorful. Recommended!
