A young Korean artist lives in Amsterdam. She is a bit lonely and has never had a serious relationship, insisting she "wait" for the right person. She works in the public space and paints portraits for passers-by, but she also mentions her favorite daisies, Daisies. But suddenly she has a secret admirer. Flowers are delivered to her home every day at 4:15 am, usually daisies, but she can not catch the sender. This is because her friend, who is unknown to her, is a Korean batsman and he wants to remain hidden for the time being. One day, however, another attractive Korean artist is sitting on the square for the artist, accidentally carrying a pot of daisies. She concludes that he is her shy hero and also the man she has been waiting for. This second gentleman also has a secret; He is an Interpol agent. The assassin can see everything that happens in the square because there is an apartment nearby. Of course he is disturbed that another man has entered the life of the young girl. How will this shady love triangle take place? This is a beautiful picture. The environment in Amsterdam and the surrounding countryside is very, very nice. Add three most attractive young Korean actors and visually every viewer has a breathtaking panorama in front of them. The story is also pretty nice, as it's a mix of drama and action, with a dash of the unexpected. Costumes are very productive and production values ​​are high. In short, if you're looking for a high-quality foreign movie with a compelling story and a great backdrop, this movie will be a wonderful choice for an entertaining evening. If you have someone's hand to hold, so much the better.
