This story about a man's 28-year struggle for death, which would free him from his already dead body, becomes a masterpiece to be remembered thanks to an art team in a state of grace. Directed, written, edited and enumerated by Alejandro Amenabar, it touches you from the first pictures and does not rest your eyes and heart until the last credits, thanks to Alejandro and a group of wonderful actors and actresses. Bardem is an acting beast: one of the few comedians to make a masterpiece of almost any character, the supporting actresses are great in their roles, and the story is narrated with such sensitivity that you laugh and cry as we used to to do the great old masterpieces. The best movie of the year in all directions. 10/10
