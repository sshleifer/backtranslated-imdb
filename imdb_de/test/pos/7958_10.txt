Palwol ui Christmas is very Korean. If you were in Korea or have Korean friends, you should know what I mean. Koreans are very traditional people, they love to be very calm. a kind of feeling that you can not find in today's world. I think that's why this movie is so special. It's wrong to compare this movie to "Love Letter" by Shunji Iwai, Japanese are very good, but could never make one like Palwol ui Christmas. If you have a chance, watch this movie.
