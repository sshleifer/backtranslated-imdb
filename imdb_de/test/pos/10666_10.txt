Much has already been said about this film and its outstanding importance for the design of film language. If you are interested in the history of film, you can really understand Potemkin in addition to most of the films made before the film to understand the innovations that Eisenstein gives to the medium. It should be allowed that Eisenstein was not the only montage theorist and the principles of montage editing would probably have been discovered at another time. To date, however, few directors have turned to the ability with which Eisenstein has created meaning through the combination of images at such an early stage in the development of the medium. Potemkin is still one of the most beautiful and moving films ever made , You should see it, buy it and tattoo it on your chest.
