Teen Deviyaan, one of the best romantic classics, is a movie about choosing among your softest emotions, love. This is a simple story of a middle-class man who has come to the fore with his poetic talent and charming personality. He is attracted by three women and vice versa, one a Hindi movie star, the second a rich woman and the third one who lives in his neighborhood. <br /> <br /> The situation confuses him as he has to choose from them as his life partner. He asked her every few days. Finally, he goes to an astrologer and asks him whom he loves the most. His confusion is gone and he met the lady living in his neighborhood who really cares about him and to love him is his true love. Devanand played the main character and has completely established his contemporary romantic image.
