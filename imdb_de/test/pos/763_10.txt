This movie is a great movie. The movie shows so many topics in an amazing movie. Driving Lessons revolves around Ben (Rupert Grint), a shy 17-year-old who comes of age. The film continues on how Ben is fed up with his dominating mother and how he just wants to be himself. Ben then meets Evie, with whom he makes a special connection. They go on a journey and Ben finds himself and what a relationship he and Evie share. This is the movie you see alone or with another person. No matter what, you will all feel what Ben is going through in the movie. It's a very heartwarming movie that just makes you think about whether your driving lessons have ever been so emotional or a journey like Ben's Lessons.
