From the late Sydney Pollack comes an adult love story about human nature, pain, passion and betrayal. Dutch police officer Van Den Broeck (Harrison Ford) is devastated when he learns that his beloved wife died in a plane crash. He is still angry when he learns of her affair with the husband of a famous congresswoman Kay Chandler (Kristen Scott Thomas). He makes an appointment with her and sees if she knew or knew that he had no idea. They begin to befriend, and out of their mutual pain and suffering begins an intense affair (which can be considered as a cure for both). Memories of the dead and the pressure of their respective jobs drift apart and do more harm than good or good can you escape the past? Well, you have to look at Random Hearts and find out. A moving and well-made film with a great cast.
