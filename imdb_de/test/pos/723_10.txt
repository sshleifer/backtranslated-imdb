Mostly in the back alleys of Toronto NG is a dark, mysterious journey that takes the viewer into the minds of a young man and a young woman (Vern and Sarah), each of whom has a fascination with riddles and a catastrophic incident in their past. Fine dialogues and first-class casting drive this inconspicuous, noiric journey into the girl's search for the meaning behind the word puzzles that crop up again and again in her life. With their help, an all but enthusiastic male friend, the two reach the end of their search, but with a payable price. The film never wants to answer all its secrets, but turns out to be excellent in presenting several plot changes
