In a time of predictable films in which violence, cheap romance, and melodrama abound, it is extremely surprising to find such a strange movie. The plot itself is captivating and the actors are excellent, especially Alan Rickman. If you want to watch a movie that does not provide all the answers before you ask the questions, a movie that will surprise you (in good or bad), Dark Harbor is for you. And if you are not convinced, believe me, that Alan Rickmans performance is worth it ... especially in the end, ladies ...
