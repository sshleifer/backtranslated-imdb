"Strike Force" or "The Librarians" is an entertaining action movie that does not take itself too seriously. William Forsythe plays the lead role as Simon, who seeks a missing daughter of a wealthy customer. He meets with Sandi (Erika Eleniak), who is also looking for someone - her sister. But there are bad bad guys. The worst of all is Marcos Canarious (Andrew Divoff). Marcos likes to kill people. Now Simon and Sandi have to band together to defeat the bad guys. The whole cast is great, Divoff steals the movie. There are also cameos by Ed Lauter and Burt Reynolds. If you are looking for a good action movie, look at it and have a good time.
