This mini series is a symbol of the Australian spirit. While there may be disagreements that may be considered glaring inconsistencies, the film represents a spirit that is unique in Australia and should be nurtured. If anything, this miniseries demonstrates the Australian humor. The ability to laugh at the most ridiculous. Our willingness to laugh even in the worst situations. Much of this series focuses on camaraderie and how humor can be used as a survival tool, but it also contains elements of the drama that evoke real emotions. The leading actors who appear are absolutely sensational and convince in their different roles. Capture the essence of their characters perfectly. This miniseries should be mandatory for all Australians.
