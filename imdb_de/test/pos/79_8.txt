Again, the sacred home of Bugs Bunny is hurt by careless people. This time, the traveling circus placed a lion cage directly over the rabbit's hole. So Bug has to come out of the hole and face Nero (that's the name of the lion), which he does in his usual comic style. After a slow and rocky start of the car, things get better and it gets pretty funny. Nero, the lion, turns out to be a pretty good foil for bugs and this makes these shorts a very memorable, if still classic figure.
