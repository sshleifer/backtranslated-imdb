Years ago, Madonna broke with "Ray of Light" to a truly amazing level of musical skill, and since then, she has occasionally even exceeded her own standards. This concert production with hypnotic cut, amazing dance, hallucinatory light effects and trance-inducing arrangements destroys all previous efforts. Madonna's apparent ambition to create world peace through music and dance alone may seem hopeless or absurd to some. But hell, somebody has to do it! With their compilation of the remarkable talent of all those involved in the production, "Confessions Tour Live from London" once again ranks them among the ten best artists in the world working in every medium.
