The most beautiful animal movie since Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around a 23-year-old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems still seem to fail in his boss' company. Brian also dates the boss's daughter, Casey. Brian, however, secretly invented a robot dog named CHOMPS, modeled after his own dog Rascal. But CHOMPS is no ordinary dog, he is as fast as a cheetah, he has an X-ray, he can jump 6 feet and he has a strength of 20 men. The new invention impresses its boss and makes his business a success. When the company's competitors learn of CHOMPS, they try to find a way to catch it. Can CHOMPS trick her before it's too late? This movie is a classic for all ages.
