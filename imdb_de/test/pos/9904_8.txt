At the Phoenix Film Festival 2005, it was no secret which movie was at the top of the list. Checking Out (2004) brought Harkins Cine-Capri to Scottsdale with many movie lovers. Festival participants waiting in line for hours were dismissed at the door despite the capacity of two theaters. an inspirational comedy for a broad audience. Director Jeff Hare combines traditional film technique with a new creativity and captures Peter Falk in his biggest and most sentimental moments (mostly in intimate close-ups) on the screen Checking Out (2004) is a groundbreaking independent film with its uplifting mix of witty humor and narrative plot who deserves your attention.
