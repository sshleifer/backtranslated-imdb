Frank Horrigan (Clint Eastwood) is an intelligence agent plagued with guilt over the assassination of President John F. Kennedy during his service. Thirty years later, the current president enters a re-election campaign, but he receives death threats. and Horrigan was called in to help with routine research. John Malkovich plays the professional assassin and master of disguise following the president, and using the past, he begins to torture Horrigan in a psychological duel between cat and mouse. Malkovich, Eastwood and Rene Russo all do wonderful things in this first-class thriller. The direction is excellent and the whole picture is charged with tension and intrigue throughout. <br /> <br /> A must for thriller fans 8/10
