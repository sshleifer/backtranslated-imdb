In this flame of emotions, we are presented to several different high school teens on an eventful day of school. Everyone struggles with belonging, finds themselves, and keeps their painful secrets from being revealed. They feel that there is a documentation of the coming of old age that focuses on the students' inner landscape and their handling of everyday challenges. Like a hymn requiem. Thalluri moves the camera with great skill and slowly invites us into our thoughts and hearts of teenagers. A fictional reconstruction that enlightens us all and reminds of the frailty of youth. One of the most important films of today. Every teenager should be given the opportunity to see this moving piece of film.
