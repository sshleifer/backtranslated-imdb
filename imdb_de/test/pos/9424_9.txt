Basil Rathbone and Nigel Bruce return as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson returns in this superior story of Holmes fighting against the 3rd empire and the genius of Professor Moriarty. The film opens in Switzerland, as Holmes disguised himself as an "old bookseller". He has to Dr. Bring Tobel and the Tobel Bomb Site to England before the Germans are able to abduct Tobel. Holmes succeeds and the Germans catch up with the evil professor. Moriarty manages to outsmart Scotland Yard and LeStrad "Dennis Hoey" by kidnapping Tobel. The only clue left by Tobel is a list of "dancing men". Who will break the hidden code of dancing men, Holmes or Moriarty first? Can Holmes prevent the bomb site from falling into German hands, saving England from the precision bombing techniques developed by Tobel's bomb site? Watch and enjoy.
