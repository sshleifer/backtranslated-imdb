A young woman leaves her provincial life for a new one in the city and there she meets another woman whom she falls in love with. Their relationship quickly becomes physical and they both believe they are soul mates until one day the girls from the province come home and find a man in their bed. Her lover tells her that her relationship was just an experiment and she really likes men. Uh, kind of like Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. The provincial girl, broken, torn and shattered by this discovery, is definitely moving out and discovering what the real world is all about, while falling into the hands of all kinds of vengeful and ravening people in England.
