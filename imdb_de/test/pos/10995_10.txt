I really enjoyed the story of Gabrielle Burton about a mysterious gift and its impact on recipients in the past and present. The talented Burton family, consisting of five film sisters, a writer and a dancing father, offers a charming action that has been respectfully edited for clarity, memorably selected songs, and a beautifully filmed piece that made me laugh and cry as the vulnerability of the characters invited me to their predicamants. There was a farce-like attitude to this work with touching undertones of innocent amazement. Fanatastic
