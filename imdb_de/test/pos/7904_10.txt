"On Your Own" is a touching story of four children who ran away from the nursing home after their mother's death, with only one suitcase, her dog Ralph and $ 9, driving her old car in search of her uncle Jack set out. On the way, their car breaks down and they meet Peggy Williams, a school teacher on vacation, and she offers to help them. Together, she and the children are looking for Uncle Jack, but they have to hide from the authorities who intend to bring her back into nursing care. This film tells a story about love, friendship and how important it is to stay with your family as the children embark on their amazing journey.
