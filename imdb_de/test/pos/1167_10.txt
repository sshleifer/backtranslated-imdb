Sean Connery is very good as the Great Raisuli, Lord of the Rif and defender of the faithful. This is an adventure movie featuring Arabs, Germans and the USMC, all of which come into play at one point or another. There is also a lot of humor in the interaction between main and secondary characters. The story is based on the true incident in which a wealthy Greek-American businessman was abducted by the Raisuli in the early 1900s. Milius has replaced Candace Bergen and her two children as victims of the kidnapping, and this opens up a lot of literary license to viewers of two of the most famous (though mostly forgotten) political quotes of the TR era. Brian Keith (very good like TR) says: "Pedecaris is alive or the Raisuli is dead!"; and John Huston (also good at Secretary of State John Hay) asks the Japanese Ambassador at a White House dinner: "You like knives?
