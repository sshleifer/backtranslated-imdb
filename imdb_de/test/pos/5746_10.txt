A young man discovers that life is valuable after being seriously injured in a road accident. His father is a pastor who changes his attitude to life, and he spends the entire movie sharing his newly discovered love for others in this shocking and heartwarming movie. The theme is built around the street car and it's a great movie for people who are interested in movies like "the fast and the furious". This shows how people can overcome their problems and gives the general message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
