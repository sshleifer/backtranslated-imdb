Too bad, nobody seems to know this little thriller masterpiece. Where bigger, budgeted films fail, "Terminal Choice" delivers a lot of thrills, shocks and bloody violence. A little gem that needs to be searched in your local video store. The anonymous guy below is absolutely right when he says you'll never trust hospitals again ... it's so effective! Good ending too, not really a twist, but it does not end up the way you would imagine. Yes, Ellen Barkin is in an early role ...
