This is one of the best martial arts (kung fu) movies of all time. If you love martial arts movies, you should not miss this. From flying nuns to practicing monks, this movie has top kung fu styles and a good story. It's about a priest from the Wu Dang clan who is trying to kill all Shaoulin fighters to get the title of best martial arts. After killing important members of the Shaoulin Temple, the Shoulin faith remains in the hands of two boys secretly training under a Shaoulin monk. The white abbot or priest from the Wu Dang clan develops new techniques that turn him into iron. Well, this is a classic not to be missed. These are ninjas, shaoulin and nuns. It's a great classic
