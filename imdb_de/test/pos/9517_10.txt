If you want to get an excellent overview of Byzantine history in a colorful way, this is for you. This documentary is also great for educators who teach about Roman or medieval history. This documentary consists of three parts, which deal first with the fall of the Western Roman Empire, the rise of Christianity and the beginning of the Byzantine Empire. The second video deals with Byzantine diplomacy and the iconoclastic controversy. The third and final video explains the decline and fall of Byzantium. The series is shot in several countries and miraculously integrates Byzantine history into the realities of the modern world. It shows the location of this civilization as part of human civilization in general. Take your time to watch.
