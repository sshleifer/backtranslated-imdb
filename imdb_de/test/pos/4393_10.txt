Talking about the South, the Rednecks and the Hillbillies ... Well, I'm from the South: Argentina, to be exact, near the South Pole. Is that enough south? Seriously, some stories are universal. We've never been to Greece and have not experienced the classic time of Homer, but he speaks to us today. So also Shakespeare and Dante. And stories from widely unknown places also reach us when told with sensitivity, intelligence and humor, just like "O Brother". Besides, we all (the rest of the world) have our own Hillbillies! And our own depression era (which you ever heard of Argentina in 2001?), Our politicians and racism, our gentle souls like Delmar and Tommy ... I just loved this movie, folks. Despite the subtitles on the other side of the world.
