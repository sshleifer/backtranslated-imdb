Lost is not the biggest TV show in history, but it's not far off. It does not have the plot or characterization of the West Wing, or possibly even the early ER, but it's probably the most thrilling show I've ever encountered. I love how I can not guess what's going to happen. I love to recount the characters' backstories, which often lead to new dimensions in which we can see them. In a way, I want the show to last forever, but I think they can pull 6-7 seasons out of it before they finish on a glorious climax. The combination of the characters and their nationalities, coupled with the flowing movement of the show to and fro, extending the "dead characters" adds to the overwhelming feeling that this show is something completely different than we are used to. It's captivating, surprising and (here's a small suggestion for all conspiracy theorists) more than a little interactive. Maintain these internet discussions - you just add the storyline ...
