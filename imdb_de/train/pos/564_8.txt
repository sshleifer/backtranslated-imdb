Western Wyoming cowboy James Stewart moseys sets off for the Yukon Territory to dig for gold, sell his oxen, and try meeting pretty saloon owner Ruth Roman if he can shake the tail of crooked Sheriff John McIntire, who wants Stewart's cattle. Beautiful cinematography in collaboration of William Fritzsche and Bill Daniels. Good Technicolor adventure by Universal International, directed by Anthony Mann. <br /> <br /> Also with Corinne Calvet, Connie Gilchrist and Walter Brennan as Stewart's buddy.
