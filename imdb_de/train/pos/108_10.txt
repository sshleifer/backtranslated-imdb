"Europa" (or "Zentropa") is one of the most impressive films I have ever seen. The mix of grayscale and color photography is almost seamless ... a true eye candy. The picture was a contender for a Golden Palm in Canners in 1991. The award went to Barton Fink (of the Coen brothers); a movie that is very similar to Zentropa. Here is an exercise in class: Borrow both films and set up your own.
