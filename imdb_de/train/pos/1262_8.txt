A young and promising college graduate (played by Blair Underwood) is jailed for murdering an innocent young girl and put on death row. Sean Connery plays the "Happy Go Lucky" lawyer, whom the grandmother of the retired young man follows and asks to take over the case in the hope of freeing her grandson. Playing a cop well involved in the case, Laurence Fishburne is determined to expose a theory that Connery could dig up to try and rescue the boy he and the entire police force swear the crime is to blame , Ed Harris, Kate Capshaw, Ruby Dee and late Lynne Thigpen play the main role in this very dramatic thriller.
