Reese Witherspoon plays Dani, a young country girl who falls madly in love with the new 17-year-old neighbor Court, in love with Jason London. Court tries his best to make Dani realize that the difference in her age would make a love affair unlikely. Soon, the irresistible charm of Dani will win the will of Court. Next comes the meeting of Dani's older sister, played by Emily Warfield, and the beginning of a short-lived love / jealousy problem. Tess Harper and Sam Waterston complete the cast. This is a fresh, free spirit. but a heartbreaking drama that touches deeply. Feel free to cry.
