"Fido" is recommended because it has turned a tired genre, zombies, into a most original movie experience. The atmosphere of the early '50s is breathtaking, the acting great and the entire production shows a careful planning. Suddenly the viewer dives into a world of beautiful vintage cars, "Eisenhower Era" dresses, Art Deco furniture and zombie servants. It is very easy to reject "Fido" as a cartoon-like fluff, similar to "Tank Girl," but the two films are very different. "Fido has a structure, a script that tells a story and a superior attitude, do not make mistakes, this is a daring black comedy that succeeds when so many others fail
