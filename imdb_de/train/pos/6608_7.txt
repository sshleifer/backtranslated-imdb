There is about 25 years of inspiration inside. Starting with Blade Runner's existential themes and the vision of the future - with billboards for their products, the technology of the later Matrix movies and the Spielberg AI, and finally, the black and white Sin-style comics look similar to the city. The creators have put a lot of effort into the visual department and the result is a well-planned neo-noir of the future. Add a detective story and you have an interesting movie. I know what it was meant to be, but regardless of the impressive images, it was not enough to make it to the finish.
