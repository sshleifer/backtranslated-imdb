The promise of a brother is a wonderful family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, an Olympic Olympic champion. The film shows the life of this athlete from a young age to full adulthood. The love and support of family members is consistently evident. How Dan and the rest of his family deal with winning and losing races is a lifetime for all of us. The dedication and determination of Dan's coach and teammates show what it takes to build a true team. How Dan and his family deal with a devastating illness of a loved one is portrayed without suppressing the feeling or sugars. The faith of the family is shown in basic terms and is obviously an integral part of their life. This is a strong family movie that can be important to a person of all ages.
