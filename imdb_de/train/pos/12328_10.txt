"Bread" spits out the conventions of horror movies in general and "Night of the Living Dead" in particular very sharply and is always inventive. The production values ​​are sometimes a little rough (after all, it's a student movie), but they never lose sight of the entertainment goal. Hey - George Romero liked it enough to record it on the remastered "Dead" videotape, laserdisc and DVD ... that should tell you something.
