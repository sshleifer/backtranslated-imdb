Jacknife is a masterpiece of the 80s. It's a movie that touches on breathtaking drama and a very interesting direction. In Jacknife, lovers of both European and American cinema can find things to relate to. The script is very captivating and full of beautiful characters. Ed Harris gives one of the best performances so far. He portrays his alcoholic hero, who feels his broken heart in every line and every movement. Robert De Niro lets us consider him again as one of the greatest actors of all time in one of the simplest but most realistic performances of his career. Jacknife never gets bored, because he shows his heroes the typical characteristics of Hollywood. Nobody is a hero after the war. Everyone is a loser, and this movie is about this simple truth. No one can make up for his pieces after a war, just like the heroes of this film. Jacknife is about the diseases caused by the war. Simply great movie.
