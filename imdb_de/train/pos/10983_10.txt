'Gray Gardens' (1975) is the bizarre documentation of the Maysles brothers about Jackie Bouvier Kennedy Onassis' eccentric aunt and first cousin who live like pigs in a run-down 28-room mansion on East Hampton on Long Island. "Big Edie 'Bouvier Beale, 78, witty and dry, and her daughter' Little Edie 'Beale, 56, (emotionally about 13 years old), a still beautiful woman who once had a bright future, lives from the rest of the world isolated, with the exception of their many cats and raccoons the loft, they enjoy themselves by arguing, listening to the radio or singing together all day long (you do not even own a TV) Viewers are attracted by these two very special but obviously dysfunctional people One of the better documentary films ever made, and still a cult classic today.
