This is a must for anyone who loves thrillers especially political thrillers. One scene that stands out is Milgram's experiment, it is shot to perfection. Very rarely do we see a movie being filmed and shown in the way this film is presented. The three-headed team is formed to investigate the murder. However, one member disagrees with the final findings of the committee. According to the stipulated conditions, this member would initiate a one-man investigation of the murder. Through this investigation, he becomes involved in deep and dark secrets of the high office politics and how they are controlled.
