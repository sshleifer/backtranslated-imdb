THE INVADERS IS A QUICK-MOVING SCI-FI-THRILLER STARTING BEN CROSS AND SEAN YOUNG. BEN PLAYS RACE, A TRAVELER FROM ANOTHER GALAXY TRYING TO FIND ANNIE (PLAYED BY SEAN) THAT IS PHYSICALLY IDEAL TO HAVE HIS CHILD. THIS CHILD, IF HIS PLANET WAS BORN AND RETURNED, MAY BE THE ONLY CHANCE THAT HIS RACE CAN SURVIVE. THE ENEMY, AN ALIEN THAT DESTROYED RACE'S PEOPLE, FOLLOWED RENN AND THE STARS TO COMPLETE THIS BIRTH. CROSS AGAIN SHOWS GREAT RANGE OF COMICAL BEGINNERS WHEN THE ALIEN ENTER INTO A LOCAL BAR AND FIRST FIRST OIL COCKTAILS IN HIS RACES WITH THE ENEMY AND THE DRAMA IF HE CAN KEEP KINNE AND WEAR ALIVE THE CHILDHOOD.
