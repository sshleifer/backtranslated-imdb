This is a must-have documentary for those who fear that modern youth has lost their sense of adventure and their sense of morality. Darius Goes West is an amazing roller coaster ride of a story. We live the life of Darius and the crew as they embark on the journey of their lives. Darius has Duchenne muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects all the muscles in his body. He is confined to a wheelchair and needs attention around the clock. So how could this crew of young friends manage to take him on a 6,000-mile trip to the West Coast and back? Watch the movie and experience the highs and lows of this great adventure - laugh and cry with the crew as they master unimaginable challenges, and enjoy the final triumph when they return to their home town three weeks later for a rapturous reception come back and have some great surprises!
