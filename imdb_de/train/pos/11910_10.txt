An adorable little thriller starts with Trevor Howard in his Jag convertible and ends at a dock in Liverpool. It's a thrill as the ex-spy has to restart his career while receiving some serious R & R catalogs (how British is that?). Trevor Howard and Jean Simmons frogs from London to Newcastle-upon-Tyne to Liverpool (via Ullswater) - he's just been kicked out of MI5 or so and she's on the run, wrongfully charged with murder. There are shabby docks, hilly hills in the Lake District, sheep, country pubs, lost copper spikes, waterfalls, a bunch of amateur riders, rooftop chases and lots of Chinamen (do not ask). <br /> <br /> ..and an overwhelming example of British Noir ...
