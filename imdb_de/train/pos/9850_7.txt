An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a sloth and a mammoth, who rescued a baby who was about to be killed by a group of tigers during the Ice Age. The moral of the film shows that good behavior can bring benefits to others at the end. One of the tigers in the group was ordered to finally catch the baby, barely rescued by his mother when the tigers attacked their community. The baby was then rescued by the sloth and the mammoth, but the tiger joined with the aim of finally taking the baby away. They went on very arduous roads with great danger, and immediately the tiger should fall and be rescued by the mammoth. In the end, the group of tigers tried to catch the baby, but the mammoth unbelievably helped him overcome this attack and return the baby to his father and the community to which he belongs.
