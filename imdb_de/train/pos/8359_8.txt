Bertrand Blier is indeed the terrible French cinema and in the seventies he always shocked the public. Filmed with his favorite duo (Depardieu and Dewaere) and the usual dose of sex (Miou Miou plays their typical role, at least those from the seventies, so little did we know that ten years later she would be the best French actress). First and foremost, "Les Valseuses" is also one of the first road movies, as the viewer is only taken along by two small criminals for traveling. For those who are just happy with family life or just do not know any more, the movie would be a real shock, but this movie is more than that, it just makes you think of all the usual things in life (working for the car, being ) limited at work, etc.). It is a kind of critic against the hypocritical society in which we live. Great work and it just makes you want two things: Dewaere died too young, because he was a top actor, and of course Depardieu would rather have to stay French movies, as he proves here, that no one can beat him. Timeless classic and 20 years later it will still shock some ...
