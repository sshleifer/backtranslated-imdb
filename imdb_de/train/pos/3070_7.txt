"Panic" is a captivating, blurry genre film about a brooding and conflicted hitman reconciling being in love with a younger, uninhibited barber, his love for wife and son, his duty to his employer / father with his own identity. Although the film has a nebulous purpose and an ambiguous ending, it is an excellent production in almost all areas. The clarity and sincerity of the characters in such an unlikely story can fascinate and disturb the audience.
