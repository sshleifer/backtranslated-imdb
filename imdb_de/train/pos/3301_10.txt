Apart from the fact that it was one of the few films I ever shed a tear in (bye-bye-manhood), this is one of the most beautiful Indian movies ever made. From the finely crafted sets to the haunting looks that Meena Kumari gives, no one can forget it. The music of Pakeezah is amazing, even more so if you can understand the sublime poetry, and is definitely one of those movies "OMG, 5 minutes another song". Feeling hooked, Sahibjaan is caught between all the amazing jewelry she wears and a manicured yard she casually passes by. A parody of all your dreams you ever had ... .....
