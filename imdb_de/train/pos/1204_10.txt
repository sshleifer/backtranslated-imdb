Recognized by Variety as one of the largest documentary films ever to come from Canada. Al Pacino, Roger Ebert, Neil Simon, Matt Dillon and a number of celebrities make this film a Canadian classic. The movie is really best described as "Roger & Me" and "The Player". Watch as Kenny Hotz and Spenny PITCH show their script to Hollywood's big boys. The only American film to ever leave Canada, the 1997 Toronto Film Festival opened the Toronto Indie Film Award and was the winner of the Best Indie Film Award in Toronto. European premiere was at the renowned HOF Filmfest in Germany. First American Comedy Festival Aspen 1999. For more information, visit www.kennyhotz.com
