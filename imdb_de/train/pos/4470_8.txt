Similar to "On the Town" this musical misses sailors on shore leave the later classic in terms of pace and quality of the songs, but has its own charm. Kelly has three fantastic dance routines: one with Jerry, the cartoon mouse of "Tom and Jerry," one with a little girl, and a fantasy sequence in which he's a Spanish lover determined to set his lady high To reach the balcony. Sinatra, who plays Kelly's shy, inexperienced buddy, and Grayson, the woman who serves the two men as a love interest, sing most of the song. Iturbi offers some good piano playing. With almost two and a half hours it is a bit too long for a light musical, but it does not pull.
