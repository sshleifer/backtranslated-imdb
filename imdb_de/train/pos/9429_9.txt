This film is a classic in the truest sense of the word. It is very entertaining and also very disturbing. The acting in this movie is well done. The story itself is believable, exciting and thoughtful. The character development is also done well, the audience can clearly see how each character is emotionally tested by this movie. The bad guys in this movie are very threatening. From the first moment, viewers can see that they are up to something. This film shows how a person, when taken from civilization and in the midst of a forest, faces a life or death situation, becomes almost as wild and wild as the animals that inhabit it. Not everyone will like this movie, there are many disturbing content that can make some viewers uneasy. It is definitely worth seeing. This movie is a masterpiece.
