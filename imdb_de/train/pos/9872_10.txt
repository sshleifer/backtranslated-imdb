Once again, the two contentious professors must unite to save the lost world. The five members of the first expedition return (for a list of actors, see The Lost World, 1992). A man looking for oil brings a drilling crew to the plateau. Instead of fueling oil, they are tapping an underground volcano that threatens all life in the lost world. The oil crew collides with the natives and the scientific expedition. Although the situation looks hopeless ... (I will not tell you the end).
