Simple story ... why do you say more? It nails it's premise. World War III kills all or much of humanity, and we see two of the survivors. The message is that the two belligerent sides should not primarily conflict with each other. Focused on representatives from each side, we see that they have everything to come together: <br /> <br /> Security ... find resources ... food, shelter, etc ... survival ... love .... <br /> <br /> In the end, they decided to pool their resources (they finally do) to survive. Simple story, expressed in the limited budget of the early television landscape of the 60s. We see it in 2009 as a bit old and maybe predictable. In the early sixties nobody had seen such a thing ... I give it a 10 ...
