A great Bugs Bunny cartoon from years ago has bugs as a performer in a shop window of a local department store. After he's done for the day, the manager comes in and tells him he'll move soon. Bugs is glad that he finds out that the new task is a preparation ... and this preparation has something to do with stuffing animals. Animals like a particular rabbit. This leads to a dispute between the mischievous rabbit and his former employer. I thought that was short to be delightful and definitely one of the better of the early 1940s. Almost 60 years later, it still remains so funny. This animated short film is on CD 1 of the Looney Tunes Golden Collection Volume 2. My degree: A-
