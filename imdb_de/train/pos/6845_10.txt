"Dressed to kill" is certainly one of the best horror / thriller movies ever made. It's a tight, stylish and extremely exciting mix of sex and violence. The spectacle is pretty good, the orchestral score of Pino Donaggio is unforgettable and there are many surprises Thriller fans remain fascinated. "Dressed to Kill" is a murder mystery involving a sexually frustrated housewife (Angie Dickinson), her adolescent son (Keith Gordon), her psychoanalyst (Michael Caine), and a high-priced call girl (Nancy Allen) The Murderer in the film is a transsexual named Bobbi, who is also one of Caines patients. The film is full of breathtaking moments: The infamous elevator murder scene is extremely stylish and also quite bloody. We recommend.
