Just like its predecessor (Paris Je T'Aime), New York I Love You is a mix of different stories that reflect the different types and aspects of love. In contrast, the rhythm is much faster and the stories much shorter. The film offers a unique view of the city of New York with its diverse and diverse landscapes. New York, I Love You offers a stellar cast, with such great actors as Shia LaBeouf, Natalie Portman, Ian McKellen, Hayden Christensen, Chistina Rcci and Orlando Bloom, Ethan Hawke, James Caan and Robin Wright Penn and other outstanding writers and directors Brett Ratner and Anthony Mingella.
