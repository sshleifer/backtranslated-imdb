Aggie (Diane Keaton) is an excellent and award-winning author with a beautiful face and an independent spirit. Hugh is in love. He persuades Aggie to become his fiancee. But does Aggie have to sacrifice her principles of honesty in the world of politics, where things are not always what they seem? And will she be able to withstand the rigors of a harsh media flash? This is above all a beautiful romance for those who love love stories. Hugh and Aggie are absolutely in love and their banter and entertainment are a good view. Although the film tries to show the political life in its reality, it does not succeed completely. Never mind. The production values ​​are high and the script is very elegantly written.
