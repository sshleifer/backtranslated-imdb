"Gunga Din": one of the biggest adventure stories ever told! A story about the British Foreign Legion in India in the 19th century and a simple "water carrier" named Gunga Din, a citizen of the region who wants to seek his military colleagues; Three British sergeants whose loyalty and camaraderie extend far beyond the confines of patriotism. Theirs is a true and lasting friendship with each other and everyone would be willing to sacrifice their own lives for the good of others. Gunga Din longs to be a soldier, especially a bugler, but can not reach that rank due to his subordinate social status. However, heroes are not made according to their social background, but by their willingness to sacrifice for the good of others. Gunga Din tries to prove his courage every turn, but will he ever reach the rank he so passionately seeks? ... "You are a better man than me, Gunga Din"! One of Hollywood's classics and a perfect 10 !!!!
