Rachel Griffiths writes and directs this award-winning short film. A heartwarming story about dealing with grief and remembering those we loved and lost. Although Griffiths is only 15 minutes long, he manages to bring so many emotions and truths to the film in a short time. Bud Tingwell gives a touching performance as Will, a widower who has to contend with the death of his wife. Will faces the harsh reality of loneliness and helplessness as he continues to look after Ruth's pet cow Tulip. The film shows the grief and responsibility felt for those who loved and lost them. Good cinematography, good direction and great acting. It will cry to all who have lost a loved one and survive.
