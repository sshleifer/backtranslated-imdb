Paris Je T'aime is a film that explores the different types and aspects of love and all the emotions it evokes. This film brings together some of the best directors from around the world, including Gus Van Sant, Joel and Ethan Coen, Walter Salles ... (read more) and Alfonso Cuaron, who shares short stories about love in Paris with a certain kind of direction. In this film we also have one of the best actors ever seen in a movie, including such great actors as Willem Dafoe, Steve Buscemi, Natalie Portman, Elijah Wood, Gerard Depardieu and many more with great performances. In summary, this film is a compilation of stories about happiness, separation, unexpected encounters, and love.
