A group of people are invited to reunite high school, but when they arrive, they discover that it is a scam by an old classmate they have played a near-fatal trick on. Now he's trying to take revenge on all those who hurt him by sealing all exits and cutting off all phone lines. Dark Slasher movie with an extraordinary premise. A few good performances, a few more creative death scenes, a lot of excitement and fear, some humor and an original ending. Unrated for Extreme Violence, Graphic Nudity, Sexual Situations, Profanity and Drug Use.
