An update on the skits and jokes you saw on a burlesque stage in the first half of the 20th century. It's a series of jokes. Some of them might tell you to your grandmother, others not, but it's a pretty sure bet she's heard them all before. For what it tries to be, it is not so bad. Before you hire it, remember that this is an older entertainment style that has more value as a story than comedy or tickling. Robin Williams has a few pieces, but is interchangeable with the other players.
