A production line with fart jokes pretending that "Babe" was never made; The writers clearly hoped that the trick of seeing animals speak would be enough to keep the movie going. It is not. Eddie Murphy sells himself again as a doctor, rediscovering his forgotten childhood, because he understands the incessant and funny chatter of guinea pigs, tigers, rats, dogs and pigeons. The cast is impressive (Albert Brooks, Julie Kavner, Reni Santoni, John Leguizamo, Garry Shandling, Ellen DeGeneres, Paul Reubens, Brian Doyle-Murray), but the script is so unimaginative, charming, and depressing that the whole thing rattles off pretty quickly ,
