Divorced lawyer Rick Magruder (Branagh) stumbles drunk from a party held one night by his law firm and has a chance to meet a woman named Mallory Doss (Davidtz), who was a waitress at the party and lost her car seems to have. Rick drives the woman to his home and there they discover that their car was parked there apparently by her father Dixon Doss (Duvall). Rick and Mallory go into the house and argue about the situation when Mallory undresses in front of Rick and then spends the night with her. Rick wakes up in the morning and Mallory meets him later in the day, asking him to sue her father for his dangerous behavior. Rick, now obsessed with Mallory and ready to do anything for her, succeeds in getting Dixon to justice and being sent to jail. However, when he is freed from his local friends, Rick gets into trouble and tries to protect himself and his children from the danger he unknowingly brought to life.
