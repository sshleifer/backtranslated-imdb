Bela Lugosi plays dr. Lorenz, who loves his wife so much that he does everything to keep her young. This movie begins with a wedding as the bride takes her vows, which she suddenly collapses. She is pronounced dead and taken away by funeral directors. The problem is that they are not real undertakers, but body-catchers. A wave of the bride's death at the altar and its disappearance confused the police. Enter the reporter Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She has Dr. Lorenz found out, but also decides to use her youth to keep his wife young.
