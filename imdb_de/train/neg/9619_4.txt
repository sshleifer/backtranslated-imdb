If you can believe it, * another * group of teens will return to * another cabin on the lake shore, three years after * one * other * of these deadly "accidents", one of which claimed one of their numbers. Low and lo and behold, a psycho wearing a patterned hockey mask (plus a cheap, papery one) emerges and wastes it one after another. This mechanical "Friday the 13th" Outlet has been easily recognized as one of the first digitally shot features, but here ends the interesting facts and all that's left is a predictable amateur production with below-average performances and a recurrent boom-microphone slump. A last-minute twist does little to lift the mood, and Memorial Day is something best thrown into a lake and forgotten. Only for crazy Slasher collectors.
