This is a real and funny film about a Japanese man suffering from a midlife crisis. In Japan, dancing was not approved in the dance floor. But when Shouhi Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting the beautiful girl he sees in the window of a dance studio, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance lessons. No one is surprised when he begins to admire him. But he must keep his secret pleasure from his colleagues and family. When the truth comes out, it's very funny.
