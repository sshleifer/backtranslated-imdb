Twisted Desire (1996) was a television movie starring Melissa Joan Hart. Melissa's character, Jennifer Stanton, 17, seduces her current boyfriend Nick Ryan to kill her parents. The film is based on the murders of the parents of 14-year-old Jessica Wiseman. Jessica had her 17-year-old friend Douglas Christopher Thomas shooting and killing her parents! Thomas was executed in 2000! Jessica was released from prison when she was 21 years old. Evidence now indicates that it was Jessica who fired the fatal bullet that killed her mother. Jessica is now known to live somewhere in Virginia.
