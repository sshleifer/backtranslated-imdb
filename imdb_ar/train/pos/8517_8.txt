Ross and Valerie are having discussions about establishing a family. The couple live in a luxury apartment and run an auction company dealing with valuable holdings. At the same time, the owner of a dedicated adoption agency takes a small leave and leaves the orphanage under the responsibility of his father (Leslie Nielsen). Father Harry works in the rental business and gets a great idea to "rent" some orphanage children to couples like Ross and Valerie. Harry, who realizes the dilemma of the couple, presents a family of siblings for 10 days renting! Brandon, Kyle and Molly move into the apartment with their temporary parents, leading to amusing consequences, due to inexperienced caregivers. But, where is the prospect of a happy ending? This is a beloved family movie. The actors, including Nielsen as a taxi dealer and Christopher Lloyd as a pleasant apartment doorman, are all fantastic. The script is fast and fun and the overall production values ​​are very high. Yes, if life is just that way! Orphans everywhere deserve a chance to prove that they are loved and can give a lot of joy to parents who are thinking about adoption. If you want to show a movie to your family rooted in good values ​​but also very entertaining, look for this movie. It is guaranteed that everyone laughs, even when their hearts melt.
