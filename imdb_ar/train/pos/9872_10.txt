Once again, the rioters must come together to save the lost world. The return of the five members of the first expedition (see Lost World, 1992, for a list of actors). A man looking for oil brings crew digging to the plateau. Instead of pumping oil, they exploit an underground tank that threatens life in the lost world. The oil crew collides with the indigenous people and the scientific mission. Although the situation seems hopeless ... (I will not tell you the end).
