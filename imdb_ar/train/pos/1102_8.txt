In Manhattan, Jim Blandings (Carey Grant) lives in the middle class with his wife Muriel (Mirna Loy), two teenage girls in one bedroom and one rented bathroom. Jim works for an advertising agency that collects $ 15,000.00 a year and is uncomfortable in his apartment due to lack of space. When he sees an advertisement for a huge home for sale in Connecticut at a reasonable price, he drives with his wife and real estate agent and decides to buy the old house without any technical advice. His best friend and lawyer, Bill Cole (Melvin Douglas), sends an engineer to get to know the house and tells the man he must put down the house and build another house. Jim checks the information with the other engineers and condemns all of them.
