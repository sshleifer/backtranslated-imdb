A fun action comedy in which Damien Zephron takes the life of Diaz, a cop who tricked his wife. He lives in a hotel that feels guilty about betraying his wife, gets depressed and stops caring, runs red lights just for fun, and feels sorry for himself. Silberman, a Jew who is shrinking under surveillance, is a leftist liberal who does not trust the police at all. He was told to accompany Diaz in his daily duties, unable to refuse because of the conditions of his testing. Soon, the situation will come back when Silberman finds out that his wife is betraying him, and ends up relieved by the person who was supposed to help him, a person he never trusted at first. Nuclear plot, car thieves, international spies, hysterical wife. Strange characters in a fun comedy about friendship and heroism.
