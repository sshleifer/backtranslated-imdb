This effort is based on the true story of Jim Morris, a high school baseball teacher / trainer, who is inspired by his players' experience and his long-term dream of playing in major disciplines. Denis Quid, who is not a stranger to sports movies, plays Maurice with enough conviction to fulfill his role as producers do a credible job of reviving the real world events that briefly led Morris's role as a relief hitter in Tampa Bay Devils Race. The first half of the film, which deals with a handful of high school baseball players (all of whom look older than actually being in high school) is less effective and perhaps very long. In general, the film suffers from some quick problems and some additional sub-points that we could have made without it. However, the film still involves a sensible engagement with an inspiring theme proving once again that baseball is a national hobby for some reason. Grade B-
