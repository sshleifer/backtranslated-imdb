Slow and full of inaccuracies. In view of its shortcomings, this account remains interesting for the famous and heroic siege of Alamu during the Battle of Texas for independence from Mexico. James Arnes as Jim Bowie. Brian Keith as Davey Crockett. Alec Baldwin as Colonel Travis. Raul Julia as General Santa Ana. This project is specifically designed for television starring David Ogden Stearns, Kathleen York and Jim Metzler. The original music is very good by Peter Bernstein.
