Brother's promise is a good family film. This is the biography of Dan Jansen, the skater of the Olympic Games. Movie shows the player's life from a young age through full life. Family love and support are obvious. How Dan and his family to deal with winning and losing society is a lesson for all of us. Dedicated to the decision of the Dan coach and its partners, it shows what it takes to make a real team. How Dan and his family are affected by the good disease of a loved one is shown without removing feelings or sugar. The family's faith is expressed in basic words and it is obviously a large part of their lives. This is a powerful family film that can be meaningful to someone of any age.
