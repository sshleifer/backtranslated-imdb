This movie is a classic in every sense of the word. It's very fun and also very disturbing. Acting in this movie is well done. The story itself is credible, thrilling, and well thought out. Character development is also done well, the audience can clearly see how each of the characters is emotionally tested in this movie. These banners in this movie are very threatening, since the first time the audience saw them to say they are up to something. This film shows how a person, when taken to civilization and placed in the middle of the woods, faces a life or death situation can slowly become almost as wild and weak as animals living there. Not everyone will love this movie, there are a lot of disturbing content that can make some viewers uncomfortable. It is definitely worth seeing. This movie is a masterpiece.
