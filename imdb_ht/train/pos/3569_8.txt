Creative use of modern and mysterious elements: 1956 Cadillac Convertible to transport bad stepmother Kathleen Turner (John Waters' "Serial Mom") and 2 tight sisters; Queen Marb as the faerie Queen; David Warner (Evil in Time Bandits) reduced to court; Cinderella (it is a babe) charcoal shoveling into a insatiable oven; Cinderella and her good heads both look like (and act like) rock stars.
