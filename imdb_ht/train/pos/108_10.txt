'Europa' (or, as it is also known, 'Centropa') is one of the most visually stunning movie I've ever seen. The mixture of gray and color photography is near seamlessly ... a true feast for the eyes. The photo was a contender for the Golden Palm 1991 in Canners. The award went to Barton Fink (by Coen brothers); a stylist film very similar to Zentropa. Here's a class exercise: rent both films and be a judge for yourself.
