This film plays in the 60s and is about an Italian family: Romano, Rosa wife and two children Gigi and Giancarlo emigrated from Solino to Duisburg in the Ruhr area. I love this movie, because I think it is quite realistic: it shows many foreign family problems when they come to another country: they have to get used to a new culture, a new environment and this can be difficult: especially if you do not know the language .... It is difficult for the family, but they find one way: to open a restaurant that offers typical Italian food, and it is called "Solino", as the cities. The film also shows different conflicts - Gigi and Giancarlo fell in love with the same girl, and although Rosa worked very hard, Romano refused to pay money to engage more workers, etc. etc. But stop, I do not want to tell you how it goes so.
