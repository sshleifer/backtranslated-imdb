In World War II, America developed a new huge bomber. Lois Lane and Clark Kent tell a story when the plane leaves for its first mission. Lois retracts into the plane before he takes off. There are also several Japanese saboteurs on board who intend to fly a bomber in Tokyo. Will Lois be able to warn Superman? What do you think? <br /> <br /> A fast-paced, colorful cartoon. The animation is a bit jerky, but much better than everything we see today. Color and sound have been beautifully restored. Only two problems - lousy score and racism (especially in the title).
