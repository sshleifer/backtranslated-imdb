The Silver Screen has been recently polished with this beautiful film about happy aging and enjoying the rainbow of life. This silver screen is full of silver hair, but the film's namesake looks more like 85/25 with its energy, humor and thirst for life. The story of Uncle Frank's extraordinary entertainer, his devoted wife Aunt Tilly and the abrupt inhabitants of local nursing homes inspire us to “live every day as if he is your last” and gives a glimmer of hope to those often scary golden years
