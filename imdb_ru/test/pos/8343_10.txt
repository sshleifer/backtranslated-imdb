This film is about a teenager who struggles with his social status at school. He is a “Good Christian” and feels that he lacks all the fun in high school. Therefore, he would like him to never become one. Having received his desire and tried the worldly lifestyle, he realizes that the quality of his life has deteriorated sharply, and wants to return to what he was. A good family movie with a positive message about pride in who you are, even if you are not the most popular.
