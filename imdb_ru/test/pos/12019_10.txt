Richard Norton really lights up the screen in this masterpiece of martial arts in Portland, Oregon. Norton, a heartbreaker from Australia, plays the evil Mr. Milversted, who runs a successful import / export business, smuggling weapons and engaging in trafficking in female flesh. Usually, women are pulled out of his favorite dance club with the help of a Hun detachment, the most famous of which is Bolo Jung, who plays the role of Ice. Worry comes to Milversted when a new policeman in the city, John Kim (Britton Lee), is about to avenge the murder of his dead partners from the hands of Milversted. If you have time to see only one martial arts film this year, do not miss this classic.
