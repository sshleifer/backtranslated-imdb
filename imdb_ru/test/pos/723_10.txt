Mostly in the remote streets of Toronto NG is a dark, mysterious journey that plunges the viewer into the minds of a young man and woman (Vern and Sarah), each of whom is fascinated by riddles and a catastrophic incident in the past. Excellent dialogue and first-class cast inspire this modest, arrogant journey in search of the meaning of the word puzzles girl who continue to appear in her life. By helping her, anything but an enthusiastic male friend, they both reach the end of their quest, but with pay. The film never intends to answer all its mysteries, but copes well with the exposure of several plot twists.
