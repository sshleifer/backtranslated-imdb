This film is one of those that cannot be judged by its appearance. Indeed, at first glance it seems that the plot simply focuses on the desire to have more money. But ... look at the other side ... What do you see? You see that money is only a metaphor for ambiguous feelings that a person feels: should I do the right thing or not? And ... what is "right"? Le will do a deeper analysis ... -What does this mean a small town on the border? - This means that sometimes we can get too close to the border, doing what we thought we could not ... - What does this mean arid soil shown in this photo? - It means dryness, which sometimes seizes our offended hearts ... - What does the character's phrase (KRISTEN) mean: “Now I belong to him”? - This means the loss of our free will due to our unintended actions. In my opinion, this is the author of the story, and the director tried to "tell" us. By the way ... do you remember that another character (JACK BARNS) said: "Nothing is so simple ..."
