Cliche is a romantic drama film with a very simple plot, but with a very good cinema and screenplay. The script, direction and acting was also good. The flow of the film is kind of manipulative to bring the audience to tears through the excellent music of love and the circumstances that work, but later, after the film, makes you feel raped in some way. Although Jones makes her character very memorable and attractive. A deeper story may have raised this film to a higher level, but, nevertheless, the film provides its genre. for hopeless romantics, big fans of a love story, big fans of soap drama, fans of the cinematography of the 50s Cinemascope and fans of leading actors .....
