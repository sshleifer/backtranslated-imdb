A young Korean artist lives in Amsterdam. She is a bit lonely and never had a serious relationship, insisting that she is “waiting” for the right person. She works in the town square, draws portraits for passers-by, but for herself she also indulges in her favorite flowers - daisies. But, suddenly, she has a secret admirer. Flowers are delivered to her residence every day at 4:15, usually with daisies, but she cannot catch the sender in the act. This is because she does not know that her beauty is a hired assassin from Korea, and he wants to remain hidden for the time being. Once in the square, however, another attractive Korean man sits behind the artist and, as it turned out, carries a pot of daisies. She concludes that he is her shy hero, as well as the man she was waiting to find. This second gentleman also has a secret; he is an Interpol agent. The killer can see everything that happens on the square, due to the fact that there is an apartment nearby. Naturally, he is concerned that another man has entered the life of a young girl. How will this shadow love triangle play out? This is a beautiful picture to view. The situation in Amsterdam and its surroundings is very, very nice. Add the three most attractive young Korean actors, and visually any viewer will see an amazing panorama in front of them. The story is also quite pleasant, it is a mixture of drama and action with a dash of surprise. Costumes are very attractive, and production values ​​are high. In short, anyone looking for a quality foreign film with a fascinating history and magnificent landscapes will find this film an excellent choice for an exciting evening. If you have someone's hand to hold during the review, all the better.
