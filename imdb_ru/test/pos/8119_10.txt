Bedrooms and hallways are a movie that makes you realize that the drama of life is hidden in the details. And this drama can also be very fun! Even though your personal life and your own friends may not be as unique as the characters in this film, everyone will definitely recognize themselves and others in the bright main characters of this film. And this is the great strength of bedrooms and hallways, in that the irony of life is that the most intricate things that happen to you can be the most interesting / fun / funny / sexy / ... things too! I hope you enjoy the movie as much as I do!
