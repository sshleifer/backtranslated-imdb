What attracts a man to military service? What prepares a person for cruel torture as a prisoner of war? What despair leads to planning and executing a runaway in the jungles of Southeast Asia? How does he cope with the ghosts and memories with which he returns home? Herzog tries to answer these questions in his documentary film about Dieter Dengler, a German immigrant and a pilot of the US Navy, shot down over Laos in 1966, who was captured, tortured and starved To the end of his life, Powerful and personal report about a person, his life and experience first-hand tells about the carefree integration of interviews, archival materials and new materials. You will never forget this story - or that person.
