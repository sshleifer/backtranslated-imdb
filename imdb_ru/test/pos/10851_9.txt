A young woman leaves her provincial life for the sake of a new one in the city, and there she meets another woman in love with. Their relationship quickly becomes physical, and they both consider themselves soulmates until one day the provincial girl returns home to find a man in her bed. Then her lover reveals to her that their relationship was just an experiment, and she really likes men. Hmm, kind of like Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. Anyway, a provincial girl, broken, torn and broken by this discovery, comes out and begins to discover what the real world is like when she falls into the hands of all the vengeful and audacious people in 19th century England.
