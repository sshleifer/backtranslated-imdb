"Big Scent" came into my life quite by accident and left an indelible mark on my soul. The scar of love, destruction and meaninglessness, which will forever remain a part of my life. This is a story about wonderful futility. We are helpless without each other. We are helpless against government misconduct. We are helpless about the choices we all try to make when in love. Deaf to mumble and continue the peace outside the window. Blind to the inevitable apocalypse. Dumb hate and greedy opinions of an overly condescending society. This is a story about personal commitment and victorious love conquering the ideology of war. Their petty quarrels are the sublime observation of human nature and how love comes with its pain and darkness. Each has some annoying aspect in their personality, and the creators observe this in the most simplistic and fantastic way. We travel only a short distance with the two main characters, but we still have the desire to complete our own travels with some simple, loving, honest philosophy in tow. I am so glad that I exist in this “our time”. Another 20 years on both sides of the date of my birth, and I would not see and feel .... .... Big Nyuka. William White. Sheffield.
