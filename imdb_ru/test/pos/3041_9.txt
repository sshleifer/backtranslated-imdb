Jon Voight plays the title role in this film based on the autobiography of the author Pat Conroy (Prince of Tides). A good film for teachers, he tells the story of the naive Pat Conroy, a young English teacher whose first assignment is in a primary school on a rural island. The only white man on the island, he must fight internal and external pressure when he tries to instill education and values ​​in children who have been systematically denied such things for several generations. Reliable work that really makes you think.
