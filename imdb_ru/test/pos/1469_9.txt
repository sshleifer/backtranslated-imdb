Going 'Down To South Park is a 1-hour documentary about South Park featuring interviews with Trey Parker and Matt Stone and all other people working in South Park. In fact, nothing special to say, it shows the story of South Park and what it takes to make one episode. This is mainly behind the scenes of the show. It shows the different products that South Park made (it was really the central comedy that produced it). It also shows the controversy that was caused by South Park.
