Based on a true story, John Burman shows the struggles of an American doctor whose husband and son were killed, and she constantly suffered from his loss. A holiday in Burma with her sister seemed like a good idea to get rid of all this, but when her passport was stolen in Rangoon, she could not leave the country with her sister and had to stay until she could receive health information. documents from the American Embassy. To fill the day before she could fly, she went on a trip out of town with a guide. “I tried to find something in these stone statues, but nothing moved in me. I myself was a stone. ” <br /> <br /> Suddenly, all hell collapsed, and she fell into a political uprising. At the moment when it seemed to her that she had run away and got on the train safely, she saw her guide being beaten and shot. In the blink of an eye, she decided to jump off a moving train and try to save him without thinking about herself. Constantly her life was in danger. <br /> <br /> Here is a woman who demonstrated spontaneous, selfless charity, risking her life to save another. Patricia Arquette is beautiful, and not just looking at her; she has a beautiful heart.
