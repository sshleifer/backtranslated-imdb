This is a pretty smart, well-played version of the tale of the "modern" woman of the 30s. In this case, she helps the man she loves to become head of the company, being his secretary, and ultimately wins his love from an intriguing social butterfly. Interestingly, her business sense is manifested as slightly parallel to her homemade skill, and women in the office are depicted as “powers for the throne”. He rose much above average thanks to the smart work of Mrs. Astor.
