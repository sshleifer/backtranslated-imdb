From the end of Sydney Pollack comes an adult love story about human nature, pain, passion and betrayal. Police sergeant Dutchman Van Den Brock (Harrison Ford) is devastated when he learns that his beloved wife died in a plane crash, he is even more upset after hearing about her affair with the husband of famous congresswoman Kei Chandler (Kristen Scott Thomas). He agrees to meet with her and see if she knows or not that he had no idea. They begin to be friends with each other, then because of their mutual pain and suffering, an intense affair begins (which can be regarded as healing for both of them). Does the memory of the victims and the stresses of their respective jobs sever them and cause more harm than good, or can they leave the past? Well, you have to watch "Random Hearts" and find out. Moving and well-played film with a great cast.
