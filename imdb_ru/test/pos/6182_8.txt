Gary Cooper (Michael Brandon) played the role of an American millionaire who had seven unsuccessful marriages, but he always remained divorced from his wife with a lot of money to live. Michael is in Paris on business and goes to a French department store to buy a pair of pajama tops, and sellers refuse to sell him only tops, he is forced to buy lower or no sales. Nicole DeLuazel (Claudette Colbert) listens to this conversation and offers to buy the bottom of this pajamas. Michael is very interested in Nicole, and they have the opportunity to meet and go on dates. Soon, Michael proposes marriage to Nicole, and she is very excited about his marriage request, as she really does not know him very well. However, as soon as she finds out that she is going to become the Eighth Wife of Michael, she begins to change her mind, and this story becomes quite interesting and funny. Do not miss this movie, this is a great entertainment of great veteran actors. Enjoy.
