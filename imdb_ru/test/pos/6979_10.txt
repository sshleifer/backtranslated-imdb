For avid fans of fiction, this film is exactly what you have been waiting for. Watching this movie, you get lost in the characters, especially in Riddick, the bad guy from the movie. This is the case when you support a bad guy and want to see him alive and victorious. Watching the survivors fighting for survival, you cannot wait to see who lives and how they survive in these unknown creatures that have captured this planet. A great movie, Vin Diesel did a fine job with the role of convict Riddick, and acting and suspense were amazing.
