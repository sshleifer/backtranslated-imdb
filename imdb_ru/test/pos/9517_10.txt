If you want to make a colorful review of a wonderful overview of the Byzantine history, this is for you. This documentary is also great for teachers who teach Roman or medieval history. This documentary is divided into three parts, primarily about the fall of the Western Roman Empire, the rise of Christianity and the beginning of Byzantium. The second video is devoted to Byzantine diplomacy and iconoclastic disputes. The third and last video explains the decline and fall of Byzantium. The series is filmed in several countries and perfectly integrates Byzantine history into the realities of the modern world, showing the place of this civilization as part of human civilization as a whole. Do not rush to watch.
