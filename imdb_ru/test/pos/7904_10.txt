“Independently” is a touching story about four children who run away from a foster family after the death of their mother. They have only one suitcase, their dog Ralph and $ 9, which they went to their old car in search of their uncle Jack. On the way, their car breaks down, and they meet Peggy Williams, a school teacher on vacation, and she offers to help them. Together, she and the children are looking for Uncle Jack, but are forced to hide from the authorities, who plan to return them to a foster family. This film tells the story of love, friendship and how important it is to stay with your family when the children go on their amazing journey.
