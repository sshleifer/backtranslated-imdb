This film is an absolute cinematic genius. This is a well-assembled cast that gives an almost magical performance. The effects are stunning, and the story will make you hang out of your chair throughout the entire movie. Jack Long plays the role of abbot white exceptionally well; he provides an extremely exciting image of absolute evil. If you are a Kung Fu fan or just an action movie lover, this movie will be an excellent choice for those lucky enough to find a copy. For any big Kung Fu fan, this film provides a convincing insight into the Shaolin world. This film is definitely a quality 10/10 and should be considered as one of the greatest Oriental films of all time.
