The most pleasant movie about pets since the days of Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around a 23-year-old inventor named Brian Foster, whose systems in the company of his boss seem to continue to fail, Brian also meets with the boss's daughter, Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robotic dog named CHOMPS, modeled after his own dog, Rascal. But ChOMPS is usually not a dog, it is as fast as a cheetah, it has X-ray vision, it can jump about 6 feet and has a strength of 20 people. The new invention impresses his boss and makes his business a success. But when the company's competitors hear about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to catch it. Can CHOMPS outwit them before it is too late. This film is a classic for all ages.
