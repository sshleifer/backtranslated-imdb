After watching this film, you will not only get acquainted with “Turkish music”, but also get to know the city of Istanbul with beautiful paintings and scenes from all important areas of the city. There are many delightful conversations with various musicians and their Thoughts about music and culture. There are also discussions about a mixture of East and West, like in Istanbul, how they make their music, how they see themselves compared to musicians from other countries. It consists of the music of Sesa, Duman, Baba Zuly, Ainur, Moiseyan Senar, Orhan Gensbey .. Turkish music queen Sezen Aksu ... Important work of art!
