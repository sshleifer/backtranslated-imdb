It is a pity that no one seems to know this little thriller-masterpiece. Where films with large budgets fail, Terminal Selection delivers a lot of thrills, shocks and bloody violence. A little-known gem to look for at your local video store. The anonymous guy downstairs is absolutely right when he says that you will never again trust hospitals ... this is so effective! A good ending is also not exactly a twist, but it does not end the way it was thought. Yes, this is Ellen Barkin in the early role ...
