When I first saw “Before the Night Falls,” Javier Bardem was just nominated for an Oscar. I thought "he has it!". He did not. I watched "Mar Adentro" last night. Please give it to him this time ... This is an actor. Convincing, touching, emotional, brilliant. See also “Los Lunes al-Sol” and you will understand what I am talking about. He is an absolute chameleon, and I swear that this is not only the result of makeup. This film is so beautiful, so good, the characters are so real. Congratulations to Alejandro Amenabar (how about the nomination "Best Director"?). I should also mention makeup artists. I can't think of another word besides great. I really hope this film gets the recognition it deserves. As I understand it, it already has ...
