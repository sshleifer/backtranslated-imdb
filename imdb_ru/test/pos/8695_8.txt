This film has a great premise, and he really wants to become a Hollywood blockbuster. As far as I remember (and it has been several years since I saw the movie), the action begins with a London omnibus filled with people. A terrible accident occurred, and one passenger died. The rest of the film is then recalled in the memoirs: 13 characters who were on the bus studied their last lives in tangled details. At the end of the film, we return to the catastrophe and find out which of these bright, bright characters met a terrible end. Great stuff, a bit like a good news story, thought out to the smallest detail. If it were available on video ...
