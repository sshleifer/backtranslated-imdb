In this flame of emotions, we meet several schoolchildren for one busy day at school. They all struggle with affiliation, find themselves and do not reveal their painful secrets. Has a sense of documentary maturity, focusing on the students' internal landscape and their way of solving everyday tasks. as a requiem anthem. Talluri moves the camera with great skill and slowly invites us into the minds and hearts of young people. Fictional reconstruction that enlightens us all and reminds us of the weakness of young people. One of the most important films of today. every teenager should be given the opportunity to see this touching film.
