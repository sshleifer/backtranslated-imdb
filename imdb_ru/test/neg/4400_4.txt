The situation in Palestine is fertile and still largely fallow ground for filmmaking. “Divine Intervention” is trying its best and gives us an insightful look at the almost surrealistic life of those who are in trouble, but the film is just a handful (admittedly beautiful) visual jokes thrown on celluloid, while the links between They become more and more obscure as the film progresses. Missed opportunity to say something more coherent about a very topical issue.
