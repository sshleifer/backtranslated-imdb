This film had the largest advertising campaign ever in Russia. “Epic film about Russian culture”, “The Great Saga of the Russian spirit”, endless articles and interviews. For me, this film was the biggest disappointment. The main character, performed by Oleg Menshikov, is a stupid, immature boy, ready to substitute his comrades for a woman who does not even look like a lady. What is there to admire? In the first part of the film, the story does not develop at all. The scenes of folk festivals are like boasting Russian courage. <br /> <br /> I respect Mr. Mikhalkov for his previous work as an actor and director, but this film only demonstrates his ambitions to be considered the “king” of Russian cinema,
