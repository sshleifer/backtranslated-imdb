What a waste of a great cast. I decided to check it out because it looked like a good comedy with a lot of fairly well-known actors. What turned out to be a meaningless collection of boring intertwining stories about several characters with minimal links to each other. Characters who initially look like decent people, but ultimately have no pleasant or interesting characteristics among them. Calling it a comedy was also a stretch ... the only thing that made me laugh was Jack Black's song, which was mostly Tenacious D. I was waiting for something big, but in the end I lost no more than 97 minutes of my life,
