The young man falls in love with the princess, but then must go to battle to save his father's kingdom. Far away, he accidentally kills an enchanted animal that brings a curse on him. He becomes a beast and begins to kill even his comrades. When no one returns to the kingdom from battle, the king curses the country of battle and forbids anyone from going there. One day, a rebel who wants to marry a princess decides that it is time to go to the cursed land to demand her for the king, and the king agrees when they reach the land, the king is captured by the beast, and the rebel returns home to lie to the kingdom that the king was captured and killed . He takes the throne and prepares to marry the princess, but the night before her wedding, the princess flees to the ground to go and fight the beast. Only when she gets to the cursed land will she begin to realize that her father is still alive and that the beast may not even be so evil. Unfortunately, her discoveries made her pay the final price in their revelation.
