I found this film to be an interesting study of cause and effect, but no more. The main plot tells about the life of a handful of people and how their actions (deliberate and others) affect the lives of others. The premise of the film contains great promises, but I feel that he is not keeping up with his promises. It takes too much time to tell the audience about chaos theory, and too little time to show it. As a result, I never felt a true feeling for any of the characters and never established an emotional connection with them. By the end of the film I had a “so what” attitude to all of them.
