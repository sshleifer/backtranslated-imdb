Bela Lugosi plays Dr. Lorenz, who loves his wife so much that she will do everything to make her young. This film begins with a wedding, when the bride is about to take her vows, and she suddenly falls. She is declared dead and taken away by the undertakers. The problem is that these are not real entrepreneurs, but captors of the body. The wave of deaths of the bride at the altar and the disappearance of their bodies confuse the police. Enter reporter Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She tracks down Dr. Lorenz, but he also decides to use her youth to save a young wife.
