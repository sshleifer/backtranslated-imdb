This British psychological thriller is known in the United States as CLOSURE. Exploitation of the X-Files by Jillian Anderson, who plays an attractive middle-aged businesswoman with a substance called Alice. She has to attend a business party and invites Adam (Danny Dyer), who has just installed a security system for her, to accompany her. On the way home, driving through the woods through a narrow street, Alice's car collides with a deer. Pulling a wounded animal out of the way, the couple was severely attacked by a drunken gang of thugs. Adam is beaten half to death; Alice was gang-raped, and both were emotionally and physically devastated by a ruthless attack. When the identity of their attackers revealed, Alice and Adam decided to take revenge ... cruel revenge. The couple sometimes faces disagreements on how to deal with ruthless attackers. Their final decision - revenge without mercy. Let there be no error, retribution is hell. Also starring Anthony Calf, Ralph Brown, Francesca Fowler and Anthony Byrne.
