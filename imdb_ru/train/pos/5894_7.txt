Brother's promise is a great family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, Olympic champion. The film tells about the life of this athlete from his youth to full maturity. The love and support of family members is evident everywhere. How Dan and the rest of his family cope with victory and defeat in racing is a life lesson for all of us. The dedication and determination of coach Dan and his teammates show what is needed to create a real team. The way Dan and his family deal with the destructive illness of a loved one is depicted without sentimentality or embellishment. Family faith is shown in basic terms and is obviously a major part of their life. This is a powerful family film that can be meaningful for a person of any age.
