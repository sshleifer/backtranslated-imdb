Darius Goes West is a touching story about a brave teenager who copes with Duchenne muscular dystrophy and his personal desire to see the Pacific Ocean. He gets help and support from a group of young people who love him and take care of him during this quest. <br /> <br /> In the plot there is a natural drama and an honest image of the commitment of young people to help one of them was struck by this incurable disease. <br /> <br /> Anyone who considers young people to be selfish and narcissistic will find this film to turn this stereotype upside down. It is the strength of young people and their participation in the plight of Darius that is very convincing in this documentary.
