A fun exacting caricature of trying to sell a script. The documentary is striking with all the blows, plot points, character arcs, seductions, moments of delight and disappointment and an attractive but insane prospect of selling a script or getting an agent in Hollywood, as well as all fleeting realizing fantasy, but ultimately empty rites of passage. accompanying socialization in the "system". Hotz and Rice capture the moment when you think that you are finally a player, and find out that everything goes up quickly and blindly, that for unexplained reasons, Hollywood moved on and left you to check your heart, your dreams and your pockets. Film school students need to see Pitch to get a taste of the mind and beat the ego, which for most people is the way to go, in order to sell themselves and their projects in Hollywood. If your teacher or guru has never been there, they cannot tell you what you need to prepare for this glove. To enter the "business", talent is needed, but far from sufficient
