When I first saw this animation (I was 15), I was really impressed! He has a completely different style compared to Japanese animation, and I like it more. In general, the impression of the film is more sinister and dark ... The colors are not so ... colorful. Characters do not talk much, there is no long and boring talk about that and about that (as in “Ghost in the Shell”). With his dark pictures, sorts of strange beasts and a sense of magic, he looks like one of those ancient Scandinavians. stories full of violence and horrible creators, enchanted forests and deep caves inhabiting dragons, trolls, orcs ... and one mysterious hero to stop the evil ...
