This film is played in the 60s and tells about an Italian family: Romano, his wife Rosa and their two children Gigi and Giancarlo emigrate from Solino in Italy to Duisburg in the Ruhr region. I like this film because I think it is quite realistic: it shows the problems that many foreign families face when they come to another country: they have to get used to a new culture, to a new environment, and it can be difficult: especially if you do not know the language ... It is difficult for the family, but they find a way: they open a restaurant that offers typical Italian dishes, and it is called "Solino", like their hometown. The film also shows different conflicts - Gigi and Giancarlo fall in love with the same girl, and although Rosa must work very hard, Romano refuses to pay money to attract more workers, etc. Etc.
