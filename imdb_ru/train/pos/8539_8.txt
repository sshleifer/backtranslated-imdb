A touching and sad movie. Describes the trials and tribulations of a writer trying to deal with the paralysis caused by a bicycle accident. The film tells about his relationship with his married lover, with whom he is often very hostile, and about his communication with other victims of the accident, especially with the black loser and the sucker-rider biker. The film is often humorous, often sad and always believable. Take out the box with kleenex and watch it on a cozy Sunday with your partner.
