This is the story of the Australian commandos captured in shape after a raid. Because they are out of shape, they are justly treated like spies. As such, they are tried, convicted and sentenced to death. The Japanese military tribunal, admiring their heroism, allows them the death of a warrior. Of course, according to the Bushido code, this means that they should be beheaded. Fate, to which, as residents of the West, they are not ready.
