This is a must-have documentary for anyone who fears that today's young people have lost their taste for real-life adventure and a sense of morality. Darius Goes West is an amazing rollercoaster of history. We live the lives of Darius and the teams when they go on a journey of a lifetime. Darius has Duchenne muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects all the muscles in his body. He is confined to a wheelchair and needs round-the-clock attention. So how did this crew of young friends manage to take it 6,000 miles back and forth to the west coast and back? Watch the movie and experience the great ups and downs of this great adventure - laugh and cry with the team as they cope with unimaginable trials on their way, and enjoy the ultimate triumph when they return three weeks later to their hometown for an enthusiastic reception and some great surprises !
