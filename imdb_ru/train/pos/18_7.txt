"Stanley and Iris" is a soulful film about two people who find each other and help each other overcome their life problems. Stanley's life is difficult because he never learned to read and write. Iris widow with two teenage children working in a bakery where she meets Stanley. In her free time, she decides to teach Stanley to read at home. Over time, they become romantically involved. After Stanley learns to read, he goes to a good job in Chicago, only to return to Iris and ask her to marry him. <br /> <br /> This is a really good film without nudity, violence or profanity that is rarely found in modern films.
