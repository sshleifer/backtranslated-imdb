This is an interesting true story about Archie Gray Owl, who dreamed of becoming an Indian when he was a child, up to 17 years old when he was born in England, then moved to Canada, where he was chosen by Indians, and he wrote collages in magazines, wrote a book that attracted attention of millions of people, a book about his life. But in the end he told his wife that he was not a real Indian, and she was fine, and he died at the age of 43 years two years after returning to wildness.
