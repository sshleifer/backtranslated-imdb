Merry comedy thriller in which Damian Sifron takes the life of Diaz, a police officer whose wife cheated on him. He lives in a hotel, feeling guilty because of his wife’s infidelity, falling into depression, he stops caring, lights red lights just for fun, feel sorry for himself. And Silberman, a probationary Jew, a left-wing liberal who doesn’t trust the police at all. He is ordered to accompany Diaz in his daily duties, and he cannot refuse due to the conditions of the probationary period. Soon the situation returns when Silberman himself finds out that his wife is cheating on him, and as a result he is comforted by the person he was supposed to help, the person he didn’t trust at all. Nuclear conspiracy, car thieves, international spies, hysterical wife. Strange characters in a delightful comedy about friendship and heroism.
