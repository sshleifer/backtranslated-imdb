In this magnificent story about the weekend told great literary figures. Mary and Percy Shelley, Lord Byron and others created an exciting group. Showing their quests for sexual enlightenment. Personal freedoms from political to moral. Liberal drug use for both stimulation and as a drug addiction. Their creative views on life and writing. It is not boring to describe to the viewer how each writer seeks to find his muse. Along with distractions and affections each share. With a breathtaking scenery that does not distract, but greatly enhances the story. Well-made characters from dark to loving, and then angry to peaceful. With some of the most beautiful and stage-enhancing costumes to be had.
