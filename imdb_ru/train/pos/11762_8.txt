Have you ever played sports in your life, tried your best, and then you ended up in an important segment, where for a short time you were given a chance to become a hero and a champion. , , failed? I believe that many of us have had this moment in our life. This is the premise of the movie "The Best of Times". In this story, a middle-aged banker named Jack Dundee (Robin Williams) suffers from a deep melancholy football error that occurred many years ago and inspired him to replay. again To achieve this, he must convince the once-great football quarterback Renault Hightower (Kurt Russell) to return. Renault, who is satisfied with his current position in life, sees that there is no need to change past records that get better with age. To their problem was added the fact that years had passed, and, moreover, both marriages were crumbling and in need of renewal. It is not easy when his father-in-law (Donald Moffat) usually reminds him of the biggest fall. However, Dundee is persistent and will do everything to try to correct the biggest mistake in his life. Great fun for those who want to enjoy their youth again. ***
