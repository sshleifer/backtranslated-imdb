Richard Attenborough, who has already given us great films like The Line Choir and Gandhi, once again surprises us by making a beautiful hymn to Nature. Indeed, the vast and (at that time) unexplored territory of Canada helps to compose the stunning beauty of the landscapes captured by the camera. If the film is really based on a true story, it becomes once again obvious that “people of vision”, in truth, people who live outside their time, from a historical point of view, that only Time will give them a reason. The cinematography is gorgeous, like, for example, the cast of Pierce Brosnan, whose performance is connected with Attenborough’s masterful hands. A pleasant surprise is the appearance of Annie Galipo in the role of the beloved Archie. The film, which should appear in the list of those who really loves nature ...
