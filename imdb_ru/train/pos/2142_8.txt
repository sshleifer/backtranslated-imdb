This is a very real and funny movie about a Japanese in a midlife crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting a beautiful young girl, whom he sees in the window of a dance studio, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance lessons. No one is surprised when he starts to like it than he. But he must keep his secret pleasure from his colleagues and family. When the truth comes out, it's pretty funny.
