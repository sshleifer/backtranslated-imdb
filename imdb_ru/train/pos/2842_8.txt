New York I Love You, like its predecessor (Paris Je T'Aime), is a mixture of different stories reflecting different types and aspects of love, but unlike them, the rhythm is much faster, and the stories are much shorter. The film offers a unique view of the city of New York with its diverse landscapes. New York “I Love You” is offered by a first-class cast that includes such great actors as Shea LaBeouf, Natalie Portman, Ian McKellen, Hayden Christensen, Chistina Richie and Orlando Bloom, Ethan Hawke, James Caan and Robin Wright Penn, and also some great writers. and directors like Brett Ratner and Anthony Minghella.
