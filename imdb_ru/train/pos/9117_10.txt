This film gives us an interesting reminder of how easy it is for so many to get involved in busy work when nothing is done. We, people of African descent, are obliged to change this cycle “to speak and not take any action” ourselves and to start to realize that making changes requires less talk and more action. This is a powerful statement about the disagreements of our people on minor issues, and that we could not recognize the big picture and the need to unite in order to make a difference ... Despite mentioning the black community, all viewers can learn something from the message to portray.
