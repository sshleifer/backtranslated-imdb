Four teenage girlfriends go to Fort Lordale for spring break. Unfortunately, they get a flat tire in Madeley, Georgia, and one of the girls witnesses a brutal murder in the depths of the forest. The local sheriff is behind the crime, and the nightmare begins ... "" The Small Grave "is a pleasant low-budget surprise. The cast is pretty enough, the direction is steady, and the violence is especially vile and misogynist. Especially the second murder is rather gloomy. The killer sheriff is not a one-dimensional character. It seems that in several scenes he feels remorse for what he has done. The plot, which involves two boys, whom they meet in a diner, is not going anywhere, but the scenes of persecution in the forest are tense and exciting. 7 out of 10.
