This film is the next segment in Pokemon films, which tells about the hopes and dreams of a Pokemon warrior named Ash Ketchim and his friends. they go out and watch the battle, face new pokemons and take on new adventures with Pikachu and other Pokemon favorites. This adventure begins with a new Pokémon named Selebi, a temporary Pokémon. Join Ash Brock and Misty to find all sorts of new things!
