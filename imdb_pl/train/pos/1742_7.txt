Perhaps one of the most important and most enjoyable Greek movies that I've seen in the last ten years. Excellent performances (especially yiannis zouganelis is great), well written script and effective direction from very exceptional, Greek very medium standards, auteur. The film, obviously influenced by Sam Peckinpah's Straw Dogs, which can be a masterpiece if it eventually avoids certain evident and exaggerated situations and symbols. Nevertheless, it is a film that deserves our attention and belongs to a rare category of Greek films that should be watched outside of Greece. It is a pity that in Greece it does not operate commercially, in addition to other fake and cursory big productions, such as Politiki Kouzina ..
