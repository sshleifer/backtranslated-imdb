This movie provides us with an interesting reminder of how easy it is to get so many caught up in a busy profession that it does nothing. We, as people of African descent, owe it to ourselves to change this cycle of "all conversations and no actions" and to start realizing that to make a change, we need less talk and more action. This is a powerful statement of the division of our nation into minor problems, as well as our shortcomings in recognizing a larger image and the need to unite to change something ... Despite the reference to the black community, all viewers can learn something from the message that this film tries to present .
