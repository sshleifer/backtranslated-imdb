This is a very real and funny movie about a Japanese having a midlife crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting the beautiful young girl she sees in the dance studio window, she is suddenly in dance classes. No one is more surprised when he starts to like him than he does. But he must keep secret pleasure from his colleagues and family. When the truth comes out, it's quite funny.
