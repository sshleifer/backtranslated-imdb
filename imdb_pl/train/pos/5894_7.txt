A brother's promise is a great family movie. This is the biography of Dan Jansen, the Olympic speed skater champion. The film presents the life of this athlete from an early age to full adulthood. The love and support of family members are visible all the time. As Dan and the rest of his family cope with winning and losing races is a lesson of life for all of us. The commitment and determination of coach Dan and his teammates show what needs to be done to create a real team. As Dan and his family cope with the debilitating disease of a loved one, he presents himself without retreating fondness or sugar coating. The faith of the family is shown in the basic categories and is of course the main part of their lives. It is a powerful family movie that can make a difference to people of all ages.
