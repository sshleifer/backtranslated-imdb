A great documentary about the life of firefighters from New York during the worst terrorist attack of all time. That's why this reason should be an obligatory collector's item. What shocked me is not only the attacks, but the "high fat diet" physical appearance of some of these firefighters. I think many physicians would agree with me that in the physical shape in which they found themselves, some of these firefighters would NOT reach the 79th floor, carrying more than 60 kg of equipment. Having said that I now have more respect for firefighters and I realize that becoming a firefighter is a life-changing work. The French have a story of making great documentaries and that's how it is, the Great Document ...
