Interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, laziness and mammoth, who saved a child who was close to death by a group of tigers during the Ice Age. The movie's morale shows that good behavior with others can bring benefits at the end. One of the tigers in the group got the order to finally catch a child whose mother did not save when the tigers attacked her community. The child was saved by laziness and mammoth, but the tiger joined them to finally take the child. They passed through very embarrassing paths with a great deal of danger, and the tiger immediately collapsed and was saved by a mammoth. At the end, a group of tigers tried to capture the child, but the mammoth, which was helped by his tiger friend, was able to overcome this attack and give the child to the father and the community to which he belongs.
