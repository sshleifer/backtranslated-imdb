Two important scenes illustrate Tony's dilemma. The first of them takes place in the office of the producer Max Lasker. Acting is a matter of talent for practical Lasker. But Donlan, Tony's friend, disagrees: "No, no. When you do it the way Tony does it, it's much more. The way you want to be someone else every night ... so completely. the whole system is not affected. "... Another scene occurs in the apartment of waitress Pat. Tony tells her that his name is Martin. She thanks him. Then he says: "Or Paul. Hamlet. Joe. Or maybe Othello. "... When Tony starts practicing Othello, we find out that although he is trying to separate his real life from the stage life," Part begins to penetrate into your life and the battle begins Reality against your imagination.
