It is a documentary film obligatory for anyone who fears that modern youth have lost the taste of real adventure and its sense of morality. Darius Goes West is an amazing roller coaster from history. We live the lives of Darius and the crew when they embark on the journey of their lives. Darius has Duchenne muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects all of his body muscles. He is limited to a wheelchair and needs 24-hour attention. So how did this team of young friends manage to take it for 6,000 miles on both sides of the West Coast and back? Watch the movie and experience the ups and downs of this great adventure - laugh and cry with the crew that can handle the unimaginable challenges along the way and enjoy the final triumph when they return to their hometown three weeks later in an enthusiastic welcome and some great surprises!
