Russ and Valerie talk about starting a family. The couple lives in an elegant apartment and runs an auction company that deals with valuable collector items. At the same time, the owner of a dedicated adoption agency takes a mini vacation and leaves the orphanage under the care of his father (Leslie Nielsen). Father Harry is in the rental department and gets a brilliant idea to "rent" some children from the orphanage to couples like Russ and Valerie. Harry, who is aware of the couple's dilemma, offers his siblings a 10-day rental period! Brandon, Kyle and Molly move into the apartment with their temporary parents, with funny consequences, because the new guardians have no experience with children. But where is the possibility of a happy ending? It's a beloved family movie. Actors, including Nielsen as a wheel dealer and Christopher Lloyd as a nice porter in the apartment, are great. The script is lively and funny, and the overall production value is quite high. Yes, if only life can be this way! Orphaned children all over the world deserve a chance to prove they are loved and can give so much joy to parents who are considering adoption. If you want to show your family a movie that is rooted in good values ​​but is also very funny, find this movie. It guarantees that everyone will laugh, even when their hearts are melting.
