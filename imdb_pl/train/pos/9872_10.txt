Once again, two quarrelsome professors must join to save the lost world. Five members of the first expedition will return (see The Lost World, 1992, list of actors). An oil-seeking man takes a drilling crew to the plateau. Instead of hitting oil, they tap into an underground volcano that threatens all life in the Lost World. The oil crew clashes with the indigenous people and the scientific expedition. Although the situation looks hopeless ... (I will not tell you the end).
