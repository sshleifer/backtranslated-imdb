This is the story of Australian commandos who were captured from the uniform after the air raid. Because they do not have a uniform, they are rightly treated as spies. As such, they are tried, condemned and sentenced to death. The Japanese court martial, admiring their heroism, authorizes them to the death of a warrior. Of course, according to the Bushido Code, it means that they are to be beheaded. Fate, for which Westerners are unprepared.
