This was the black society in front of the crack epidemic, gangsta rap and AIDS that hit the ghettos in the eighties. Decent, hardworking families who fought for survival and all the traumas and torments that they struggled with. Black America was a different group of people in the seventies. Still full of hope and flying high on the civil rights movements of the sixties, times were difficult, but it is still worth fighting for. Holding his head above the water, making a wave when you can, this show showed how black society was struggling to work together as people and families before they started feeding on each other and everything else to survive the horrors of the ghettos. It is crucial to see what the black ghettos were like and what they have become.
