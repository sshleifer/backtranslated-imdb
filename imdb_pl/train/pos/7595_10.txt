Lost is not the best television program ever, but it is not far away. There is no plot or characteristic of The West Wing, or maybe even early ER, but this is probably the most thrilling program I've ever seen. I love the way I can not guess what will happen. I love telling stories of heroes, which often give rise to new dimensions in which we can see them. In some respects, I want the show to last forever, but I think they can get six to seven seasons before they have to end it gloriously. The combination of characters and their nationalities combined with the liquidity of the series to move back and forth, thus prolonging the dead characters "life expectancy", all contribute to the overwhelming sense that this performance is something completely different than that what we are used to. It is captivating, surprising and (here is a small suggestion for all of you, conspiracy theorists) more than a bit interactive - keep these internet discussions - you only add to the story ...
