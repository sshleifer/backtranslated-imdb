Creative use of modern and mystical elements: Cadillac convertible from 1956 to transport the evil stepmother Kathleen Turner ("The Mom Series" by John Waters) and 2 twisted sisters; Queen Mab as the faerie godmother; David Warner (Evil in Time Bandits) in a redcoat outside; Cinderella (she is a child) pushes coal to an unsaturated furnace; Cinderella and her charming prince look like rock stars.
