What happened to Michael Keaton? What a great actor and he proved it in this film. This movie is really FUNNY! And the reason why this movie is fun is for two reasons: a great script and Michael Keaton. This movie is one of the funniest comedies in Hollywood history. This film is the ultimate parody of gangster movies. In this movie, Hollywood really has a great time using the gangster film genre as the basis for a truly original comedy. The rest of the cast is also fun, especially supporting the cast. If you like to laugh and you want to watch a movie that contains constant humor, this movie is for you.
