Rachel Griffiths writes and directs an award-winning short film. A warming story of struggling with sadness and nurturing the memory of those whom we loved and lost. Although only 15 minutes, Griffiths manages to capture so many emotions and truths in a short time. Bud Tingwell gives a touching performance as Will, a widower struggling with his wife's death. Will faces a difficult reality of loneliness and helplessness when dealing with Ruth's pet, Tulip. The film shows the sadness and responsibility that is felt for those whom they loved and lost. Good cinematography, great direction and excellent game. It will bring tears to all who lost their loved one and survived.
