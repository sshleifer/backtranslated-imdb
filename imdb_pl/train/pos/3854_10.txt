As for Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think about "Basic Instinct" and some of the "Showgirls". But Verhoeven created his best erotic thriller years before these two films: "De Vierde Man". This film is mesmerizing and amazing - and above all, the story is absolutely reliable, which makes the whole experience even more intense. The performances of Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thoma Hoffman are exceptional, and Verhoevena's direction is doing the rest. "De Vierde Man" makes even "Basic Instinct" look pretty tame ... It can not be much better than this one, a real classic of an erotic nightmare. 10 out of 10, at least ...
