A hilarious caricature trying to sell a script. The documentary hits all the rhythms, plot points, character arcs, seduction, moments of elation and disappointment, as well as the charming but crazy prospect of selling the script or getting an agent in Hollywood, as well as all fleeting, fanciful but ultimately empty transition rituals accompanying socialization in the "system" . Hotz and Rice captured the moment of thinking that you are ultimately a player, just to discover that what happens is falling fast and blindly, that for inexplicable reasons Hollywood has moved and checked your heart, your dreams and pockets. Pitch is a must-have program for film school students who want to try their mind and hit the ego, which is for most of the way you need to travel to sell yourself and your Hollywood projects. If your teacher or guru has never been there, they can not tell you what you need to prepare for this gantlet. To enter the "biz", talent is necessary, but far from sufficient
