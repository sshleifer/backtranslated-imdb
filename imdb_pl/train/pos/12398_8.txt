What was always missing in the Matrix history was how things began to appear in the real world. Do not say more because this part of the story covered most of the bases. It was interesting how much political and maybe even cheap shots were there for the current presidential administration. Fascism and violence were the only things that a man could come up with regarding the fight against a horde of robots who were nothing more than servants of humanity. It was also interesting to use fear and consolidate it by the idea of ​​the unknown. We, people, often fall into this trap, letting the lack of logic and thought overtake us, because people can not believe the opposite. Well represented and connected, it is a real testimony of how illogical people can be.
