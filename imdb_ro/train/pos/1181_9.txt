Rachel Griffiths writes and directs this award-winning short film. An unimaginable story about coping with pain and cherishing the memory of those we loved and lost. Although, for only 15 minutes, Griffiths manages to surprise so much emotion and truth on the film in the short run. Bud Tingwell gives an emotional interpretation to Will, a widow who struggles to face his wife's death. Will faces the harsh reality of loneliness and helplessness as he continues to take care of Ruth, Tulip's cow. The film shows the pain and the responsibility I feel for those I love and lose. Good cinematography, great direction and superb action. It brings tears to all those who have lost their lover and have survived.
