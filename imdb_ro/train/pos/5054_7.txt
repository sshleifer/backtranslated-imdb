Richard Attenborough, who has already given us some magnificent films like "The Choir Line" and "Gandhi," surprised us once again to make a beautiful hymn for Nature. Indeed, the vast and unexplored territory of Canada contributes to the amazing beauty of the landscapes taken over by the video camera. If the film really relies on a true story, it becomes once again obvious that "viewers" are in fact people who live beyond their time, with a historical perspective that only time will give reason. Cinematography is magnificent, like Pierce Brosnan's performance due to Attenborough's hands. A pleasant surprise is the appearance of Annie Galipeau as Archie's lover. The film that must appear in a list of those who really love Nature ...
