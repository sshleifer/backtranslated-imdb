Very good horror film featuring the biggest, Boris Karloff and Bela Lugosi. Dr. Janos Rukh (Karloff) is on an expedition in Africa, trying to find an ancient meteorite. Once he finds it, Rukh is poisoned by his radiation. Everything that touches dies, and Ruk's dark side makes him become a selfish killer. His friend, Dr. Felix Benet (Lugosi) finds a limited remedy for this problem and at the same time realizes that radiation could be used for the good of mankind by curing diseases. The two beings will fight for the possibilities of radiation. Special effects quite good. Others in the cast: Frances Drake, Frank Lawton, Beulah Bondi and Frank Reicher.
