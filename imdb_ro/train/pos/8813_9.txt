Although the pieces are uneven, this collection of 11 short films is truly a moving and human experience. There were some who, after their bombardment anniversary emotions, considered this anti-American. I do not think that is the case, even if some parts could be taken this way if you do not look behind obviously. Finally, the film is nothing more than an attempt by people to express their confusion, sympathy, and feelings about what has happened. These are the stories of the people that the world has been shaken by what happened on a Tuesday in September. As I said, this movie will move you, perhaps to tears. It's not always easy to watch, for example, the film in Mexico is a bit more than a black screen with sound, but its effect is such as to put even the strongest of the little ones. If you can be strong, you should see this movie. It will comfort you and light you and it will affect you ...
