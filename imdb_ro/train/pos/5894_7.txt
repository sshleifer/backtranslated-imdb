The Promise of Brother is a wonderful family movie. This is a biography of Dan Jansen, an Olympic champion speed skater. The film describes the life of the athlete from an early age to adulthood. The love and support of family members are obvious throughout their lives. How Dan and the rest of his family are in charge of winning and losing races is a life lesson for all of us. The commitment and determination of Dan's coach and teammates shows what it means to make a real team. How Dan and his family face a devastating illness of a boyfriend is described without avenging the sentiment or sugar coverage. Family faith is presented in basic terms and is obviously an important part of their lives. This is a powerful family film that can be meaningful to a person of any age.
