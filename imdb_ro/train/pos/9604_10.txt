"Darius Goes West" is the exciting story of a brave teenager who is experiencing Duchenne's muscular dystrophy and his personal quest to see the Pacific Ocean. He receives help and encouragement from a group of young people who love and care for him as he goes to this search. The story has a natural drama and an honest portrayal of the commitment of young people to help one of their own affected by this incurable disease. Anyone who believes that young people are self-centered and narcissistic will find this film to turn the stereotype on their heads. The power of the young people and their involvement in Darius's situation is very convincing in this documentary.
