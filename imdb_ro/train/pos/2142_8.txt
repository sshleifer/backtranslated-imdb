This is a very real and funny film about a Japanese man who has a mid-life crisis. In Japan, dance in the ballroom is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting the beautiful young woman he sees in the window of a dance studio, he suddenly wakes up enrolled in dance classes. No one is more surprised when he starts to like him than he is. But he has to keep his secret pleasure from his colleagues and his family. When the truth comes, it's pretty funny.
