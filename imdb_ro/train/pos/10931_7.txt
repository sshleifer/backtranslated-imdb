There is no romance or other side plot for this movie, it's action and intrigue all the way, making it a real movie of a kung-fu man. An elderly master sends his last disciple, Yan Tieh, the former pupils who are styles are five centipede venomous animals, snake, scorpion, lizard and frog. Despite the word "Venom" in the title, none of these children use venoms to kill their opponents. Yan Tieh said by his teacher that he does not match the five former students, he must find one in which he can form an alliance to defeat the other four. How to find Yan Tieh and the others is an intrigue in the story, with good action kung-fu spread throughout the story. Recognized as a classic cult, this film has already been established in the annals of kung fu action films. It is well known that other films refer to the five styles described in this story. It's not an artistic masterpiece, with the usual doubling of evil and a horn action, but the film is one of the best because it is so focused on all the components of the kung-fu action film of its time and delivers a further concentrated dose of these. A movie you have to watch if you are a kung fu film fan.
