Steven Rea plays the role of a forensic scientist serving Soviet Russia in 1982. In the first hours of his job a body of a girl is killed ... his workers return to look for evidence and bring them back five more bodies. This begins the story of hunting for one of the worst serial killers in modern day history. It is a dark, depressing film exploring how bureaucracy in the old Soviet Union has indirectly contributed or caused the deaths of many of the killers' victims. It also explores the character of Donald Sutherland in how the correct use of bureaucracy in -a Communist government can help reach the ultimate goal of finding a monster An exciting movie not for all but for those who like a good detective story that holds the interest you need to see on a scale from one At ten
