If you think "Weird Al" Yankovic is hilarious, you will not be disappointed by COMPLEAT AL. Not only does this mockumentar rarely feature many of Yankovic's most memorable videos ("As A Surgeon" and "I Love Rocky Road"), but they are intertwined with funny vignettes, which obviously highlight the appearance of the parodist to fame . Yankovic is not for all tastes, but his humor is harmless and imaginative enough that even non-fans will be at least slightly amused. Eager farmers will enjoy not only its content but also its relatively early appearance in Yankovic's career for almost three decades. Suitable for all ages, kids will undoubtedly love funny visuals.
