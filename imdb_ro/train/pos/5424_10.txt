"Gunga Din": one of the greatest adventure stories ever told! A story about the 19th century British foreign legion in India and a "bearer of water" called Gunga Din, a locals who aspire to be like his military counterparts; three British sergeants whose loyalty and camaraderie extend far beyond the boundaries of simple patriotism. Theirs is a true and persevering friendship for each other and everyone is willing to sacrifice their own lives for the good of the other. Gunga Din wants to be a soldier, especially a Bugler, but he can not reach this rank because of his subordinate social status. However, heroes are not made in accordance with their social credentials, but by their desire to sacrifice themselves for the good of others. Gunga Din tries every turn to prove his boldness, but he will ever reach the rank he is looking for so passionate ... "You are a better man than me, Gunga Din"! One of the classic Hollywood and a perfect 10 !!!!
