Russ and Valerie have talks about starting a family. The couple live in a pleasant apartment and run an auction business that deals with valuable collections. At the same time, a dedicated adoption agency owner has a wonderful vacation and leaves the orphanage to his father (Leslie Nielsen). Father Harry is in the rental business and receives the brilliant idea of ​​"renting" some of the children of couples' orphanages such as Russ and Valerie. Harry, who becomes aware of the couple's dilemma, offers a family of brothers for a ten-day tenancy! Brandon, Kyle and Molly move to the apartment with their temporary parents, with funny consequences, because new caregivers are not experienced with children. But where is the possibility of a happy ending? This is a dear family movie. The actors, including Nielsen as a motorcycle dealer and Christopher Lloyd as a luxury goalkeeper, are great. The scenario is fast and fun, and global production values ​​are quite high. Yes, if only life could be so! Orphaned children everywhere deserve a chance to prove they are lovers and can offer so much joy to parents who are thinking about adoption. If you want to show a movie to your family that has roots in good value, but it's also very fun, find this movie. It is guaranteed that everyone laughs, even if their heart melts.
