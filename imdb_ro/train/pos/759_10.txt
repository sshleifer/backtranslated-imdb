For many, Beat Street has inspired his lifestyle to something creative about hip-hop culture. Young Lee lives in NY in the 1980s, when hip hop was at the beginning. Member of his crew in "Beat Street" - crew of b-boys. The film follows Lee on his average day, dancing, graffiti, etc. The director managed to make a film with a plot and at the same time to present hip-hop to the rest of the world. The film has old school features such as Afrika Bambaataa & Sonic Soul Force, Grand Master Melle Mel and Furious Five, Rock Steady Crew, Breakers in New York City and many more. <br /> <br /> Beat Street, and the Beat Street spirit will never die.
