"Europe" (or, as is known, "Zentropa") is one of the most amazing movies I've ever seen. The blend of shades of gray and color is almost no problem ... a true eye feast. The image was a contestant for a Canadian Golden Palm from 1991. The award was given to Barton Fink (Coen Brothers); a stylistic film very similar to Zentropa. Here's an exercise in the classroom: rent both movies and be a judge for you.
