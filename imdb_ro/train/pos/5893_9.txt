Story beautiful native of Wisconsin, Dan Jansen, and his life real, agonizing fight to win the 1994 Olympic gold medal in speed skating, despite its overwhelming emotional loss with the death of beloved friend and family member; his sister, Jane. The main priority of this story is to reflect the sensitive life of suffering emotional pain that feels to lose a loved one around, and struggle to cope productive and to rise above the great loss! It is the incredible story of suffering Dan Jansen losing beloved sister to leukemia, struggle to cope with the intensity of his pain, while still retains the dream of winning the Olympics and triumph final to win medal Gold in Speed ​​Skating for America and in honor of his sister's memory; thus fulfilling the promise of his childhood to Jane! After the strong performance of gold medal of Dan Jansen in the Winter Olympics in Lillihamer, Norway, he established a foundation in 1994 to help fight leukemia, which claimed the life of his sister, and to support sports programs for youth, educational prizes and scholarships. Dan Jansen Foundation promotes philosophy, "as his father always told him from an early age," there is something life than skating in circles! "Maintaining a proper perspective is essential! It is also setting goals and achieving dreams with perseverance, overcoming adversity and abandonment!" <br /> <br /> Recommended for anyone who has ever suffered a great loss of someone very special and dear loved in your life! And for anyone who still thinks about dreaming and never did!
