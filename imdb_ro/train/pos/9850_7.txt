An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a lazy and a mammoth who saved a child who was almost killed by a group of tigers during the frozen era. The morale of the film shows that good behavior with others can bring benefits at the end. One of the group's tigers was ordered to finally capture the child, which was hardly rescued by his mother when the tigers attacked her community. The child was then rescued from laziness and mammoth, but the tiger joined with the goal of eventually taking the baby. They went through very disturbing ways, with great distress, and immediately the tiger fell and was saved by the mammoth. At the end, the group of tigers tried to capture the child, but the mammoth helped incredibly with his tiger colleague that he managed to overcome this attack and give the child back to his father and the community he is part of.
