"Fido" must be appreciated for taking a tired genre, zombies and turning it into an original cinematic experience. The early 50s atmosphere is amazing, spectacular, and the whole production has a very careful planning. Suddenly the viewer is immersed in a world of beautiful classic cars, the "Eisenhower era" dress, art deco furniture and zombie servants. It would be very easy to quit Fido as a cartoon puff, similar to "Tank Girl," but the two films are very different. "Fido has a structure, a script that tells a story and action that is superior. It does not make any mistake, it's a bold comedy, which succeeds when many others have failed.
