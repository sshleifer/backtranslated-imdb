This is probably one of the worst murderous killer movies I've ever seen. It follows the typical premise of a laboratory experiment that went wrong and a giant crocodile with a fast-growing chemical in it escapes. The monster looks a lot like a dinosaur, with Tyranosaurous back legs, when it should look like a big crocodile. Everything about this movie is non-original and constantly an oasis on "cliche," minute after minute. Why are there always two drunken red hunters who separate after dark? In addition, there is always a guy and a girl who share a lame, an obvious interest in love while in life-threatening situations. Many things have already been said by me and I feel as if I miss the time to write this ...
