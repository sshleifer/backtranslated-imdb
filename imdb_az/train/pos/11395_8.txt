I got some old music from my garage and this is nothing but my favorite. Written by Jay Alan Lerner and managed by Vincent Minelli. He won two Oscars for Best Picture and Best Screenplay of 1951. The story of an American artist in Paris is trying to make it bigger. Nina Foch is an advanced lady and is very interested in helping her, but soon she finds out she loves the boy. Meanwhile, Gene Kelly falls for her boyfriend Leslie Caron. His main dance fan and I have to say that they are fantastic somewhere and somewhere. Famous French singer Georges Guietary also reads. So, if you want good slick dances and full of entertaining scenes with Oscar Levant's bare piano fingers, George Gershwin's songs will live forever on this colorful ship. 8/10
