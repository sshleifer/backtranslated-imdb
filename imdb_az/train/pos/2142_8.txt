This is a very realistic and funny movie about a Japanese man with an average age crisis. Ballroom dances in Japan are not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama gets acquainted with the beautiful young girl he sees in the dance studio, she suddenly goes to dance classes. No one is more surprised when he begins to resemble it. But you have to keep your curiosity hidden from her employees and your family. The truth is very funny when it comes out.
