MELTDOWN Very interesting SCI-FI No major budget, very few special effects; But decent acting and worldwide ruins are enough to survive watching a story. An asteroid grazes the atmosphere and emphasizes the earth in an orbit around the sun. Global warming quickly becomes intolerable. A determined LAPD Police (Kaspershire Van Deeen) is going all out to save the world from certain ruins as rising temps are devastating. There is pressure to save mankind from this solar disaster; As well as protecting her daughter, nurse ex-wife and TV reporter girlfriend. Cast includes: Stefani von Pfetten, Venus Tezo, Amanda Cruz and Vincent Gould.
