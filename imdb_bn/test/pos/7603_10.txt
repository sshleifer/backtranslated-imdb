This movie may appear superficially flaky and for teenie-boppers. It seems to be a stereotypical high school movie. But in fact, it takes a honest and sensitive look at high school experience. It does not offer some unrealistic completion where Geek and popular kids meet. It does not change what happened in teenage years; It just tries to calculate with it. This is a really cool, fun, heart-warming movie, good for teens and for those who can still remember ...
