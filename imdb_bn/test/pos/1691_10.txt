One of the best films directed by Andrzej Wajda, the story tells of the young writer, who seeks his position after the second war (he lives in the German camp). Excellent true environment (action goes to camps in transfers), the main protagonist (best Polish actor Daniel Allbricksky plays one) finally falls in love, but unfortunately his lady is killed. There was a beautiful scene while talking to the American soldier and saying (about her daughter's death) "Nothing is happening, only you are shooting for us now ... her soul's condition has been destroyed. 10/10
