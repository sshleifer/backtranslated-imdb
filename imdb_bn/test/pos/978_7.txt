This movie was fun! Especially if you are between 8 to 15 years. Frankie Muniz and Amanda Bains bring a credible team of 8th graders back to an adult who is really worthy of it. Paul Giamatti makes a great adult you really want to 'get'. My 13 year old girl and her friend liked it. Parents do not have to worry about a bad word or a gender. Recommend it for the 'right' crowd.
