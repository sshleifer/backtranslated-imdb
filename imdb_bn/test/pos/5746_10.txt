A young man discovers that life is valuable after being seriously injured in a road crash. His father is a pastor who has changed his attitude towards life and he spent the whole film sharing new love for others in this frustrating and heartfelt film. The theme is built around a street car racing and it is a great film that includes movies like 'Fast and Furious'. It shows how people can overcome their problems and give general messages about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
