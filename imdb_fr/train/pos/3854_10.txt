As for Paul Verhoeven and the erotic thrillers, most people think of "Basic Instinct" and sometimes even "Showgirls". But Verhoeven realized his best erotic thriller long before these two films: "De Vierde Man". This film is mesmerizing and breathtaking - and above all, the story is absolutely plausible, which makes the whole thing even more intense. The performances of Jeroen Krabbe, Renee Soutendijk and Thom Hoffman are exceptional and the Verhoeven management is doing the rest. "De Vierde Man" gives a rather tame look to "Basic Instinct" ... It can not be better than that, a true classic nightmarish erotic cinema. 10 out of 10, at least ...
