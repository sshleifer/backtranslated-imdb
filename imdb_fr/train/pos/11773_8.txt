A big cartoon of Bugs Bunny from previous years presents Bugs as an interpreter in a showcase of a local department store. Once he has finished his day, the manager comes to tell him that he will make a transfer soon. Bugs is happy to make it clear that his new job is taxidermy ... and that taxidermy is about stuffing animals. Animals like, for example, a certain rabbit. This causes a battle of wits between the rogue rabbit and his former employer. I found this short film delicious and certainly one of the best in the early 1940s. It's still as funny almost 60 years later. This animated short film can be seen on Looney Tunes Golden Gold 2, Volume 2. <br /> <br /> My note: A-
