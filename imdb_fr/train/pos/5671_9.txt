THE INVADERS IS A SCI-FI THRILLER MOVING RAPIDLY WITH BEN CROSS AND SEAN YOUNG. BEN PLAYS RENN, TRAVELER OF ANOTHER GALAXY THAT TRY TO FIND ANNIE (PLAYED BY SEAN), WHO IS PHYSICALLY IDEAL TO HAVE HIS CHILD. THIS CHILD, IF AUTHORIZED TO BE BORN AND RETURN TO HER PLANET, MAY BE THE ONLY CHANCE TO EXIT FROM HIS RACE. THE ENEMY, A FOREIGNER WHO DESTROYED THE PEOPLE OF RENN, ALSO FOLLOWED RENN THROUGH THE STARS TO STOP THIS BIRTH. CROSS AGAIN INDICATES A GREAT DISTRIBUTION OF COMIC BEGINNINGS ALIEN ENTERS A LOCAL BAR AND COMMANDS ITS FIRST COCKTAIL TO THE EARTH, TO HIS RACE WITH THE ENEMY AND HEAD OF KEEPING ANNIE AND HIS CHILD.
