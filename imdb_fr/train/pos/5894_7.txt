A Brother's Promise is a great family film. This is the biography of Dan Jansen, Olympic speed skating champion. The film describes the life of this athlete from young age to adulthood. The love and support of family members is obvious. The way Dan and the rest of his family manage victories and defeats is a lesson in life for all of us. The commitment and determination of Dan's coach and his teammates show what it takes to make a real team. The way Dan and his family manage the devastating illness of a loved one is portrayed with no sense of damage or camouflage. The faith of the family is expressed in fundamental terms and is obviously an important part of their lives. It's a powerful family film that can make sense for anyone of any age.
