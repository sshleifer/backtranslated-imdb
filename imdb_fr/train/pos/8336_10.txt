This film tells the story of a love story between Albert Einstien's niece and a pump attendant. In order to bring the two together, Einstien agrees to help Ed (Tim Robbins of Hudsucker Proxy) learn to act smarter. This impresses Catherine (Meg Ryan). Unfortunately, Einstien goes too far and Ed is considered a genius. Hilarity ensues. Do not miss. Filmed in Mercer County, New Jersey, Princeton University, Lawrenceville Prep School (doubling for Princeton University) and a beautiful vintage gas station in Hopewell.
