Revenge is the theme of this Denzel Washington thriller that offers its share of action, chaos, murder and appalling bloodshed. The essential is the search and destruction mission of a bodyguard. The young Dakota Fanning, whose load is his responsibility, is removed from school. This is exactly what Washington has been hired to prevent. The locations in Mexico City are as chaotic as the plot advances toward its predictable and violent conclusion, with twists along the way. Washington, a former CIA agent with a drinking problem, receives a good referral from a former agent, who sets out the outline of the conspiracy. Washington and Fanning have great chemistry between them and after a difficult start, the bodyguard and his charge become the best friends in the world. Christopher Walken, Rachel Ticotin, Radha Mitchell, Giancarlo Giannini and Mickey Rourke are part of the good cast in support of the two stars.
