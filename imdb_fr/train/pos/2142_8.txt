It's a very real and funny movie about a Japanese man in a mid-life crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing is not approved. But when Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting the beautiful girl he sees at the window of a dance studio, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance classes. Nobody is surprised when he starts to like it. But he must keep his secret pleasure with his colleagues and his family. When the truth is revealed, it's pretty funny.
