When you find that a video game is scary or disturbing, you know that the developers have done a very serious and difficult job to make it all work. Undying used the Unreal engine, but had very little resemblance to this game with respect to the actual gameplay. Speaking of gameplay, the pace is slow and the simple difficulty of progressing through the very difficult enemies to kill makes the gaming experience very mediocre. The production values ​​are so high that you can even forget that this is a video game. The game itself is also filled with secrets that you must discover using a special vision. The level design in fantastic and the weapons as well as the enemies will really shock you.
