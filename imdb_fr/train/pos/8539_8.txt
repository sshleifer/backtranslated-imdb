Touching and sad film. Describes the hardships and tribulations of a writer trying to overcome paralysis caused by a bicycle accident. The film focuses on his relationship with his married lover, who is often very hostile, and his interactions with other accident victims, particularly a black biker and a supremacist biker. The film is often humorous, often sad and always credible. Take out the box of Kleenex and watch it on a Sunday afternoon with your partner.
