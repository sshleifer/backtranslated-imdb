Rachel Griffiths writes and directs this award-winning short film. A heartwarming story about managing grief and preserving the memory of those we loved and lost. Although Griffiths only has 15 minutes, he manages to capture a lot of emotion and truth about a movie in a short time. Bud Tingwell gives a touching performance as Will, a widower struggling to cope with the death of his wife. Will is confronted with the harsh reality of loneliness and helplessness as he cares for Ruth's domestic cow, Tulip. The film shows the sorrow and responsibility that we feel for those they loved and lost. Good cinematography, good direction and superbly played. It will make everyone who has lost a loved one and who has survived cry.
