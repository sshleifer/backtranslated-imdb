"Europa" (or, as it is also known as "Zentropa") is one of the most breathtaking films I have ever seen. The mix of gray levels and colors is almost seamless ... a real treat for the eyes. The photo was a Canners Golden Palm candidate in 1991. The award was given to Barton Fink (Coen brothers); a film stylistically very similar to Zentropa. Here's an exercise in class: rent both movies and be a judge for yourself.
