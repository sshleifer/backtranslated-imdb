Credited by Variety as one of the greatest documentaries ever made in Canada. Al Pacino, Roger Ebert, Neil Simon, Matt Dillon and countless celebrities make this movie a Canadian classic. The movie is really better described as "Roger & Me" meets "The Player". Watch as Kenny Hotz and Spenny Pitch their screenplay for the big boys of Hollywood. Called the only American film ever to have left Canada, this film opened the Toronto Film Festival in 1997 and won the "Best Independent Film" award in Toronto. The European premiere was at the prestigious HOF film festival in Germany. First American American comedy festival, Aspen, 1999. More information on www.kennyhotz.com
