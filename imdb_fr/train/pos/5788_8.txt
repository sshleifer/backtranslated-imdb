This episode seems to be born of the cold war. There was a holocaust, but Elizabeth Montgomery and Charles Bronson managed to escape. This then becomes a battle for turf. She is drawn to him and vice versa, but the survival instinct takes over. It is a silent and slow chess battle that tries to gain confidence. They come to truce but distrust takes over and they start again. Of course, the female feminine role of the sixties comes into play and modern viewers might find that her need to follow him is a little offensively. But it's always captivating and interesting. Because she does not speak, we do not know very well here, but in the end we can guess.
