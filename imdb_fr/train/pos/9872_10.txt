Once again, the two bickering teachers must work together to save the lost world. The five members of the first expedition return (see The Lost World, 1992, for a list of actors). A man in search of oil brings a drilling team on the board. Instead of hitting the oil, they exploit an underground volcano that threatens all life in the lost world. The oil crew meets the natives and the scientific expedition. Although the situation seems hopeless ... (I will not tell you the end).
