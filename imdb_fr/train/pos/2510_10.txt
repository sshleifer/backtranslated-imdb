Moon Child tells the story of two brothers and a friend trying to survive in a futuristic and economically unstable Japan. After a sinister trick that went wrong, a new person enters the life of Sho, a special friend called Kei. Years later, they came together and found a way to combine their talents into one team. During another escapade, they meet a new friend and his mute sister, who are now part of their group of friends. Before long, the disaster happens again and the group collapses. Alliances turn to enemies and their worlds are all upset. Regrets and despair claim some while power and success carry others. Tragedy claims more. Truths are revealed and lives are changed forever. <br /> <br /> And you will never see a more beautiful sunrise. <br /> <br /> This film is a captivating story of eternal friendships, networks of relationships and a team that even death can not separate for too long. Moon Child combines science fiction, drama and action with the perfect cast and talent to create the most moving and sensational film of its time, and ideal for most audiences. It minimizes everyday romances and puts more emphasis on important values ​​that we can all understand, such as friendships, loyalty and self-confidence. Nothing could compare. Personally, I have never seen anything like it and I do not think I'll see it again. <br /> <br /> This appeals to the general public in many ways and is a must for everyone.
