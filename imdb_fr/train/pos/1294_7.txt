Tirelessly gloomy and depressing, the film evokes as well as possible the legendary misery and emptiness that characterized the controversial novel of the same name by Houellebecq. Like many French films, its depth is one of melancholy but it is terribly slow - or should it be slow, painful? Although this film is excellent and stimulating, it is not pleasant and it is difficult to think at any time where one can be in a good mood to see it.
