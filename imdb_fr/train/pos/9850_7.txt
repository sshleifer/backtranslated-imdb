An interesting animation about the fate of a giant tiger, a sloth and a mammoth who saved a baby, who was about to be killed by a group of tigers during the ice age. The morale of the film shows that good behavior with others can bring benefits in the end. One of the group's tigers was ordered to capture the baby, who was barely saved by his mother when the tigers attacked his community. The baby was then saved by laziness and mammoth, but the tiger joined them in order to get him out of the baby permanently. They took very difficult paths with great danger, and the tiger had to fall immediately and be saved by the mammoth. In the end, the group of tigers tried to capture the baby, but the mammoth helped incredibly by its tiger colleague was able to defeat this attack and return the baby to his father and the community to which he belongs.
