It was my first introduction to the world of Bollywood and I am now addicted! OK, so you have to adopt a different mood to watch American movies, but once you have the pleasure of enjoying it, you will not be disappointed. The songs are beautiful, melodic and very catchy. The actors are visually compelling, especially Karisma Kapoor, who is surely one of the most beautiful actresses in the world of cinema. The locations, the color are mesmerizing. If you want something different and you want to be high, encouraged and stimulated, I recommend you watch this movie.
