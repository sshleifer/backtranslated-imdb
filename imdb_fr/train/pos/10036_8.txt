Titanic is a long but successful tragic adventure love story that unfolds during the unhappy journey on the unsinkable ship. Screenwriter / director James Cameron did a great job in making this film about a fictional love story between two very different people and combining it with the real Titanic event that sank after hitting an iceberg on April 15, 1912 , killing thousands in the icy waters of the North Atlantic. The two main roles of the film are excellent in their roles, including Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet. They make a good couple on the screen. DiCpario and Winslet also had a real chemistry, which made the romance that flourished between them even more believable. They both showed real talent when it came out and they both continued to show it in their most recent movies. Other actors, including Billy Zane, Kathy Bates, Frances Fisher, Jonathan Hyde and Bill Paxton in a small role are just as impressive as their characters who help them bring this film to life. The love story, the action, the suspense and the special effects are beautiful, made especially for this time. The horror of the characters' situation was so real that it happened, which made you want most of them to survive this life and death situation. The pace was a bit slow sometimes and it was a bit long but the rest of the film made up for some of its flaws. Titanic is an excellent rendezvous movie that will definitely make girls cry almost every time they watch it. The fact that it really happened definitely added to the film, which makes you sorry for all the lives lost when the Titanic sank in the Atlantic after hitting an iceberg. Overall Titanic is a heartbreaking and heartbreaking story about two people falling in love with this unfortunate ship made alive by the exceptional performance of the cast, including DiCaprio and Winslet, which make this movie worth seeing.
