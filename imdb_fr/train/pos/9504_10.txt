As many people know, Mexican cinema was very poor after the so-called golden age of Mexican cinema, fortunately, at the end of the 90s and at the beginning of the 21st century, great films like La Ley de Herodes, Bajo California , Amores Perros, Y Tu Mamá Tambien and, of course, El Coronel No Tiene Quien the Escriba appeared. El Coronel ..., is a wonderful film that tells the classic story of Gabriel García Márquez, eliminating the elements of magical realism and replacing them with the raw reality lived in Mexico, not only by people like the colonel, who expect their pensions, but by more than half of the Mexican population, living in absolute poverty. The characters in the film satirically represent the classic characters of Mexican society, such as the nationalist colonel, the cold and ambitious priest, the hypocrite but also the faithful compadre, the tolerant and patient wife, the hidden homosexual, etc. . movie, is a must if you want to know more about Mexican society, and most importantly, if you want to watch a wonderful movie, by one of the best filmmakers in Mexico
