"Dressed to Kill" is surely one of the best horror movies / thrillers ever made. It is a tight, elegant and extremely charged mixture of sex and violence. "Dressed to Kill" is a murder mystery involving a sexually frustrated housewife (Angie Dickinson), her teenage son (Keith Gordon), her psychoanalyst (Michael Caine) and a call-girl dear to purchase (Nancy Allen L The film's assassin is a transsexual named Bobbi who is also one of Caine's patients.The film is full of breathtaking moments: the infamous scene of elevator murder is extremely elegant and beautifully bloody.
