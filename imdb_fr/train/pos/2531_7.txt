Very good horror film starring one of the greatest, Boris Karloff and Bela Lugosi. Janos Rukh (Karloff) takes part in an expedition to Africa to try to find an old meteorite. After finding it, Rukh is poisoned by his radiation. Everything he touches dies and Rukh's dark side makes him a selfish murderer. His friend, Dr. Felix Benet (Lugosi) finds a limited cure for the problem and realizes at the same time that radiation could be used for the good of humanity by treating diseases. The two demons will fight for the possibilities of radiation. Very good special effects. Other actors: Frances Drake, Frank Lawton, Beulah Bondi and Frank Reicher.
