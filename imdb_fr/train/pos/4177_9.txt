A delicious piece of movie narrative in a simple but effective way. After all, cinema is a visual medium and Igor has used all his potential. A worried young man gets on a train without a destination. In one of the compartments, he meets a girl. The words are not exchanged, but their clothes are washed and from there, we go on a ride with other characters and special situations. The two main roles are perfectly melted because their unique characteristics tell you a story without words.
