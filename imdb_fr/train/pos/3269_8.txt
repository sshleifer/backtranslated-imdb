CHANCES ARE is a charming romantic fantasy about a woman (Cybill Shepherd) whose husband (Christopher McDonald) is killed shortly after learning that she was pregnant. We then see the husband in heaven informing the authorities that he was taken too early and that his wife needs him. He is told that he can return to earth but not like himself. Flashforward 19 years old when we see Shepherd's daughter (Mary Stuart Masterson) prepare for a university degree and meet a young man (Robert Downey Jr.) who, ultimately, is the reincarnation of his father . The movie is a little predictable ... the story goes to all the places you expect, but it is played so charmingly by an energetic cast (especially Shepherd and Downey) that you can not help but stop to wrap up. pleasure. Shepherd has rarely been seen better on the screen and she and Downey are backed by a talented group of character actors playing supportive roles. A charming and charming fantasy that will engulf you and enchant you.
