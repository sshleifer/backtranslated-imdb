"Darius Goes West" is the touching story of a courageous teenager struggling with Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy and his personal quest to see the Pacific Ocean. He receives help and encouragement from a group of young men who love and care for him throughout this quest. <br /> <br /> The story presents a natural drama and an honest portrayal of young people's commitment to helping one of their own victims of this incurable disease. <br /> <br /> Anyone who thinks that young people are egocentric and narcissistic will find this film that will overthrow this stereotype. It is the power of young people and their commitment to the fate of Darius that are very convincing in this documentary.
