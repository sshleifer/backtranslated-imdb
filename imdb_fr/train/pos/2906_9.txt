A hilarious action comedy in which Damian Szifron is interested in the life of Díaz, a policeman whose wife deceived him. He lives in a hotel, feeling guilty of the infidelity of his wife, falls into a depression that he ceases to care for, lights red lights just to have fun, is pitying himself. And Silberman, a Jewish psyche on probation, a liberal on the left who does not trust the cops at all. He is told to accompany Díaz in his daily tasks, unable to refuse because of the conditions of his probation. Soon the situation subsides when Silberman discovers that his wife is cheating on him and ends up being comforted by the very person he was supposed to help, a person he did not trust at all at first. A nuclear conspiracy, car thieves, international spies, a hysterical woman. Strange characters in a delightful comedy about friendship and heroism.
