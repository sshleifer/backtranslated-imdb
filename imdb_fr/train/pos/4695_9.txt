What happened to Michael Keaton? What a great actor and he proves it in this film. This movie is really funny! And the reason why this movie is funny is for two reasons: an excellent script and Michael Keaton. This film is one of the funniest comedies in Hollywood history. This film is the ultimate parody of gangster movies. In this film, Hollywood makes fun of itself by using the genre of gangster film as the basis of a truly original comedy. The rest of the cast is funny too, especially the secondary cast. If you like to laugh and want to watch a movie that contains non-stop humor, then this movie is for you.
