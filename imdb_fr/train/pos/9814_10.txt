This award-winning episode of the Peabody Award is one of the highlights of the first season when a holodeck malfunction traps Captain Picard, Beverly, Data and a Starfleet historian named Waylan in a 1930s San Francisco backdrop. This episode is a tribute to "Maltese Falcon" by Raymond Chandler where Patrick Stewart takes on the role of Humphrey Bogart - with fedora and trench coat. The desk itself is almost an exact replica of the one featured in Bogey's "Maltese Falcon". <br /> <br /> This episode also briefly introduces us to a mysterious insect race called the Jarada, which communicates mainly with a high-pitched buzz. Communication with this extraterrestrial race is difficult, and it is up to Picard to communicate with this race in their mother tongue so that negotiations and diplomacy can finally begin. The best part of this episode, however, is the appearance of the famous Hollywood actor B Lawrence Tierney, in the role of gangster Cyrus Redblock. He was such a handsome man in the 1940s.
