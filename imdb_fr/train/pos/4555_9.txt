This endearing film, based on Christy Brown's autobiographical novel, tells the story of his life. He has cerebral palsy and is not considered a person by all, including his mother. Surprisingly, he learns to draw and write using his foot, which is the only part of him that he can control. An incredible story of courage with a truly incredible and unforgettable performance of winning Daniel Day Lewis's Oscars. 9 out of 10
