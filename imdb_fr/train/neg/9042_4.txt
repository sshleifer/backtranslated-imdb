I wanted to love this movie. But it falls apart in the middle. the premise is good and well connected, but the middle is tangent. The people I was watching were pissed off as the game went on, and hoped it would end sooner than expected. Another person really fell asleep during the middle segment! I found myself dreaming somewhere else during the Schtick games that had nothing to do with the plot. I bought it for the pleasure of the eyes and it worked well, but it misses the writing and the soul of Pixar. I think kids 8 and under will enjoy the walk-and-go ride while missing the plot. People who are old enough to follow a plot will find it difficult to come back quickly and easily. Remove most of the middle section, set it to 50 minutes and you will get a solid film.
