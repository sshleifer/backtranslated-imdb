Bela Lugosi plays Dr. Lorenz who loves his wife so much that he will do anything to keep her young. This film begins with a wedding when the bride is about to say her wishes, she suddenly collapses. She is declared dead and taken away by funeral directors. The problem is that they are not real entrepreneurs, but body thieves. A wave of married dead at the altar and the disappearance of their bodies confuse the police. Enter reporter Patricia Hunter to solve the case. She tracks down Dr. Lorenz but he also decides to use his youth to keep his young wife as well.
