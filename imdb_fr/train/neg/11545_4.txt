This British psychological thriller is known in the United States as CLOSURE. Exploitation of Gillian Anderson of X-Files, who plays Alice, a seductive middle-aged businesswoman. She has to attend a company party and invites Adam (Danny Dyer), who has just installed a security system for her, to be his escort. On her way home, crossing the woods by a narrow lane, Alice's car hits a stag. After removing the injured animal from the road, the couple are savagely attacked by a gang of drunk thugs. Adam is beaten to pieces. Alice is gang-raped and both are emotionally and physically devastated by this ruthless attack. When the identity of their attackers is discovered, Alice and Adam try to take revenge ... a brutal revenge. The couple sometimes disagree on how to deal with ruthless attackers. Their final decision is to take revenge without mercy. Make no mistake, the return on investment is hell. Also in the cast: Anthony Calf, Ralph Brown, Francesca Fowler and Antony Byrne. Brutal violence, disturbing images, nudity and graphic rape.
