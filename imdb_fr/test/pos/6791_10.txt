National Velvet (1944) The movie that put Taylor on the map. Starring Mickey Rooney, box office champion at the time. Taylor embodies a girl in love with his horse and when jockey Mickey Rooney is unable to compete in the Grand National race, Liz cuts his hair, pretends to be a boy and runs the horse to end for you.
