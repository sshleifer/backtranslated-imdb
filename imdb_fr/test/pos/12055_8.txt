The story of an obsessed lover (Shahrukh Khan) and his efforts to find his true love (Juhi Chawla) who is already married to her husband (Sunny Deol). Regarded as one of Shahrukh Khan's best performances, the film has earned him praise from critics and the public. Fear that your love is not reciprocal, fear losing the one you love, fear that your loved one will change. In short, fear is the villain of all love stories. <br /> <br /> But in Darr, fear is the ultimate expression of passion, obsession and sacrifice. 'Darr' is the story of Rahul (Shahrukh Khan) whose love and obsession for Kiran (Juhi Chawla) free him from any fear of life or death. 'Darr' is the story of Sunil (Sunny Deol), whose enduring love and passion for Kiran give him the courage to face the fear of death. <br /> <br /> And finally 'Darr' is the story of Kiran who is caught between the love of man and the obsession of another man. She fears one and fears the other. One represents love, the other life. In this battle between love and life, the supreme conqueror is love, for love always wins, in life as in death. simply "Darr" is one of the best Indian films ever made.
