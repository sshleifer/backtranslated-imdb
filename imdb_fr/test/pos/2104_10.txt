This film is an absolute cinematic genius. He has a well-rounded cast that gives an almost magical performance. The effects are just sensational and the story will keep you going all the way through the movie. Jack Long plays exceptionally well the role of the white abbot and offers an immensely thrilling portrait of absolute evil. If you're a fan of kung-fu or just an action movie lover, this movie is a great choice for those lucky enough to find a copy. For all the big fans of kung-fu, this film provides a convincing glimpse of the world of shaolin. This movie is definitely 10/10 quality and should be considered as one of the greatest oriental movies of all time.
