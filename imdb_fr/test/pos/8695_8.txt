This movie has an excellent premise and really needs to be turned into a Hollywood blockbuster. If I remember correctly (and it's been a few years since I did not see the film), the action begins with a busy London omnibus. There is a terrible accident and a passenger dies. The rest of the film is then told in flashback, with 13 characters who were in the bus and who explore their recent lives in complex details. At the end of the film, we return to the crash and find which of these cheerful and bright characters had a horrible end. An awesome article, a bit like a good tabloid article elaborated with precise and fair details. If only it was available on video ...
