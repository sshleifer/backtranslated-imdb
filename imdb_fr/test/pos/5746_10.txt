A young man discovers that life is precious after being seriously injured in a street racing accident. His father is a pastor who changes his vision of life and he spends the whole film sharing his new love for others in this shocking and comforting film. The theme is built around street car racing and it's a great movie for those who like it and movies like 'the fast and the furious'. This shows how people can overcome their problems and give the general message about the meaning of life in a new and exciting way!
