This mini-series is emblematic of the Australian spirit. While some inconsistencies may be considered blatant, the film portrays a spirit unique to Australia that needs to be preserved. On the contrary, this mini-series demonstrates the Australian sense of humor. The laughing ability of the supreme ridiculous. Our will to laugh even in the most difficult situations. While much of this series focuses on camaraderie and how humor can be used as a means of survival, it also includes drama that evokes real emotions. The main actors that appear are absolutely sensational and very convincing in their different roles. Capture perfectly the essence of their characters. This mini-series should be mandatory for all Australians.
