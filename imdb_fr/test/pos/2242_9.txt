So, there was a hole in the plot where you could drive a zeppelin, but how far were emotions managed? It would have been so easy to sink into the cheesiness but the writer took it off. The image of the cyberman ex-wife who cries when she sees her reflection after having regained her emotions is the one that will stay with me forever. It's been two times now that the monsters showed a soft side and were presented with fleeting sympathy, the previous being the last Dalek of the first series, but by Jove it works. Add to that the other ex-wife who was "improved" on the eve of her wedding, and Jackie Tyler recognizing her husband after she became "cyber" and you have a permanent throat. Continue!
