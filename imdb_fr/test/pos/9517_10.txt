If you want an excellent study of Byzantine history made in a colorful way, it's for you. This documentary would also be excellent for educators who teach Roman or medieval history. This documentary is divided into three parts, first dealing with the fall of the Western Roman Empire, the rise of Christianity and the beginning of the Byzantine Empire. The second video deals with Byzantine diplomacy and iconoclastic controversy. The third and last video explains the decline and fall of Byzantium. The series is shot in several countries and magnificently incorporates Byzantine history into the realities of the modern world, showing the place of this civilization in human civilization in general. Take the time to look.
