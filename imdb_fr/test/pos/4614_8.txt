In this flame of emotion, we meet several young high school students during a busy day at school. They all find it difficult to be heard, to meet and to hide their painful secrets. It is inspired by a documentary of maturity, centered on the students' inner landscape and their way of facing the challenges of everyday life. like a requiem of songs. Thalluri moves the camera with great skill and slowly invites us into the minds and hearts of the youngest. A fictional reconstruction that enlightens us all and reminds us of the fragility of youth. One of the most important films of today. every teenager should have the opportunity to see this moving film.
