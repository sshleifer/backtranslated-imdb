"Brotherhood of Satan" is one of the most underrated horror movies of all time. Why not have acquired the status of worship, it exceeds me. It's a scary story of terror and witchcraft that encloses one of the most powerful climaxes of the horror screen. In the small American city children have disappeared and their parents have been violently murdered. the cause of hysteria? The film is really disturbing, some scenes are really annoying and certainly not easily forgotten. The suspense up, the acting is really good, and the highlight is weird and disturbing. Discover this forgotten jewel of satanic horror. Absolutely recommended.
