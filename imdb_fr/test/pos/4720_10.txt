Beyond Rangoon is one of the most emotional and intense films ever made. Superbly directed by John Boorman and intensely interpreted by Patricia Arquette, this film can easily be described as one of the best films of the 90s. The story and the lively characters capture the audience from the beginning and never let go. After seeing the film, the viewer will never forget "Beyond Rangoon". The film has made little money at the box office and is little known, but it should be highly publicized. Looking at it, you can say that it was meant to be seen by a wide audience. It is a very important and moving film, which should be seen by everyone. <br /> <br />
