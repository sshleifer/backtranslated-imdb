Frank Horrigan (Clint Eastwood) is a secret service agent tormented by the guilt of the assassination of President John F. Kennedy while on duty. Thirty years later, the current president is starting a re-election campaign, but he is receiving death threats. and Horrigan has been called to help with what should be a routine search operation. John Malkovich plays the professional assassin and master of disguise who hunt down the president and, using the past, begins to torture Horrigan in a psychological duel of cat and mouse. <br /> <br /> Malkovich, Eastwood and Rene Russo all give wonderful performances in this first-rate thriller. The direction is excellent and the whole picture is loaded with tension and intrigue. <br /> <br /> A must for thriller fans 8/10
