The most enjoyable pet movie ever since Scooby Doo and Garfield. The story revolves around an inventor by the name of Brian Foster, 23, whose systems of his boss's company seem to continue to fail, Brian also leaves with the boss's daughter, Casey. But Brian secretly invented a robotic dog named CHOMPS, inspired by his own dog Rascal. But CHOMPS is not an ordinary dog, he is as fast as a cheetah, he has X-ray vision, can jump about 6 feet and has a strength of 20 men. The new invention impresses his boss and makes his business a success. But when the company's rivals hear about CHOMPS, they try to find a way to capture it. Can CHOMPS make fun of them before it's too late. This film is a classic for all ages.
