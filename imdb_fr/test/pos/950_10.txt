The word Ghilli actually means a small sharp wooden playing instrument that is used in a game called ghilli-danda (a forerunner of cricket) in India. The use of the word as nickname for the main character is elegant because it means a sharp, fast player and can be very painful if you rub it in the wrong way. <br /> <br /> Ghilli is one of the best movies for Vijay and in her is incomparable in its rhythm and action. The movie never slows down for a moment and always keeps you with an exciting action. The film takes place in Madurai and Chennai and its story is simple. A rich landlord tries to covet a beautiful girl from his city and his undisputed power and authority prevent him from seeking justice. The hero tries to save her and the majority of this film describes the week in which all this action takes place. <br /> <br /> A very exciting film and although the story is not new, the director and the actors receive praise for the rhythmic action.
