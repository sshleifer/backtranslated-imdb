People have to give a chance to this show. People who write bad reviews (there are very few) are clearly people who have not seen many episodes. You really have to sit down and pay attention to this show to appreciate it. All characters are realistic because they have many flaws. They make mistakes, but they are REALISTIC errors, a rare thing on television today. In addition, all in all, the acting is superb. Lauren Graham has been snubbed from an Emmy for six years now. Someone must give this woman the credit she deserves. It's the same for Kelly Bishop, who plays Emily Gilmore so well. In addition, it is nice to see a show that can have a girl in the center of it and not be filled with teenage angst. Rory is a smart girl, who is not much seen on television today. If only other shows could capture the completeness of this show ...
