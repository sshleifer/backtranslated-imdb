Madonna has been rocking the boat for over 20 years, which brings her a lot of experience and knowledge. She was never the one she thought of yesterday, on the contrary, she seemed to know what would become popular tomorrow. She bravely takes on new trends and technologies and integrates them into her own actions. His world tours have never been "average". When Madonna plays, she gives 120% - everything is bigger, better, theatrical and meaningful. It is inspired by the East, the West and becomes unique. A deeper meaning is attributed to everything and The Confessions Tour is no different. From the beginning, you find yourself sitting on the edge of the chair. She begins with bang, then she circuses on a horse high in the sky, she does everything possible to entertain you. She remakes her own songs giving them a modern twist. She sings on a cross disco, she comes almost in the middle of the stage and with her incredible dance group that does not know the fear, the show is much more than you expected. It's a show, an audiovisual orgasm that can not be summed up. Fortunately, those who really saw Madonna on stage. At fifty, she is still in an incredible form. The recent Sticky and Sweet Tour is even more innovative, which proves nothing else, that Madonna was, is and will always be the one and only queen of pop.
