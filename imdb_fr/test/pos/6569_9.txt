"Uzumaki" takes place in a small rural Japanese town where people go crazy. They fall into trouble after swirls of whirlpools. The crisis is getting worse as people turn into snails and swirls appear everywhere. "Uzumaki" must be a plot The plot is really smart and the scenes gores are really funny because the film does not take itself too seriously. "Uzumaki" is wonderfully photographed and the use of colors is impeccable. The characters are nice and the surprises are shocking enough to satisfy fans of Japanese horror. Despite some hilarious scenes, the tone of this film is rather dark. "Uzumaki" is not as good as "Ringu", "Ju-on" or "Audition", but if you like Japanese horror movies, you will not be disappointed. 7 out of 10
