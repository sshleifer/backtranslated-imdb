"Chez nous" tells the moving story of four children who fled their homes after the death of their mother. One suitcase, their dog Ralph, and $ 9, they left in their old car to find their Uncle Jack. On the way, their car breaks down and they meet Peggy Williams, a teacher on vacation, who offers to help them. Together, she and the children are looking for Uncle Jack, but are forced to hide from the authorities who plan to reintegrate them into foster homes. This film tells a story of love, friendship and how important it is to stay with your family as children venture into their incredible journey.
