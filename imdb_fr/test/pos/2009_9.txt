This new Jeskid movie is awesome! Check it and you will be surprised. The story of Emily Waters, once a broken-hearted girl whose only way to escape a violent father was her sketchbook. Until one night, his drawings were concretized and saved. Now she uses this power to fight against those who would do evil. The real action movie and hand-drawn animation blend together to create a unique and original experience that will shake your soul and blow you away. The music is incredible too, it really intensifies the emotional experience and plunges you into the heart of the conflict. Directed by Jesse Cowell and hosted by Erica Langworthy, starring the beautiful Marissa Parness, featuring music by Nico Audy-Rowland, Daniel Collins, Jeff Strathearn, Matt Sisco and Selcuk Bor. Support this film and support Jeskid, he is a very talented guy. Check out his movie Shades of Gray too.
