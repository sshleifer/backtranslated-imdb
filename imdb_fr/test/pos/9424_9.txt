Basil Rathbone and Nigel Bruce return as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson in this superior story of Holmes fighting the 3rd Reich and the brain genius of Professor Moriarty. The film opens in Switzerland while Holmes is disguised as "old bookseller". He must bring Dr. Tobel and the Tobel bomb site to England before the Germans can kidnap Tobel. Holmes succeeds and the Germans hire the wrong teacher. Moriarty manages to thwart the Scottish yard and LeStrad "Dennis Hoey" by kidnapping Tobel. The only clue left by Tobel is a list of "dancing men". Who will break the hidden code of the dancing men, Holmes or Moriarty first? Can Holmes stop the bomb site from falling into the hands of the Germans, saving England from precision bombing techniques developed by the Tobel bomb site? Watch and enjoy.
