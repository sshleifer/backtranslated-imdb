Sam Elliot is a brilliant detective Charlie Fallon from San Francisco. When her partner is killed while meeting an informant, Fallon attacks him, beats him to death and throws him into a river. The next day, Fallon is assigned a rookie partner and is given the task of investigating the murder of the informants. Sam Elliot does a good job in describing a man who has been tortured by the guilt of his own murder and the grief caused by the death of his partner who may have been involved in police corruption.
