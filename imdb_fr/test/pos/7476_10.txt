How "Thursday" managed to avoid attention remains a mystery. A powerful mix of comedy and crime, this one takes risks when Tarantino plays the security with the Hollywood formula. The risks are not always profitable: a character in a sequence deflates inappropriately and falls flat. In the lead role, Thomas Jane gives a wonderful and complex performance and two brief appearances by Mickey Rourke suggest the high potential of this very underused and misused actor. Here is a director to watch.
