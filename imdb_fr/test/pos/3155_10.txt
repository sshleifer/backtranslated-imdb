Just see it! It's a smart movie but too difficult to understand the first time. See more than once. Everything that is seen or heard on the screen is intentionally designed to evoke a feeling, sensation or tone. The clarity of certain visuals and the sharpness of certain sounds deliberately contrast with fuzzy images, crackles, whistles and barely audible sounds. The structure is non-linear and cyclic. Patterns are repeated over time, clockwise and counter-clockwise. Expectations about "plot" and character "are broken, signaling a non-traditional use of the film to tell a story both contemporary and as old as humanity, and deeply disturbing, surprisingly unconventional. "Dion Dennis, PhD
