As in "The lovers of the Pont Neuf", two foreigners live a love story without concessions. The film is composed of many interesting conversations and sweet moments. The best comes in a listening booth. They listen to a record together and look at each other from time to time. They talk, they love each other. She suggests a change in their lives but he is out of hope. The realistic style is situated above the realistic (but) dialogues. A truly mathematical scriptwriter's job for this film. The director of Spanish novels Jesus Ponce creates one of the most perfect galleries of the last year of Spanish cinema.
