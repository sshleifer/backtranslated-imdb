A group of sorority girls pledge to spend the night in a scary mausoleum. Of course, the dead do not stay long dead and all hell is unleashed. "One Dark Night" is a pleasant horror of the 80s with ugly corpses floating around that are controlled by the spirit of a dead psychic Raymar. no gore and nudity, but the atmosphere of a mausoleum is very strange. The acting is solid, but the screenplay takes too long to develop the characters until the last 20 minutes of Raymar finally come out of his grave. The cinematography is impressive and The mausoleum is a great place for events climate. The film takes so long to start and is its main defect.7 out of 10.
