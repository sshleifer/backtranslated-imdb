"Capt. Corelli's Mandolin" is a romance to the old-fashioned Hollywood war, but with sex and nudity, and supposedly no American. The story takes place on a Greek island during the Second World War. The Italians arrive to seize the island, but under German surveillance. There is a romantic triangle composed of a Greek couple and the Italian captain. Good performances of all actors; The best work of Penelope Cruz to date.
