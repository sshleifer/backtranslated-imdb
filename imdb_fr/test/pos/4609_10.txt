All fans of Boris Karloff will love this classic film, where Karloff is the doctor of the castle and pays great attention to his patients. Sir Ronald Burton (Richard Greene), an English adventurer of the eighteenth century, thinks that his two friends were murdered by Count Von Bruno (Stephen McNally) in his Black Forest estate. Arriving at Von Bruno's castle to accumulate evidence, Burton learns Von Bruno's unfortunate wife, Elga (Paula Corday),. and Dr. Meissen (Boris Karloff), the doctor of the castle, are virtual prisoners. Suspecting Burton's motives, Von Bruno and Gargon (Lon Chaney Jr.), a giant, mute hand man with scars, discover that the Englishman was responsible for their capture and torture. You will definitely have to watch this wonderful classic Karloff movie to enjoy the end.
