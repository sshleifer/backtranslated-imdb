A young woman leaves her provincial life for news in town and meets another woman she falls in love with. Their relationship turns quickly to the physical and they both believe that they are soul mates, until the day the provincial girl comes home to find a man in their bed. Her lover reveals to her that their relationship was just an experience and that she really likes men. A bit like Anne Heche and Ellen Degeneres. In any case, the broken provincial girl, torn apart and broken by this discovery leaves and begins to discover the real world as she falls into the hands of all sorts of vindictive and salacious people of the 19th century in England.
