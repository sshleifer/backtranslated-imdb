To summarize this story in a few sentences: a teenager (Amy) uses her burning body and her "supposed" virginity to seduce a troubled young man (Matt) with a potential scholarship in the field of football to offer him a " complete output ". Come discover that she has quite a reputation and has slept with many footballers in the past in the hope of offering him the same contract. These two children are from troubled and dysfunctional homes. Matt's mother is an alcoholic who embarrasses him several times in front of his friends. Amy's mother had a bad reputation herself and got Amy pregnant at a young age. Matt falls in love with Amy and tries to get ready for her. Very predictable end. The actress who plays "Amy" is 33 years old and tries to play a teenager!
