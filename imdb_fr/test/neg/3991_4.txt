I have a lot of respect for Mr. Argento's work, but this film lacks many of the qualities that make his films truly unique. The opening of the film is excellent and allows you to make a horror movie really scary. What happens for the rest of the film lacks structure or purpose and does not build much. There is, however, good performance and a lot of good atmosphere. The end of the film is low considering all that implies. Where there should be a grand climax of enormous proportions, you get rather a very typical conclusion that too many other bad horror movies use, giving the impression that they just needed to wrap up. Some scenes are very memorable for their images, but in the end, the film does not make a very good movie.
