Me and my friend read the summer and watched the trailer. We were very interested and excited to go and rent this movie. BAD IDEA. We thought a film with influential actors would have been a success, but our expectations have remained extremely short. First of all, the trailer and the summer are deceiving to the point of lying. The film started slowly during the first half hours (recall, it lasts about two hours) and when it finally began to gain momentum, it sucks. In addition, the plots were very difficult to follow. This baffled us because we were moving from one story to another in a random order. The characters were not very realistic when it came to reality. Of course, mother and son could be real people in reality, but everyone seemed to be one extreme or the other. If you are a person who likes unusual, twisted or sick movies, go for it. But we advise you not to lose two hours of your life. Unfortunately, no one told us that ...
