Ed (Kel Mitchell) is a teenager who lives from his job at Good Burger, a small but friendly neighborhood hamburger stand, while his friend Dexter (Kennan Thompson) also works there. he accidentally destroyed the car of his teacher, Mr. Wheat (Sinbad), and must raise funds to pay for the damage. When Mondo Burger, a huge fast-food chain, opens on the street, it looks like Good Burger is part of the story, until Ed makes a secret sauce that attracts hundreds of new customers. However, Mondo Burger's monomaniac director, Kurt (Jan Schweiterman), is determined to get his hands dirty and put Good Burger out of business. Meanwhile, Ed and Dexter must save Otis (Abe Vigoda), the world's oldest fast food worker, from Demented Hills Asylum, and Ed could find love with Monique (Shar Jackson) if he was able to 'forget about her burgers thinking long enough to pay attention to her. Good Burger is a children's comedy, a decent story, an acting game and a rather harmless children's movie.
