Colorful Western. <br /> <br /> Wyoming Cowboy James Stewart moseys on to the Yukon gold mine to sell his calves, and try to get a date with a pretty salon owner Ruth Roman, if he can shake a tail with a crooked sheriff, John McIntyre, who wants to cattle Stewart. Beautiful cinematography in the joint efforts of William Fritsch and Bill Daniels. Good Technicolor adventure from Universal-International, directed by Anthony Mann. <br /> <br /> Starring Corina Calvet, Connie Gilchrist, and Walter Brennan as the sidekick Stewart.
