In April 1965, CBS broadcast the first television monumental events Barbra Streisand. The show is not only a stunning success ratings, but received 5 Emmy Awards as well. This is one of the most memorable moments of television in 1960, and (unfortunately) the type of television special, they do not produce more. Filled with beautiful songs and exciting performance Barbra, is a special kind is a must for any fan of Streisand and all who are interested in early television.
