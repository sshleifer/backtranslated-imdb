When it comes to Paul Verhoeven and erotic thrillers, most people think of "Basic Instinct", and some may have to "Showgirls". But Verhoeven made his best erotic thriller of the year before those two films: «De Vierde Man». This film is fascinating and mindblowing - and above all the stories are absolutely true, that makes the whole experience even more intense. Submission by Jeroen Krabbe, Reni Sautendizhk and Thom Hoffman are exceptional, and the direction of Verhoeven does the rest. «De Vierde Man» makes even the "Basic Instinct" look pretty tame ... It can not get much better than this, this classic erotic movie nightmare. 10 out of 10, at least ...
