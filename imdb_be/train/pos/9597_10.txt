This should see the documentary for those who are afraid that today's youth has lost the taste for real-life adventures and his sense of morality. Darius Goes West strange roller-coaster history. We live a life of Darius and the crew as they embark on the trip of a lifetime. Darius has Duchenne muscular dystrophy, a disease that affects the muscles in his body. He is confined to a wheelchair and needs round the clock attention. So how does a team of young friends might have been able to take it to 6,000 miles round-trip to the West Coast and back again? See the movie and experience the ups and downs of this great adventure - laugh and cry together with the crew, how they deal with incredible problems along the way and enjoy the final victory, when they come back in three weeks in his hometown, to an enthusiastic reception and some great surprises!
