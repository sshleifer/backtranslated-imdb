Really good horror film starring to the greatest, Boris Karloff and Bela Lugosi. Dr. Janos Movement (Karloff) on an expedition in Africa, trying to find an ancient meteorite. After finding it, the Movement will be poisoned by its radiation. All that he touches dies, and the dark side of the movement makes it become selfish killer. His friend, Dr. Felix Bennett (Lugosi) is limited means for this problem and at the same time understands that radiation can be used for the benefit of humankind by treating diseases. Two thieves are fighting for the possibility of radiation. Pretty good special effects. Other starring Francis Drake, Frank Lawton, Bela Bondi and Frank Reich.
