This is a very real and funny film about a Japanese man having a midlife crisis. In Japan, ballroom dancing disapproved. But if Shohei Sugiyama becomes obsessed with meeting a beautiful young girl he sees in dance studio window, he suddenly finds himself enrolled in dance classes. No one is more surprised when he starts to like than he. But he has to keep his secret pleasure from their colleagues and family. When the truth comes out it's pretty funny.
