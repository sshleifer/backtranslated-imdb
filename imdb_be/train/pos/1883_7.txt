Excellent comedy starred Dudley Moore and Liza Minnelli was supported by good language John Gielgud. Arthur Moore, a man belonging to the family of billionaire, who was there to receive $ 750 million under the condition that he would marry the lady (Susan) with another multimillionaire family. In principle, Arthur accepted the terms, but he finally abandoned when he met good and bad Linda Marolla (Minnelli). Arthur was just a parasite, because it does not work, he just had fun getting drunk and having fun with prostitutes. After some serious thought in his life and for the first time, Arthur decided not to marry Susan only a few minutes before their wedding. The end was happy for Linda and Arthur, even though he knew that his life would change in the near future. This comedy is a good lesson for life for all. Rich people usually are not satisfied with their way of life.
