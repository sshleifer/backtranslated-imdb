IQ is surprisingly original romantic comedy, which is the largest and deepest-thinking scientific minds of the 20th century as assistants Cupid. The juxtaposition of the heart and mind is the central theme of this relaxing yet thought provoking film. You do not even know how to respond, because of the time, you see more and more scientists are doing silly things to nurture promising love, but at other times, you hear them discuss some of the most profound space / time mysteries of our time. The end result is an entertaining film with surprises everywhere. Matthau is the perfect Einstein, Meg Ryan creates a bizarre, scatter brains mathematician, and Tim Robbins brings to life the contradictions poorly educated working man who is passionate about science. All together, they create a farcical journey of love and science, mind and heart.
