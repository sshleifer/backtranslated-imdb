Quiet, sweet and beutifully nostalgic film about how he has to face old friends and environment with your youth all the memories and the problems and sorrows of our time with you. A film that makes you feel good. All the ingredients are there: old jelousy, competition, friendship and loyalty. Damage, night raids on the refrigerator, and all the other interesting things that we all remember from our summer camps. All of the characters have the opportunity during the week to try it again, as the old camp leader at the moment is retiring and wants to meet with the children of the golden years of the camp. All of them are now in the thirties and in the middle of his career.
