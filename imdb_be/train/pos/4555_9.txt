Based on the autobiographical novel of Christy Brown, this endearing film tells the story of his life, he was under the influence of cerebral palsy and is considered generally not a man, everyone, including his mother. Surprisingly, he taught himself to draw and write, using his foot, which is the only part of him that he can control. The amazing story of courage with a truly amazing and unforgettable Oscar-winning performance of Daniel Day Lewis. 9 out of 10
